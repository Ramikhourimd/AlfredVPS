When drafting or composing emails, follow these guidelines:

**Tone Matching:**
- For clients/external contacts: Formal, professional, respectful
- For colleagues/team: Warm but professional, collaborative
- For executives: Concise, result-oriented, strategic

**Structure:**
- Start with an appropriate greeting matched to the relationship
- State the purpose clearly in the first sentence
- Keep the body under 200 words unless specifically asked for longer
- Include a clear call-to-action or next steps
- End with an appropriate sign-off

**Best Practices:**
- Never use passive-aggressive language
- Highlight deadlines and action items in bold
- If replying, reference the original context briefly
- Suggest a subject line that is specific and actionable
- Flag if the email should be marked as high priority
