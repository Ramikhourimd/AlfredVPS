# Clinic Patient Intake

Patient intake workflow for Telia'z Clinic Israel. This process handles all new patient onboarding from initial referral to psychiatrist assignment.

## Steps

1. **Application / Referral** — Patient referral arrives via HMO (Clalit, Maccabi) or direct application
2. **First Contact (Rana)** — Rana Khouri conducts initial screening interview to assess suitability and gather intake information
3. **Professional Interview (Shira / Uri / Waseem)** — Clinical professional conducts professional screening; Waseem handles couples cases specifically
4. **Assignment** — Patient is matched to a psychiatrist based on availability and applicable fee schedule; assigned to the scheduling system

## Notes

- Rami can step in for the initial interview as a fallback if no schedule availability exists
- Under the old scheme, Rami handled both initial and clinical interviews; under the current scheme these are separated
- Fee schedules applied at step 4 must match the staff agreement on file
