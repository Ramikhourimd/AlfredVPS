# Clinic Israel New Employee Onboarding

Structured onboarding process for new staff joining Telia'z Clinic Israel. Designed to ensure every new clinician and staff member is operationally ready before starting patient-facing work.

## Tracks

The onboarding is separated into three parallel tracks:

1. **Technology Access (MUST — prerequisite)** — Teams setup, HoViD platform access and training. This is mandatory before any clinical work can begin. New employees cannot function without technology access.
2. **Operational-Clinical** — Clinical workflow orientation, patient handling procedures, system usage protocols. Can be combined with technology track. Delivered by Stav Zamir or Shira.
3. **Administrative** — HR paperwork, agreements, billing procedures, reporting requirements. Can proceed independently.

## Cadence

Under discussion (as of 2026-02-12):
- Minimum: Once per month
- Preferred: Every two weeks (bi-weekly) to reduce wait time for new hires
- Flexible: May adjust based on hiring volume — some months may need rapid orientation for multiple hires

## Delivery

- **Technology track:** Can be delivered alongside operational track by Stav or Shira
- **Operational-clinical track:** Requires clinical management presence
- **Administrative track:** Can proceed independently
