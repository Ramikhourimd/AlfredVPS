# Maccabi Healthcare Services

Israel's second-largest health maintenance organization (kupat cholim). Maccabi is a primary payer partner for Telia'z Clinic Israel, funding patient sessions through a commitment-based model (initial allocation of 16 case manager + 4 psychiatric sessions per patient).

The email domain `mac.org.il` is used by Maccabi staff.
