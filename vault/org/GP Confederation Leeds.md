# GP Confederation Leeds

GP Confederation in Leeds, West Yorkshire. A federation of GP practices that delivers collaborative services across the Leeds area. Led by Jim Barwick as CEO/Director-Secretary. The Confederation has existing CQC registration and established credibility in the local health economy.

Currently working on ADHD service pathway design, including waiting list management and neurodevelopmental triage. Has existing relationships with the ICB (Integrated Care Board) and third-sector organizations in Leeds. Exploring partnership with Telia'z to bring AI-assisted telepsychiatry services to the region.

Key characteristics:
- CQC registered provider
- Established GP network across Leeds
- Working on ADHD service procurement (tender expected spring 2025)
- Interested in technology-enhanced clinical pathways
- Revenue share model under discussion with Telia'z
