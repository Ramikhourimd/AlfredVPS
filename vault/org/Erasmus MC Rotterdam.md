# Erasmus MC Rotterdam

Academic medical center and research university in Rotterdam, Netherlands.

## Research Collaboration with Basel Kanaaneh
- Basel coordinated a clinical research center partnership with Erasmus MC's psychiatry department
- Research focused on schizophrenia and autism in families with high prevalence of both conditions
- Collaboration began when an Erasmus MC researcher visited Basel's Shefa-Amr clinic and gave a lecture to staff
- Basel recruited Israeli hospitals (Rambam, Nazareth, Nahariya, Soroka Beer Sheva, Carmel) to participate in the research
- Multiple psychiatric departments across Israeli hospitals joined the research effort
