# Telia'z

Telepsychiatry company providing remote psychiatric care. Operates a large-scale clinic in Israel serving thousands of patients via telemedicine. The company integrates AI and technology innovation into clinical workflows, including automated questionnaires, AI-assisted clinical summaries, and dashboard-based KPI monitoring.

Key characteristics:
- Telepsychiatry model (remote psychiatric care)
- Contracts with Israeli HMOs (Maccabi, Clalit)
- Subject to Ministry of Health regulations
- AI/innovation integration into clinical processes
- Plans for international expansion (UK, France, potentially Singapore)
