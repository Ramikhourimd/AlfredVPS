# Clalit Health Services

Israel's largest health maintenance organization (Kupat Holim Clalit). Basel Kanaaneh worked at Clalit from 2017 until late 2025, progressing from clinic manager in Shefa-Amr to Field Activity Manager (national level).

## Key Facts
- Approximately 55,000 employees nationwide (second-largest employer in Israel after the IDF)
- Operates district-level clinic networks across all regions
- Mental health reform implementation partner since 2017
- Operates under strict budget allocation from Ministry of Health per district
- District structure: clinics report to district (machoz), districts report to national HQ

## Relevance to Telia'z
- Basel Kanaaneh's former employer; his deep Clalit experience informs Telia'z clinic operations
- Potential B2B partner: Clalit could outsource mental health services to Telia'z platform
- Basel noted that Clalit's organizational inertia makes innovation slow — changes take 2-3 years

## Telia'z Partnership Operations

As of November 2025, Telia'z is launching a clinical services partnership with Clalit, expanding beyond the existing Maccabi relationship. Key operational differences from Maccabi:
- Form 17 authorization requires manual approval by Clalit district contact
- Clalit pharmacies do not accept external prescriptions (GP routing required)
- Clalit uses Clicks as their EHR system
- Patient acquisition expected via GP referral brochures initially
