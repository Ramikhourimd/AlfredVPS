# Telem

Telem (תלם) is a military rehabilitation unit operating under the IDF Rehabilitation Division (אגף השיקום). It facilitates psychiatric and mental health services for families of wounded soldiers by connecting them with healthcare providers through Israeli health funds (kupot cholim).

## Key Details

- **Full Name:** Telem (תלם) — Military Rehabilitation Unit
- **Parent Organization:** IDF Rehabilitation Division (Agaf HaShikum)
- **Role:** Coordinates referrals of wounded soldiers' families to psychiatric services
- **Current Partnership:** Working with Telia'z to provide telepsychiatry services via Maccabi Healthcare Services
- **Key Contact:** Amiad (manager of Telem, surname unknown)

## Relationship with Telia'z

Telem refers families of wounded soldiers to Telia'z clinic through Maccabi. A dedicated brochure program is being developed to raise awareness of available psychiatric services among eligible families. The brochure will be Maccabi-branded (not Telem-branded) and include unique tracking links/QR codes per clinic location to measure referral effectiveness.
