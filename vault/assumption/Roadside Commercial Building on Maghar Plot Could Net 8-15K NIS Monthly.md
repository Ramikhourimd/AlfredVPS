# Roadside Commercial Building on Maghar Plot Could Net 8-15K NIS Monthly

## Claim

A small permanent commercial structure (convenience store, cafe, minimarket, or roadside kiosk) on the Maghar plot could generate approximately 8,000–15,000 NIS net monthly income, leveraging the plot's direct frontage on the regional Maghar-Gilon road and passing traffic visibility.

## Basis

The 2025-09-23 investment feasibility analysis evaluated a small commercial building / kiosk as Category 1.1, rating it as medium-high investment, active management, long timeline (permits + construction), with 8,000–15,000 NIS/month net income potential. This requires building permits, business license, and potentially Ministry of Health food compliance. The existing mixed urban zoning (ironi mesholav) permits this use without a zoning change.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-09-23: Feasibility analysis produced with income projections across multiple commercial scenarios
- 2025-09-22: Family discussion about the plot's potential (preceded the analysis)

## Impact

This income projection is a key input for the investment decision on the Maghar plot. However, confidence is low because: (1) projections are from an AI-assisted analysis, not market research or comparable sales data; (2) the medium-high investment requirement includes infrastructure connections (water, electricity, sewage) which add significant upfront cost; (3) active management requirement means this competes for Rami's time against clinical practice and Telia'z work.
