# Medical Director Is a Part Time Thirty Percent Position

## Claim

The Medical Director role — responsible for clinical protocols, medical consultations, and professional oversight of psychiatrists — is scoped as a 30% (part-time) position. This assumes the role is primarily supervisory and regulatory rather than requiring full-time clinical involvement.

## Basis

Both meeting notes state the Medical Director as a "30% position." This scoping appears driven by two factors: (1) budget constraints limiting new management hires, and (2) the belief that the role is supervisory — setting protocols and being available for consultations — rather than hands-on clinical work. The assumption is that a practicing physician can fulfill this role alongside their own clinical practice.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Both meeting notes describe the Medical Director as a 30% position without debate on this figure.

## Impact

This assumption directly affects hiring strategy (targeting practicing physicians willing to add a part-time leadership role), budget planning, and the workload distribution across the three-pillar management structure. If 30% proves insufficient — especially as the clinic scales to 30+ psychiatrists — the role may need to expand, increasing cost and potentially requiring a different candidate profile.
