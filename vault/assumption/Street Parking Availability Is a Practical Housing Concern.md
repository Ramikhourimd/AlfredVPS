# Street Parking Availability Is a Practical Housing Concern

## Claim

Street parking availability is a practical consideration in the family's housing evaluation. Parking was raised as a concern during at least two separate property viewings on 2025-10-09 — at the family-owned building and at a new building visit. However, Rami appeared to downplay the concern in one instance, calling it "easy entry."

## Basis

- At the family building viewing, street parking was discussed as "potentially problematic"
- At the new building visit, a companion raised concern about parking availability and whether spots belong to building residents
- Rami dismissed the parking concern at the new building as easy

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: Parking discussed at family building viewing (concern noted)
- 2025-10-09: Parking discussed at new building visit (Rami dismissive, companion concerned)

## Impact

Parking is unlikely to be a dealbreaker given Rami's dismissive reaction, but it may factor as a tiebreaker between otherwise comparable properties. Properties with dedicated parking may have an advantage.
