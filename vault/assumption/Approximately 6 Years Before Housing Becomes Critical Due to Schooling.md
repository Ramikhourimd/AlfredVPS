# Approximately 6 Years Before Housing Becomes Critical Due to Schooling

## Claim

The family has approximately 6 years (from September 2025, i.e., until roughly 2031) before their housing situation becomes truly critical due to children reaching school age. This timeline suggests that while the current living situation is uncomfortable, there is a window for longer-term strategies like land development or property purchase rather than only urgent rental solutions.

## Basis

Rami mentioned this timeline during the 2025-09-22 family gathering in the context of discussing whether to build on the Maghar land versus continuing the rental search. The timeline is tied to the children's schooling needs — presumably when the eldest child begins primary school and neighborhood/school district becomes a binding constraint.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-09-22: Rami states ~6 years before housing becomes critical (children's schooling)
- Existing records show wife's dissatisfaction with current living situation creates shorter-term pressure

## Impact

This 6-year window creates a tension between two timelines: the wife's immediate dissatisfaction with current housing (pushing for quick rental solution) and the longer strategic timeline that could accommodate land development or property purchase. It supports pursuing the Maghar land development option as a viable medium-term strategy rather than dismissing it as "too slow."
