# OMG Will Handle Initial Patient Triage for NHS ADHD Pathway

## Claim

OMG Healthcare will perform the initial patient triage (inclusion/exclusion screening) for the NHS ADHD pathway, using health inequity and high risk factors as criteria. This screening happens via phone or virtual appointment, not in-person. Patients who pass are handed off to Telia'z for psychiatric assessment.

## Basis

Adiel confirmed in the July 3 meeting that OMG sees the initial triage as their responsibility. OMG has existing clinical staff (nurses, nurse prescribers) who handle patient-facing intake across their other services. Their CQC governance framework requires them to maintain oversight of the patient journey from GP referral onward.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-03: Adiel confirmed OMG will handle triage in pathway design meeting

## Impact

If OMG handles triage, Telia'z avoids the cost and staffing burden of initial screening. This also means Telia'z must define clear handoff data requirements so patients arrive with sufficient clinical context.
