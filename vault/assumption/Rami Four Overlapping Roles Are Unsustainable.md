# Rami Four Overlapping Roles Are Unsustainable

## Claim

Rami currently holds four overlapping roles — CEO substitute, Clinic Medical Director, Head of Clinical Operations, and Innovation/AI Lead — and this combination is unsustainable. The restructuring assumes these must be consolidated into two clear roles: CMO (company-level clinical authority) and Innovation Lead (AI integration and product development).

## Basis

The task "Define Rami Role for 2026" explicitly lists all four roles and frames the restructuring as needing to reduce them to two. The Feb 22 meeting discussion reinforced that Rami "cannot fill this role full-time given his other responsibilities." The sheer breadth of responsibilities implies role overload was recognized as a problem.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Leadership meeting identified need to consolidate four roles into two
- Medical Director hire is explicitly framed as prerequisite for role transition

## Impact

This assumption drives three dependent tasks: hiring a Medical Director (to absorb clinic medical duties), defining the CMO role proposal, and finalizing the organizational chart. If consolidation proves infeasible — for example, if no suitable Medical Director candidate is found — the entire restructuring timeline is at risk.
