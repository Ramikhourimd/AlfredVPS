# RiseUp Algorithm Recommendation Validates Refinancing Timing

## Claim

The consultation was triggered by RiseUp's algorithm recommending a mortgage refinance review. The implicit assumption is that this algorithmic signal correctly identifies a genuinely favorable refinancing window — that current market conditions make refinancing beneficial and the timing is right to act.

## Basis

Both consultation notes explicitly state the review was triggered by RiseUp's algorithm. Rami acted on this signal by scheduling the consultation, treating it as a valid indicator that his mortgage terms are suboptimal relative to current market options.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-01-26: RiseUp algorithm recommended refinance review; Rami attended consultation
- Consultant presented Option A with ~200,000 NIS projected savings, which appears to validate the algorithm's signal

## Impact

If this assumption holds, the decision to refinance now (rather than wait) is well-timed. If the algorithm is primarily a customer engagement tool rather than a genuine optimization signal, the urgency to act may be overstated, and independent confirmation of favorable market conditions could be warranted. The [[decision/Engage RiseUp Full-Service Refinancing]] and [[assumption/Interest Rate Environment Will Not Revert to Make Current Terms Favorable]] both rest partly on this timing signal being trustworthy.
