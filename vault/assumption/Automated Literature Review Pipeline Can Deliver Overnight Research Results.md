# Automated Literature Review Pipeline Can Deliver Overnight Research Results

## Claim

An automated pipeline can process research questions from a Google Sheets document and return literature review findings by the next morning. Rami offered to build this for Rivi, enabling her to submit research topics and receive structured literature results without manual search.

## Basis

Rami proposed this during the 2026-02-18 meeting as a way to accelerate Rivi's research workflow. The assumption rests on the belief that current LLM and search tools can produce academically useful literature reviews with sufficient quality for initial research orientation. This has not yet been built or tested.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-18: Rami offers to build pipeline; Rivi tasked with populating Google Sheets with research questions
- No prototype or test results yet

## Impact

If this works, it dramatically accelerates the research phase of innovation projects — converting what would be days or weeks of manual literature search into overnight automated delivery. Key risk: the quality of automated literature review may be insufficient for academic rigor, producing hallucinated references or missing key papers. The output would need human validation before being cited in any formal research.
