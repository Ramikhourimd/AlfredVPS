# UK Launch Can Operate Under Partner CQC Without Own Registration

## Claim

Telia'z can legally and operationally serve UK patients by routing them through Leon's existing CQC-registered practice, with Telia'z providing the clinical protocol, technology platform, and case management. This arrangement does not require Telia'z to have its own CQC registration for the initial launch phase.

## Basis

Adiel Levin confirmed the operational model in the 2026-02-15 team meeting. Leon already has CQC registration. The plan is for patients to arrive through Leon's website, follow Telia'z assessment flow, and be treated under Leon's practice umbrella. Legal counsel is reviewing the contract.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-15: Adiel presented the model at team meeting. Contract in negotiation.
- Pending: Legal review of contract terms and regulatory compliance

## Impact

This assumption is foundational to the March 31 UK launch timeline. If it proves invalid (e.g., CQC requires Telia'z to be independently registered even as a technology/service provider), the launch would be significantly delayed.
