# Open Kitchen Layout Is a Strong Housing Preference

## Claim

Rami has a strong preference for an open or large kitchen layout, distinct from the separate requirement for kitchen ventilation and natural light. The kitchen as a social/functional space matters beyond just air and light quality.

## Basis

During the 2025-10-09 apartment viewing at the family-owned building, Rami "expressed a strong preference for an open or large kitchen layout." This was noted as a standalone preference alongside but separate from the kitchen window/ventilation concern raised about the mini penthouse unit.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: Strong preference stated during family building viewing (Apartment Viewing note)
- 2025-10-09: Kitchen window concern on 126 sqm mini penthouse was a separate issue (Neighborhood Evaluation note)

## Impact

Properties with closed or small kitchens may be rejected even if they have adequate ventilation and light. This narrows the candidate pool beyond the existing natural light/ventilation constraint.
