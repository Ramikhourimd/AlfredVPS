# Clinic Israel 1M NIS Monthly Revenue Is Break-Even Point

## Claim

The Telia'z Clinic Israel reaches financial break-even (covering all company costs, not just clinic operations) at approximately 1 million NIS in monthly revenue. This target was expected to be achievable within three months from July 2025.

## Basis

Based on revenue tables prepared by Shachar. July 2025 actual revenue was approximately 335K NIS with 198K NIS in mid-month pipeline. The 1M NIS figure covers all overhead costs including non-clinic company expenses.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-14: Rami presented revenue table to Shira showing created vs. actual revenue by month
- Revenue gap represents patients registered but not yet seen — these roll forward to subsequent months

## Impact

This assumption drives:
- Shira's bonus calculation (750 NIS bonus at 1M revenue level)
- Timeline for clinic financial sustainability
- Urgency of recruitment and capacity expansion
