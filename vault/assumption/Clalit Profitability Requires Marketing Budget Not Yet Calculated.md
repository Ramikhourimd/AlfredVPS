# Clalit Profitability Requires Marketing Budget Not Yet Calculated

## Claim

The Clalit partnership profitability calculations completed so far do not include marketing costs. Since patient acquisition will likely require direct marketing investment (given that GP email referrals are expected to be ineffective), the true break-even point for the Clalit partnership is unknown and potentially higher than current projections suggest.

## Basis

During a 2025-11-09 discussion, one of the team members explicitly flagged that when calculating "what we receive and what we pay" for Clalit, the marketing budget was not included. This is a critical omission because Clalit's lower per-component rates (3 components vs. Maccabi's 4, at slightly lower rates) already compress margins, and marketing costs could tip the balance.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-11-09: Team member flags marketing budget gap in profitability calculations
- Clalit offers 3 case manager components (vs. Maccabi's 4), at slightly lower per-component rate
- No profitability analysis with marketing costs has been completed as of this date

## Impact

All Clalit business case projections and go/no-go decisions are based on incomplete financial modeling. Marketing budget must be factored in before commitments are made, especially given that Clalit patient volumes are expected to require active marketing investment.
