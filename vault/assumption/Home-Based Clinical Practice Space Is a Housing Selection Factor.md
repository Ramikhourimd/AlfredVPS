# Home-Based Clinical Practice Space Is a Housing Selection Factor

## Claim

Beyond the stated requirement for a home office, the family values properties that can accommodate a ground-floor clinical practice. During a property evaluation on 2025-10-09, the ability to use a lower level as a clinic was described as "a significant advantage" — allowing Rami to work from home in a professional capacity, not just a desk-based office.

## Basis

In the Property Investment Discussion (2025-10-09), a specific property was evaluated partly because "the ground floor or lower level could serve as a clinic." The ability to "work at night from a home clinic" was noted as a benefit. This suggests Rami has a professional practice (possibly medical or therapeutic) that could operate from a dedicated ground-floor space.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: Property with clinic potential evaluated as a "significant advantage" (Property Investment Discussion)

## Impact

This criterion expands the housing search beyond standard residential layouts. Properties with dual-use potential (ground-floor commercial/clinic + upper residential) may be prioritized. This also strengthens the case for the Maghar land development option, where a custom build could incorporate dedicated clinic space.
