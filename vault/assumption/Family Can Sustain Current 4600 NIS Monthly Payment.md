# Family Can Sustain Current 4600 NIS Monthly Payment

## Claim

Rami and his wife can manage the current ~4,600 NIS monthly mortgage payment without financial hardship, despite the 35% increase from the original 3,400 NIS.

## Basis

Explicitly stated multiple times during the RiseUp consultation. Rami described the payment as manageable and chose Option A (total cost savings) specifically because monthly cash flow relief was unnecessary. Both notes confirm: "the family can still manage the payment" and "they can handle the current payment."

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-01-26: Rami explicitly stated affordability during consultation
- 2026-01-26: Chose total savings option over monthly reduction, confirming payment sustainability

## Impact

This is the foundation for choosing Option A over Option B. If financial circumstances change and the 4,600 NIS becomes unsustainable, the refinancing strategy would need to shift toward monthly payment reduction.
