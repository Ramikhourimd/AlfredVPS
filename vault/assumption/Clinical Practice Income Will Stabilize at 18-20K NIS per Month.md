# Clinical Practice Income Will Stabilize at 18-20K NIS per Month

## Claim

Rami's clinical private practice income will reach and stabilize at 18,000-20,000 NIS per month, based on the current rate structure and available hours.

## Basis

- Initial hospital allocation: 15 hours/month for 8,000 NIS
- Recent month showed 21 hours worked, still yielding ~8,000 NIS (approximately 1.5 days of work)
- Standard schedule when available: 8 hours per week (excluding leave and holidays)
- At full utilization, the projected income is 18,000-20,000 NIS/month
- Additional ~3,000-4,000 NIS/month from company deductions/benefits creates a secondary stream

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-09-23: Initial projection discussed with accountant. Based on early months of clinical practice.

## Impact

- Forms a key pillar of Rami's dual income strategy (clinical + Telia'z)
- Financial planning for savings, investments, and expenses depends on this income materializing
- If income underperforms, the keren hishtalmut preservation strategy becomes even more important
