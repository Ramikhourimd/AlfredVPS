# Self-Administered Questionnaire Must Precede Case Manager Intake in UK Flow

## Claim

In the UK NHS ADHD pathway, a self-administered clinical questionnaire (e.g. ASRS or broader screening tool) must be completed by the patient before the case manager intake interview. This is not a case manager intake — it is a self-report instrument that sharpens and focuses the subsequent clinical assessment.

## Basis

Rami explicitly corrected Adiel's framing during the July 3 meeting. When Adiel asked about combining the questionnaire with the case manager intake, Rami stated that any change from the standard Telia'z clinical flow requires justification, and the questionnaire serves a distinct clinical purpose as a pre-screening step.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-03: Rami clarified questionnaire vs case manager distinction in pathway design meeting

## Impact

The patient pathway has a clear sequential dependency: triage → questionnaire → case manager intake → psychiatrist. Skipping or merging the questionnaire step would change the clinical methodology and require explicit justification.
