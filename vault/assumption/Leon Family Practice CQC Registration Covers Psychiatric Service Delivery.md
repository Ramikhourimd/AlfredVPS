# Leon Family Practice CQC Registration Covers Psychiatric Service Delivery

## Claim

Leon holds CQC registration as a family doctor (GP). The team assumes his existing CQC scope either already covers or can be extended to cover psychiatric ADHD assessment and treatment services delivered by Telia'z psychiatrists under his registration. No discussion of CQC scope limitations or the need to vary Leon's registration has appeared in any source record.

## Basis

Across multiple Feb 15 records, the team treats operating under Leon's CQC as straightforward — contract signing is the only gate discussed. No mention of CQC scope variation, regulatory consultation about whether a GP's CQC covers specialist psychiatric services, or any compliance review of this specific question.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-15: Team meeting treats Leon's CQC as ready to use for psychiatric services once contract is signed
- No record of CQC scope review or legal opinion on this specific question

## Impact

If Leon's CQC scope does not cover psychiatric services, the entire Track 1 launch strategy would require either a CQC variation application (adding time) or restructuring how the service is classified. This is a foundational regulatory assumption for the March 2026 launch.
