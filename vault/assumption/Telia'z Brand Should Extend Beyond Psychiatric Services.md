# Telia'z Brand Should Extend Beyond Psychiatric Services

## Claim

Rami believes Telia'z should maintain a broad brand identity — "Telia'z Health by Telia'z" — rather than equating the company exclusively with psychiatric services. This positions the company for future expansion into adjacent healthcare verticals. In contrast, Adi Lavi (CMO Marketing) has defined Telia'z as equal to psychiatric services on the company website, which Rami views as prematurely narrowing the brand.

## Basis

During the December 2, 2025 management discussion, Rami observed that nobody at Telia'z — including Adi Lavi, Adiel, and other team members — truly understands the company's identity. Each person interprets it based on their own comfort zone. Rami advocates keeping the brand umbrella broad to leave room for growth beyond the current psychiatric services offering.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-12-02: Rami explicitly disagrees with Adi Lavi's website positioning of Telia'z = psychiatric services
- 2025-12-02: Rami proposes "Telia'z Health by Telia'z" as broader brand framework
- Rami believes he is the only person who truly understands the company's DNA and trajectory

## Impact

This assumption affects marketing strategy, company positioning, investor communications, and the UK expansion narrative. If Rami's position prevails, marketing would need to reposition. If Adi's narrower definition holds, expansion into non-psychiatric healthcare would require rebranding.
