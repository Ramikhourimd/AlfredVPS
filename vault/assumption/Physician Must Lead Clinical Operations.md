## Assumption

A licensed physician must lead the clinical operation at each clinic district. Non-physician managers cannot hold ultimate clinical authority, both for regulatory compliance and quality-of-care reasons.

## Basis

- Rami's position throughout the Feb 22 meeting: clinical leadership requires medical expertise
- Ministry of Health regulations in Israel require physician oversight for psychiatric services
- Quality of clinical decision-making depends on having a physician at the helm

## Risk if Wrong

If this assumption is too rigid, it may limit the talent pool for leadership positions and create bottlenecks where physician availability constrains operational decisions.

## Validation

- Check Ministry of Health regulations for specific requirements on physician oversight in telepsychiatry
- Review how competing telepsychiatry companies structure their clinical leadership
