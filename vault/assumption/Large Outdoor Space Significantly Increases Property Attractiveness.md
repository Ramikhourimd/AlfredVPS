# Large Outdoor Space Significantly Increases Property Attractiveness

## Claim

Properties with large balconies, verandas, or convertible outdoor space are viewed significantly more favorably. A 37 sqm balcony was described as a "strong positive" during the 2025-10-09 property evaluation, and the potential to convert a veranda into a garden was discussed positively for a separate property the same day.

## Basis

During apartment viewings on 2025-10-09, the 126 sqm mini penthouse's 37 sqm balcony was singled out as a major advantage — despite the property ultimately being declined on kitchen ventilation grounds. Separately, the investment property discussion highlighted veranda-to-garden conversion as adding value. This preference is distinct from the balcony privacy concern (which is about exposure, not size).

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: 37 sqm balcony described as "strong positive" in Property 2 evaluation
- 2025-10-09: Veranda-to-garden conversion mentioned positively for investment property
- 2025-10-09: 20 sqm balcony on Property 3 described as "decent" but apartment too small overall

## Impact

Properties with generous outdoor space (30+ sqm balcony/veranda) should be prioritized in search results. This criterion interacts with the balcony privacy assumption — large outdoor space is valued, but only if it provides adequate privacy.
