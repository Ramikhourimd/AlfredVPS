# Teams Video Issues Cause Approximately 10 Percent Revenue Loss

## Claim

Approximately 10% of potential revenue or gross profit is lost due to patient no-shows and delayed session starts caused by Microsoft Teams video integration problems. Patients experience technical difficulties entering calls, wasting reserved psychiatrist time slots.

## Basis

Rami's estimate during the Retool prioritization discussion. During the time patients struggle to join, the clinic pays for reserved slots. Some patients give up entirely, resulting in complete no-shows.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-12-05: Rami estimates ~10%+ loss of potential revenue/gross profit from Teams issues

## Impact

This assumption drives the prioritization of Teams video integration as a critical Track 1 item. If validated, it justifies significant investment (3-4 months contractor work). If the actual loss is lower, the priority relative to other Track 1 items may shift.
