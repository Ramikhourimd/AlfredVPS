# Residency Pipeline Blended Cost Converges to 340 NIS Per Hour

## Claim

By recruiting psychiatrists from early residency (120 NIS/hour as case managers) and limiting specialists to 20% of staff (450 NIS/hour), the blended average hourly cost per psychiatrist will converge to approximately 340 NIS/hour across the team.

## Basis

Rami calculated this based on the four-tier compensation structure (120/300/350/450) and the assumption of 4 sessions per hour at 2-3 patients per session. The 20% specialist cap is the key cost control lever.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-14: Initial calculation presented in Rami-Shira management meeting
- Previous experience with residents Sofian and Hala showed first-year residents need more support but cost significantly less

## Impact

This assumption underpins the entire recruitment strategy and the 1M NIS break-even projection. If the blended cost is higher (e.g., more specialists needed, or residents leave before reaching higher tiers), the financial model breaks.
