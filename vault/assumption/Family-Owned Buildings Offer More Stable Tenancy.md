# Family-Owned Buildings Offer More Stable Tenancy

## Claim

Renting from a family-owned building where the landlord lives on-site provides a more stable, long-term tenancy compared to renting from corporate or absentee landlords. The family ownership structure is viewed as a positive signal.

## Basis

During the 2025-10-09 building viewing, the fact that the owner lives on-site alongside his brother, father, and grandmother was noted. The owner offered a 3-year lease at a fixed price, and the assessment explicitly concluded that "the family ownership structure suggests stable, long-term landlord relationship." This was listed among the property's advantages, suggesting it influenced the family's evaluation positively.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: Family building viewed; 3-year fixed lease offered; family ownership noted as advantage

## Impact

This assumption may lead the family to prioritize family-owned or small-landlord properties over larger developments. It also sets an expectation for lease stability (multi-year, fixed price) that may not hold in all cases.
