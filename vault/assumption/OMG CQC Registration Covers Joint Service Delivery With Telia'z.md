# OMG CQC Registration Covers Joint Service Delivery With Telia'z

## Claim

OMG Healthcare's existing CQC registration is sufficient to cover the joint ADHD service where OMG handles governance, triage, and regulatory compliance while Telia'z provides the psychiatric clinical expertise and technology platform. No separate CQC registration is needed for Telia'z under this arrangement.

## Basis

In the July 2025 pathway design meeting, Adiel described OMG as holding the CQC registration and handling clinical governance/safeguarding, with Telia'z operating as the psychiatric service provider within that framework. This mirrors the Leon partnership model but at larger scale with an established NHS healthcare provider.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-03: OMG described as registered healthcare provider with own CQC license, ~100,000 GP patients, ~400,000 acute care patients annually
- OMG handles: regulatory/governance layer, physical infrastructure, CQC compliance
- Telia'z handles: psychiatric clinical expertise, AI platform, clinical workflow
- Pending: Formal CQC confirmation that this embedded model is compliant

## Impact

If OMG's CQC covers the joint service, this becomes the primary NHS pathway — a faster route to NHS referrals than obtaining own CQC. If CQC rules require Telia'z to have separate registration even under OMG, the NHS pathway timeline extends significantly.
