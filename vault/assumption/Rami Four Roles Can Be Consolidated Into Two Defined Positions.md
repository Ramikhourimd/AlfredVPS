# Rami Four Roles Can Be Consolidated Into Two Defined Positions

## Claim

Rami's current four overlapping roles — CEO substitute, Clinic Medical Director, Head of Clinical Operations, and Innovation/AI Lead — can be cleanly consolidated into two well-defined positions: CMO (company-level medical authority) and Innovation Lead.

## Basis

The Define Rami Role task explicitly targets this consolidation. The assumption is that the four roles have enough overlap that two positions can absorb all responsibilities, and that hiring a clinic-level Medical Director will free Rami from day-to-day clinical management. However, no analysis has been done on whether the CEO substitute function fits cleanly into either the CMO or Innovation Lead role, or what happens during periods when Oren is unavailable.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Define Rami Role task created with explicit 4-to-2 consolidation target
- Dependent on hiring a Medical Director to absorb clinic-level duties
- CEO substitute role not clearly addressed in either target position

## Impact

If consolidation fails, Rami remains stretched across too many roles, which was the original problem. The CEO substitute function is particularly risky — it implies executive authority that may conflict with a pure CMO or Innovation Lead scope. The transition timeline also depends on the Medical Director hire.
