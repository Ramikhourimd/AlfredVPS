# Israel Clinical Performance Metrics Are Transferable as UK Sales Evidence

## Claim

The team uses Israel clinic performance data — 2,700+ unique patients, 9,600+ sessions, 3.5-day referral-to-psychiatrist turnaround, 81.3% NPS, 800+ patients per month, 60% operational time savings for psychiatrists, 550% growth over 12 months — as primary evidence when pitching UK partners. The implicit assumption is that these metrics are achievable in the UK context and credible to NHS stakeholders.

## Basis

During the GP Confederation meeting (2025-01-22), Adiel presented the full Israel statistics as the core value proposition. These numbers drove the partnership conversation forward, with Jim Barwick and Sean Rejabris engaging on commercial terms based on these figures.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-01-22: Full Israel metrics presented to GP Confederation Leeds as evidence of service capability
- 2025-02-11: Demo rehearsal prepared around Israel workflow as representative of UK delivery model

## Impact

UK operating conditions differ substantially from Israel: different regulatory environment (CQC, NICE, NHS governance), different workforce (UK case managers vs Israeli staff), different patient demographics, and different payer structure (NHS tariff vs Israeli healthcare). If UK metrics diverge significantly from Israel benchmarks — particularly the 3.5-day turnaround or 60% psychiatrist time savings — this could undermine partner confidence and commercial viability within NHS tariff pricing.
