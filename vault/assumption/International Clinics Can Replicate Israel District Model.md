# International Clinics Can Replicate Israel District Model

## Assumption

The organizational architecture being designed for the Israel clinic serves as a template for future international clinics. Each new clinic would have its own Operations Manager, Medical Director, and Administrative Manager, while consuming shared services (HR, marketing, finance, tech) from the parent company.

## Risk If Wrong

If regulatory, cultural, or operational differences make the template unworkable, each new country may require significant organizational redesign, increasing expansion cost and timeline. UK and France may have fundamentally different requirements for clinical governance.

## Validation

- Test during UK expansion planning
- Consult with local regulatory experts in target countries
- Compare healthcare governance requirements across jurisdictions
