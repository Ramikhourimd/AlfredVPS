# New Manager First Month Should Be Orientation and Mapping Not Operations

## Claim

The incoming clinic general manager should spend the entire first month meeting staff, understanding the organization, and mapping processes — without taking operational authority. This builds relationships and contextual understanding before making changes.

## Basis

Rami stated during the 2025-11-09 organizational structure discussion that the new manager should "meet everyone who works here, understand what it means to work with us" before taking on operational responsibilities. The clinic's complexity requires deep familiarity before effective management.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-11-09: Rami and Dekkel agreed on gradual onboarding approach during organizational structure discussion

## Impact

This assumption shapes the onboarding timeline — operational handoff from Rami/Shira to the new manager will not happen immediately upon hire, adding approximately one month to the transition period.
