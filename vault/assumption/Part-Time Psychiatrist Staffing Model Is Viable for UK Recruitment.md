# Part-Time Psychiatrist Staffing Model Is Viable for UK Recruitment

## Claim

The Israel clinic operates with 10 part-time psychiatrists handling 800+ patients per month and 2,000+ monthly sessions. The team implicitly assumes this part-time staffing model will be replicable in the UK market, where psychiatric workforce availability and employment norms differ significantly from Israel.

## Basis

During the GP Confederation meeting (2025-01-22), Adiel presented the Israel model showing 10 part-time psychiatrists managing the full patient load. This was presented as evidence of operational efficiency, not flagged as a potential UK recruitment challenge. The team has extensively discussed case manager staffing for the UK but has not addressed whether UK psychiatrists would accept part-time telepsychiatry arrangements.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-01-22: GP Confed meeting — Israel model presented with 10 part-time psychiatrists as operational proof
- 2026-02-15: Team meeting — No discussion of UK psychiatrist recruitment model; focus entirely on case manager staffing (Bassem stopgap, local hiring later)

## Impact

If UK psychiatrists cannot be recruited on a part-time basis (due to NHS employment contracts, professional norms, or limited availability), the entire cost model and capacity projections based on the Israel template would need revision. Full-time psychiatrists would fundamentally change the unit economics and recruitment timeline.
