# Refinancing Expected to Save Approximately 200000 NIS

## Claim

Restructuring the mortgage under Option A is expected to yield approximately 200,000 NIS in total cost savings over the remaining life of the loan, while maintaining roughly the same monthly payment (~4,600 NIS).

## Basis

This figure was presented by the RiseUp advisor during the 2026-01-26 consultation. It is an estimate based on current market conditions, the existing mortgage structure, and projected track restructuring. The exact composition of savings (interest reduction, elimination of CPI indexation, track rebalancing) has not been detailed.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-01-26: RiseUp advisor presented ~200,000 NIS savings estimate for Option A

## Impact

This is the central value proposition driving the refinancing decision. If actual savings are materially lower than 200,000 NIS, the cost-benefit calculation (especially against the 6,000 NIS advisory fee) may shift. The estimate should be validated with formal mortgage offers from the bank.
