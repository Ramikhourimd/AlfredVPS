# Amani Rohana May Not Return to Previous Employer After Extended Maternity Leave

## Claim

Amani Rohana, who has been on maternity leave for approximately one year, is unlikely to return to her previous employment. Rami observes that she has not expressed desire to return, took three months to agree to childcare, and shows attachment patterns that suggest a long absence from the workforce.

## Basis

- Amani has been home for ~1 year on maternity leave
- She has not stated she wants to leave her job, but also hasn't signaled intent to return
- It took three months of persuasion to get her to agree to put the child in daycare
- The family advisor warned this is "a dangerous stage" — mothers who get used to staying home find it hard to return
- She would likely need at least three months notice before any return

## Evidence Trail
