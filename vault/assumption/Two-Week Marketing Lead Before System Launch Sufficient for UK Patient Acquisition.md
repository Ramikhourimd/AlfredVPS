# Two-Week Marketing Lead Before System Launch Sufficient for UK Patient Acquisition

## Claim

The team plans to start marketing campaigns in mid-March 2026, approximately two weeks before the March 31 system launch date. The implicit assumption is that this lead time is sufficient to build an initial patient pipeline for the UK clinic operating under Leon's CQC registration.

## Basis

Shachar's launch plan targets mid-March for campaign start and March 31 for full system launch. The team has not discussed or validated whether two weeks is adequate lead time for patient acquisition in a new market where Telia'z has no brand recognition, no existing patient base, and no referral network yet established.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-15: Team meeting confirmed mid-March campaign start, March 31 system launch target
- No market testing or patient acquisition modeling discussed in source records
- Google UK campaign approval process (existing constraint) may further compress actual marketing window

## Impact

If two weeks is insufficient, the UK clinic launches with no patient volume, creating a period of zero revenue while fixed costs (Leon's arrangement, insurance, Israeli case manager allocation) are running. This could undermine confidence in the UK expansion and strain the relationship with Leon.
