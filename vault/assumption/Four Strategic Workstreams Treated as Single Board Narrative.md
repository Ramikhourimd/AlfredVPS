# Four Strategic Workstreams Treated as Single Board Narrative

## Claim

The team treats organizational restructuring, clinic operations, innovation roadmap, and UK expansion as a single unified narrative for board presentation to Alex — rather than as four independent initiatives that could be approved separately. The presentation task bundles all four topics into one meeting with one slide deck.

## Basis

The "Present Clinic and Innovation Plans to Board" task lists four distinct topics (restructuring, clinic operations, innovation roadmap, UK expansion) as a single presentation. The action items include preparing one slide deck covering "all topics" and one review session with Oren before presentation.

## Evidence Trail

- Task created from Feb 22 meeting outcomes
- All four workstreams bundled into single presentation
- No indication of phased or separate board approvals
- UK expansion conditionally included ("if timing is right") but not separated out

## Impact

If any one workstream is not ready, the entire board presentation may be delayed — creating a bottleneck where the least-ready initiative blocks approval for all others. This is particularly risky for UK expansion, which is conditionally included ("if timing is right"), suggesting it may not be ready and could delay the entire presentation. The team may benefit from decoupling board approval into separate, independently-advanceable proposals.
