# Innovation Marketing Must Deliver Value Before External Promotion

## Claim
Telia'z cannot market itself as an innovation-driven workplace for clinicians until it actually delivers a structured technology training program. Promoting the innovation angle without substance risks reputational damage and unmet expectations.

## Basis
Rami Khouri argued strongly in the team meeting that before marketing Telia'z as a place where clinicians learn to be "technology-enabled practitioners," the company must first define what training it provides, build the curriculum, and ensure it creates genuine value for existing staff.

## Evidence Trail
- 2026-02-15: Rami pushed back on Dekkel and Adi's eagerness to immediately market the innovation angle, insisting on substance-first approach

## Impact
Delays the recruitment marketing shift from comfort-based to innovation-based messaging. Requires Rami and team to define and build the "Psychiatry Academy" curriculum first.
