# AI-Generated Clinical Reports Will Be Accepted Under UK Regulatory Framework

## Claim

The Telia'z platform generates AI-powered clinical summaries, agenda recommendations, and final reports (produced in ~10 seconds post-session). The team is demonstrating and planning around these AI-generated outputs for UK clinical use without explicit validation that UK regulators (CQC, NICE) will accept AI-generated clinical documentation as part of the formal patient record.

## Basis

In the platform demo rehearsal (2025-02-11), the AI report generation was presented as a core feature — the psychiatrist session produces an AI-generated summary that becomes the clinical letter. In the GP Confed meeting (2025-01-22), AI-generated reports were presented as a key efficiency gain. NICE released new digital/AI guidance for neurodevelopmental services in December 2024, but the team has not confirmed compliance with these specific guidelines.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-01-22: AI report generation presented to GP Confed as core workflow feature
- 2025-02-11: Demo rehearsal includes AI-generated summary as standard output
- 2025-12: NICE releases digital/AI guidance for neurodevelopmental services (not yet reviewed against Telia'z capabilities)

## Impact

If AI-generated clinical reports require additional regulatory approval or are not accepted under CQC/NICE standards, the entire clinical workflow must be redesigned to include manual report writing, eliminating a key efficiency advantage of the Telia'z model.
