# Marketing Budget Not Included in Clalit Growth Projections

## Claim

The financial projections for the Clalit partnership do not include any marketing or patient acquisition budget. Growth within both Maccabi and Clalit HMOs requires active field work and relationship building at the district level, which has not been costed.

## Basis

During the 2025-11-09 debrief, Rami directly asked whether a marketing budget had been factored into the equations. The answer was "no, not at all." This is concerning because HMO growth requires district-level engagement, relationship building with district managers and deputies, and potentially embedding someone within the HMO organization.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-11-09: Rami raises the missing marketing budget question — confirmed absent
- Earlier attempt to get contact names from Anat (Maccabi) was unsuccessful — she did not provide district-level contacts
- Maccabi North deputy district manager indicated the need for direct engagement at district level

## Impact

Financial projections for Clalit profitability may be significantly understated. Growth may stall without dedicated patient acquisition resources. This affects both the Clalit agreement value proposition and the broader growth strategy presented to the board.
