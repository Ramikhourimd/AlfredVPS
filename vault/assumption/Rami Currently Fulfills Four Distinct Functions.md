# Rami Currently Fulfills Four Distinct Functions

## Claim

Rami currently performs four distinct functions within Telia'z: (1) de facto Medical Director / clinical oversight, (2) operational involvement in clinic management, (3) innovation and technology leadership, and (4) strategic medical authority. The restructuring aims to redistribute these so Rami focuses on two: CMO responsibilities and Innovation leadership.

## Basis

The meeting notes state that "Rami's role will be redefined from 4 current functions to 2 primary areas: CMO responsibilities + Innovation leadership." This implies that the current organizational structure overloads Rami with responsibilities that the restructuring must decompose across new roles (Medical Director hire, Basel as Operations Manager).

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: CMO decision record notes the reduction from 4 functions to 2.
- Conversation record notes Rami needs to "reflect on his preferred role definition."

## Impact

This assumption is foundational to the restructuring logic. If Rami's current workload is not accurately decomposed into four distinct functions, the redistribution may leave gaps (functions no one covers) or create overlap (new roles conflicting with retained Rami responsibilities). The success of the Medical Director hire and Basel's Operations Manager role depends on cleanly separating these functions.
