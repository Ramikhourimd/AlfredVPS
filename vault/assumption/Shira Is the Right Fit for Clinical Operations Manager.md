# Shira Is the Right Fit for Clinical Operations Manager

## Claim

The leadership team maps Shira to the Clinical Operations Manager role in the new three-pillar district model without explicit discussion of her qualifications, readiness, or alternative candidates. The meeting note states this position is "currently mapped to Shira" as a fait accompli rather than a deliberate staffing decision.

## Basis

During the Feb 22 meeting, Basel and Leah's presentation of the district model listed Shira as the Clinical Operations Manager responsible for day-to-day clinic operations, KPIs, scheduling, case manager coordination, and audit/quality processes. No discussion of alternative candidates or gap analysis against the role requirements appears in the meeting record.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Meeting note states Clinical Operations Manager is "currently mapped to Shira" without further qualification discussion.

## Impact

If Shira lacks specific competencies required by the expanded role (e.g., KPI management, quality audit processes), the three-pillar model may underperform. The assumption that existing staff automatically fit new structural roles is common in restructurings but frequently leads to performance gaps. A formal role-fit assessment would validate or challenge this assumption.
