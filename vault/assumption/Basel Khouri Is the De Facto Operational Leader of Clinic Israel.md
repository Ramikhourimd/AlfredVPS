# Basel Khouri Is the De Facto Operational Leader of Clinic Israel

## Claim

Basel Khouri is the de facto operational leader of Telia'z Clinic Israel. He has been assigned both the clinic operational plan presentation and the Medical Director hiring process, indicating he holds operational authority over the clinic's day-to-day management and staffing decisions.

## Basis

Two key task assignments from the 2026-02-22 meeting point to this:
1. Basel is tasked with creating and presenting the clinic operational plan (KPIs, staffing, metrics)
2. Basel is assigned to lead the Medical Director hiring process (job description, interviews, contract)

These are not administrative tasks — they represent ownership of clinic operations and organisational design.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Assigned operational plan presentation task
- 2026-02-22: Assigned Medical Director hiring task

## Impact

If Basel is the operational leader, then:
- The operational plan reflects his vision and priorities
- Medical Director hiring criteria and reporting lines will be shaped by his judgment
- Clinic restructuring velocity depends on his bandwidth
- His departure or reassignment would create a leadership vacuum on the operational side
