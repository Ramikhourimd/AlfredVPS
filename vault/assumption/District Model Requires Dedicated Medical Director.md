# District Model Requires Dedicated Medical Director

## Claim

The district model being adopted for Telia'z Clinic Israel requires a dedicated Medical Director role at 30% position to handle clinical supervision of psychiatrists.

## Basis

The hiring task for Medical Director references "the district model" as the driving requirement for this role, treating it as an established organizational decision rather than a proposal under consideration.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Task created for hiring Medical Director, referencing district model as given context.

## Impact

Drives the Medical Director hiring task, defines reporting lines (to CMO on clinical matters), and shapes the overall clinic supervisory structure.
