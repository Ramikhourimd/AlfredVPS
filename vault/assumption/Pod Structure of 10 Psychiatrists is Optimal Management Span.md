# Pod Structure of 10 Psychiatrists is Optimal Management Span

## Claim

Organizing psychiatrists into pods of approximately 10, each led by a senior clinician, is the right management span for scaling Telia'z's clinical workforce.

## Basis

Proposed during the Feb 22 restructuring meeting as part of the district model. The number appears to be based on general management theory about span of control rather than empirical data from Telia'z's own operations. No evidence found that this was benchmarked against other telepsychiatry practices or tested with the current team.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Pod structure proposed in restructuring meeting discussion
- No pilot data or comparison with other organizations found in records

## Impact

The pod structure directly affects hiring plans, the Medical Director's workload, and the scalability of the district model. If 10 is too many, clinical quality suffers; if too few, management overhead becomes unsustainable. This assumption also propagates to the UK expansion — pod size will determine staffing ratios internationally.
