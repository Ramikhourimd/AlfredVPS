# ADHD Is a Familial Trait Compensated by Intelligence Across Khouri Family

## Claim

ADHD is not unique to Rami — it runs in the Khouri family. Rami explicitly states "we were all like this before," indicating multiple family members share the trait. The family compensated through intelligence, masking the condition and delaying diagnosis. Rami's own ADHD went undiagnosed through six years of medical school despite clear symptoms: inability to read textbooks, inability to sit through lectures, bringing Sudoku puzzles to class as a coping mechanism.

## Basis

During a family conversation on 2025-10-09, Rami described his ADHD history to his mother Leah. He explained that with medication he functions effectively, but without it the baseline deficit is amplified. The family pattern of high intelligence masking ADHD explains why the condition was never identified during formal education.
