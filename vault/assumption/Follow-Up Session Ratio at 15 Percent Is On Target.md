# Follow-Up Session Ratio at 15 Percent Is On Target

## Claim

The clinic's follow-up session ratio of 15% (vs 85% intake/new patients) meets the management-defined target of 15% follow-ups.

## Basis

The January 2026 KPI dashboard shows the actual session type distribution matches the stated target exactly (85% intake, 15% follow-up). The dashboard presents this as a target being met.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-01-11: KPI dashboard confirms 15% follow-up ratio matches target.

## Impact

If the follow-up ratio is genuinely on target, it suggests the clinic is managing its patient throughput and retention as planned. However, the low follow-up percentage may also indicate high patient churn — worth monitoring whether this is intentional (intake-focused growth model) or a retention problem.
