# 30% Position Sufficient for Medical Director Supervision

## Claim

A 30% (part-time) position is sufficient for the Medical Director to handle clinical supervision of all psychiatrists in the clinic.

## Basis

The hiring task specifies a 30% position without discussion of whether this is adequate for the supervision workload. This implies the team believes the current or projected psychiatrist headcount can be supervised within this allocation.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Hiring task specifies 30% position as the target contract size.

## Impact

Determines budget allocation for the Medical Director role. If the clinic grows significantly, this assumption may need revisiting — a larger psychiatrist roster may require more than 30% supervision time.
