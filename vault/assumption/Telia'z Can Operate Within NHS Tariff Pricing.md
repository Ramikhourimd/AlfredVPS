# Telia'z Can Operate Within NHS Tariff Pricing

## Claim

Telia'z can deliver its AI-enabled telepsychiatry ADHD service within NHS tariff pricing — benchmarked at approximately £360 per intake assessment and £180 per follow-up appointment — while maintaining viable margins.

## Basis

During the GP Confederation partnership meeting on 2025-01-22, Adiel presented these NHS pricing benchmarks as Telia'z's target operating range. Jim Barwick stated bluntly: "If we can't work out the commercial bit, we don't go any further." The assumption is that Telia'z's AI-driven efficiency (60% operational time savings for psychiatrists, high patient throughput) makes NHS tariff pricing commercially viable despite being below private market rates.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-01-22: NHS tariff benchmarks cited — £360 intake, £180 follow-up
- Israel reference: 800+ patients/month with 10 part-time psychiatrists demonstrates high-throughput model
- Pending: Actual UK cost modeling including insurance, CQC compliance, UK staff costs

## Impact

If this assumption holds, Telia'z can compete for NHS tenders and serve ICB-referred patients. If UK operational costs (insurance, compliance, local staff) push margins below viability at NHS tariffs, the business model shifts to private/Right to Choose patients only.
