# Natural Light and Kitchen Ventilation Are Dealbreakers

## Claim

Properties lacking natural light — particularly kitchens without windows or adequate ventilation — will be rejected regardless of other qualities. Natural light is a hard requirement, not a preference.

## Basis

The 126 sqm mini penthouse with 37 sqm balcony (a strong layout candidate) was effectively dismissed when the family discovered the kitchen had no window and the only opening faced a neighbor's bathroom wall, blocking both light and airflow. The property also had "limited natural light overall — only one main light source direction," which was flagged as a separate concern. Despite the attractive size and balcony, these light/ventilation issues were treated as disqualifying.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: 126 sqm mini penthouse rejected primarily due to windowless kitchen and limited natural light

## Impact

This narrows the search significantly. Many converted apartments (where rooms are merged or reconfigured) may lose kitchen windows in the process. Agents or listings should be screened for kitchen window presence before scheduling viewings.
