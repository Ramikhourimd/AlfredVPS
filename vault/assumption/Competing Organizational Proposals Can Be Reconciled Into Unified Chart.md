# Competing Organizational Proposals Can Be Reconciled Into Unified Chart

## Claim

The task to finalize the organizational chart assumes that Basel and Leah's structural proposals and Rami's counter-proposals can be merged into a single, agreed-upon chart. The task expects a unified deliverable despite the meeting ending without final decisions.

## Basis

The finalize org chart task lists clear deliverables (visual chart, role descriptions, reporting lines, resource sharing agreements) as though reconciliation is straightforward. However, the source conversation records "no final decisions were made" and describes fundamental disagreements — particularly about whether clinical and operational authority should be combined or separated.

## Evidence Trail

- Feb 22 meeting: Basel and Leah presented proposals; Rami counter-proposed (task/Finalize Organizational Chart.md)
- Meeting outcome: "No final decisions were made" (conversation/Telia'z Organizational Restructuring Meeting.md)
- Emotional undercurrents: Rami feeling sidelined, Oren emphasizing separation of clinical and operational management

## Impact

If this assumption fails — if the proposals cannot be reconciled — the org chart task becomes blocked, which cascades to blocking new hires (per the existing constraint) and the board presentation to Alex.
