# Leon Practice Website Can Host Embedded Patient Intake Flow

## Claim

The team assumes Leon's existing practice website has sufficient technical infrastructure to embed or link to the Telia'z patient intake flow. Yael showed Leon the patient flow design for integration into his website on Feb 13, and the team is planning marketing campaigns that drive traffic to Leon's site as the patient entry point.

## Basis

In both Feb 15 meeting notes, the patient acquisition flow is described as: patients arrive at Leon's practice website → enter Telia'z assessment flow. Yael (Li Yamin) met Leon and presented the initial patient flow design. No technical assessment of Leon's website capabilities, hosting, or integration requirements has been mentioned.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-13: Yael met Leon, showed patient flow design for his website
- 2026-02-15: Team meeting confirms website integration as the patient entry path
- No record of technical website audit or integration feasibility assessment

## Impact

If Leon's website cannot support the intake flow (e.g., no ability to embed forms, limited hosting, requires third-party web developer), the patient acquisition timeline could slip. The mid-March marketing campaign depends on this website being ready to receive traffic. A fallback would require either a standalone landing page or redirecting to a Telia'z-hosted page — which may conflict with the CQC requirement that patients enter through Leon's registered practice.
