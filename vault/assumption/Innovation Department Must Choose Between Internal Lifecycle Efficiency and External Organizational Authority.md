# Innovation Department Must Choose Between Internal Lifecycle Efficiency and External Organizational Authority

## Claim

The innovation department at Telia'z faces a fundamental design tension between two evidence-based organizational models: one prioritizing a clear, function-focused internal structure that streamlines the innovation lifecycle from clinical insight to deployed product, and one prioritizing external authority and organizational independence. This choice determines whether the department becomes "a real catalyst for breakthroughs" or "just an expensive service bureau."
