# Protocol Framework Will Convince Leadership to Change Priorities

## Claim

If Rami presents a structured, evidence-based protocol framework to Telia'z leadership — mapping clinical protocols, administrative protocols, KPIs, and the clinical-to-business outcomes cascade — leadership will shift their priorities from surface metrics (new patients, campaign ROI, revenue) toward deeper operational protocols (safety, quality, training, communication).

The assumption is that the problem is one of communication and framing, not of fundamental disagreement — i.e., leadership would agree to prioritize protocols if shown the evidence clearly.

If false: a different approach is needed — perhaps escalation, external advisor involvement, or structural authority changes.
