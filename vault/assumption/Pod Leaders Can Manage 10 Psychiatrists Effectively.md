## Assumption

A senior clinician serving as pod leader can effectively supervise and mentor approximately 10 psychiatrists. This ratio provides adequate oversight while being manageable for the pod leader alongside their own clinical duties.

## Basis

- Rami's clinical management experience suggests 10 as a workable ratio
- Similar ratios used in hospital department structures

## Risk if Wrong

If 10 is too many, quality of supervision suffers and pod leaders burn out. If too few, the model becomes expensive (too many pod leaders needed). The optimal number may vary by experience level of the psychiatrists in each pod.

## Validation

- Pilot with one pod before scaling
- Survey pod leaders after 3 months on workload and effectiveness
