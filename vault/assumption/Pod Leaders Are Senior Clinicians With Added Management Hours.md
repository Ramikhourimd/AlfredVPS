# Pod Leaders Are Senior Clinicians With Added Management Hours

## Claim

Pod leaders in the clinic's scaling model are senior clinicians who take on an additional ~10 management hours per month on top of their clinical duties. They are not dedicated managers — they are practicing clinicians with a management overlay.

## Basis

Explicitly described in the 2026-02-22 leadership meeting: "Pods: Groups of ~10 practitioners managed by a pod lead (senior clinician with added management hours, ~10 hours/month)." The model assumes clinical credibility of the pod leader is essential for team cohesion.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Basel and Leah's organizational proposal defines pod leaders as senior clinicians with 10 hrs/month management allocation.

## Impact

- If 10 hours/month is insufficient for managing a pod of 10, the model breaks down
- Compensation model must account for this management overlay
- Pod leaders need management training in addition to clinical skills
- Recruitment must target clinicians willing to take on management responsibilities
