# Clinical Practice Generates Additional 3-4K NIS Monthly via Company Benefits

## Claim

Beyond the direct clinical practice income (expected to stabilize at 18-20K NIS/month), combining it with company output (osek) creates an additional income stream of roughly 3,000-4,000 NIS per month from company deductions and benefits.

## Basis

Discussed during the 2025-09-23 financial planning meeting. The accountant noted that when clinical practice income is structured through the company entity, deductions and benefit allocations generate an additional 3-4K NIS monthly on top of the base clinical income.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-09-23: Accountant confirmed ~3,000-4,000 NIS additional from company deductions/benefits alongside clinical practice income

## Impact

- Total effective clinical income is closer to 21-24K NIS/month when including company benefits
- This affects overall financial planning and tax structuring calculations
- Depends on the clinical income actually reaching the 18-20K NIS/month base level
