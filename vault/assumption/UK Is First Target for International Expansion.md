# UK Is First Target for International Expansion

## Claim

The United Kingdom is the specific first target for Telia'z international expansion beyond Israel. The board presentation agenda lists "UK Expansion" as a distinct topic — including market opportunity, regulatory considerations, and resource requirements — indicating this is beyond speculation and is actively being planned for board-level discussion.

## Basis

The task "Present Clinic and Innovation Plans to Board" includes UK Expansion as one of four major agenda items for the Alex presentation, with the caveat "if timing is right." This is the first mention of a specific country target, moving international expansion from abstract planning to concrete geography.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Board presentation task lists UK Expansion with sub-items (market opportunity, regulatory considerations, resource requirements)
- Qualifier "if timing is right" suggests it may be deferred but is actively being evaluated

## Impact

If the UK is confirmed as the first expansion target, it shapes:
- The urgency of finalizing the Israel district model as a replicable template
- Regulatory research needs (UK healthcare licensing differs significantly from Israel)
- The need for a UK-licensed Medical Director per existing constraint
- Resource allocation between consolidating Israel operations and preparing UK entry
