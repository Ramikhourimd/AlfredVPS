# Clickx Can Support Conners Questionnaire Integration

## Claim

The Clickx EHR system can support integration of Conners questionnaires (ADHD assessment tools) for the children clinic workflow, allowing questionnaires to be administered, scored, and stored within the EHR.

## Basis

During the 2025-11-10 operations meeting, Rami raised the need to integrate Conners questionnaires into Clickx. The assumption is that the system has the capability to support custom questionnaire workflows. This has not been verified with the Clickx vendor.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-11-10: Need identified; no verification of Clickx capabilities performed

## Impact

If Clickx cannot support questionnaire integration:
- Children clinic will need an alternative workflow (paper-based or separate digital tool)
- Data will remain fragmented across systems
- Clinical efficiency for ADHD assessments will not improve
