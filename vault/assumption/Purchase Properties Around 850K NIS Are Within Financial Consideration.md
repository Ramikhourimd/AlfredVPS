# Purchase Properties Around 850K NIS Are Within Financial Consideration

## Claim

While market-ready houses in the Haifa area cost 4.3-5.2M NIS (far exceeding apparent family means), the family seriously evaluated a property at approximately 850K NIS on 2025-10-09. This suggests a tier of affordable purchase properties — likely requiring renovation — falls within the family's financial reach or at least within their consideration range.

## Basis

During the Property Investment Discussion (2025-10-09), a property with an "acquisition cost around 850K NIS" was discussed with genuine interest. The family evaluated its advantages (clinic potential, size, outdoor space) and concerns (renovation costs, location) — indicating this was a serious consideration, not aspirational browsing. This stands in tension with the existing constraint that purchase prices far exceed the family budget.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-10-09: Property at ~850K NIS evaluated with specific pros/cons analysis (Property Investment Discussion)
- 2025-10-09: Same day, rental units at 1,200-1,500/month also viewed — both paths active simultaneously

## Impact

If confirmed, this opens a third housing path alongside renting (1,200-1,500/month) and the Maghar land development: purchasing an affordable property and renovating it. However, the additional renovation capital required (electrical, finishing) means the true cost exceeds 850K, and confidence remains low until clearer financial capacity data emerges.
