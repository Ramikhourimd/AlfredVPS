# Medical Director at 30 Percent Position is Sufficient for Clinic

## Claim

A Medical Director hired at approximately 30% position (~2 days per week) can adequately fulfill the clinical leadership role at the Israel clinic, managing day-to-day clinical operations and supervising the psychiatrist team.

## Basis

The Hire Medical Director task specifies "approximately 30% position" without analysis of whether this is sufficient. The assumption appears driven by budget constraints and the belief that Rami's overall CMO authority provides a safety net. The telepsychiatry model may reduce the need for constant on-site clinical leadership compared to a physical clinic.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Hire Medical Director task created with 30% position specification
- No workload analysis or benchmarking against similar clinics found in records

## Impact

If this assumption is wrong, the clinic could face clinical quality issues, regulatory scrutiny, or psychiatrist supervision gaps. The pod structure (10 psychiatrists per pod) may require more hands-on leadership than 2 days/week allows, especially during scaling.
