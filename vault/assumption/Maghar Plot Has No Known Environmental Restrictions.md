# Maghar Plot Has No Known Environmental Restrictions

## Claim

The family land plot at Block 2054, Plot 32 (~1,100 sqm) near Maghar has no known environmental restrictions that would limit development. This was explicitly assessed as part of the 2025-09-23 feasibility analysis and found to be clear.

## Basis

The comprehensive investment feasibility analysis (2025-09-23) lists environmental restrictions as "None known" under land characteristics. This assessment covers the plot's suitability for commercial, logistics, tourism, and energy uses without environmental compliance barriers.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-09-23: Feasibility analysis explicitly states "Environmental restrictions: None known" for Block 2054, Plot 32

## Impact

This assumption underpins all 11+ development options evaluated in the feasibility analysis. If environmental restrictions were later discovered (e.g., protected species habitat, watershed protection, archaeological significance), it could invalidate multiple development scenarios and require costly environmental impact assessments. This should be verified through formal due diligence before committing capital to any development phase.
