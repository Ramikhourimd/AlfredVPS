# International Expansion Drives Restructuring Urgency

## Claim

The organizational restructuring is not merely about improving the existing Israeli clinic — it is fundamentally driven by the need to support multi-clinic international expansion, particularly the UK operation. The structure being designed must work across geographies.

## Basis

The meeting context explicitly states the focus is on "Telia'z as it transitions from a single Israeli telepsychiatry clinic to a multi-clinic international operation." The UK Expansion project is directly referenced. The shared services model, C-level role definitions, and pod scaling model all assume international operations.

## Evidence Trail

- 2026-02-22: Meeting framed around transition to "multi-clinic international operation"
- Project links include both Telia'z Clinic Israel and Telia'z UK Expansion

## Impact

- Organizational structure must be geography-agnostic or explicitly multi-geography
- Shared services model must account for different regulatory environments
- Clinical protocols need cross-border consistency framework
- Timeline pressure — restructuring must be in place before UK launch
