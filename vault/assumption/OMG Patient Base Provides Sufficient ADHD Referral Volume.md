# OMG Patient Base Provides Sufficient ADHD Referral Volume

## Claim

The team implicitly assumes that OMG Healthcare's patient base (~100,000 GP patients, ~400,000 acute care patients annually) will generate sufficient ADHD referral volume to sustain the Telia'z UK clinic operation. OMG's scale is cited as a positive factor in the partnership, but no conversion analysis (GP patients → ADHD referrals → completed assessments) has been documented.

## Basis

The OMG partnership meeting (2025-07-03) noted OMG's patient numbers as context for the partnership value. The 25-year Leeds ADHD waiting list suggests massive unmet demand exists. However, the pathway from OMG's general patient base to ADHD-specific referrals entering the Telia'z system involves multiple conversion steps: GP identification, referral decision, OMG triage acceptance, and patient consent for virtual assessment.

## Evidence Trail

- 2025-07-03: OMG patient numbers cited (~100K GP, ~400K acute)
- Leeds ADHD waiting list of 25 years suggests large latent demand
- No documented referral conversion rate analysis
- No comparison to Israel conversion metrics

## Impact

If ADHD referral conversion from the OMG base is lower than expected, the UK clinic may not reach the patient volume needed to justify local staffing and operational costs. This directly affects the assumption that UK patient volume will justify local case manager recruitment.
