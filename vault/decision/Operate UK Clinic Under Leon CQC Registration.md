# Operate UK Clinic Under Leon CQC Registration

## Context
Telia'z needs CQC (Care Quality Commission) registration to operate a clinic in the UK. Obtaining own CQC registration is a long process.

## Options Considered
1. **Option A** — Partner with Leon (UK family doctor who already has CQC) and operate under his registration
2. **Option B** — Apply for own CQC registration independently (longer timeline)

## Decision
Proceed with Option A as primary track: partner with Leon and operate under his CQC registration. Pursue Option B (own CQC via NHS) in parallel as a longer-term strategic path.

## Rationale
Leon already holds CQC, enabling much faster launch. Patients can be transferred to his practice immediately after contract signing. Own CQC pursued in parallel for future independence and NHS referral access.

## Consequences
- Dependent on Leon for regulatory compliance
- Need to share ADHD SOPs with Leon (other SOPs are his)
- Revenue sharing arrangement (percentage to Leon)
- Can begin patient operations much faster (weeks vs months)
