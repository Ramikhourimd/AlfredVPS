# Delegate Psychiatrist System Complaint Triage to Rivi Idelman

## Context

Psychiatrists were reporting system complaints (slow Retool, deleted summaries, authentication lockouts) through informal WhatsApp channels with no structured response process. Shira raised these issues during a discussion about the ER referral form and broader platform stability.

## Options Considered

1. **Shira handles triage directly** — she already receives the complaints but lacks technical context to prioritize
2. **Shachar handles triage** — R&D lead could assess directly but is capacity-constrained with the platform rebuild
3. **Rivi Idelman handles triage** — Innovation team member with both clinical understanding and technical liaison capability

## Decision

Rami delegated psychiatrist system complaint triage to Rivi Idelman. Rivi serves as the liaison between clinical staff reporting issues and the R&D team responsible for fixes. This is distinct from adding Stav Zamir to the psychiatrist communication group (which provides visibility) — Rivi's role is active triage and prioritization.

## Rationale

Rivi sits at the intersection of clinical operations and innovation, making her well-positioned to assess complaint severity, translate clinical impact into technical priority, and coordinate with Shachar's team. This also keeps Shira focused on clinical leadership rather than technical issue management.

## Consequences

- Rivi becomes the primary intake point for psychiatrist system complaints
- Complaints get structured prioritization before reaching R&D
- Shira is freed from technical triage responsibility
- Creates a dependency on Rivi's availability for complaint response time
