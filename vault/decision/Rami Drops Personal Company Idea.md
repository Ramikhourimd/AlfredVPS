# Rami Drops Personal Company Idea

## Context

[[person/Rami Khouri]] had previously explored setting up a personal company (hevra) as a vehicle for receiving on-call compensation and potentially other payments from [[org/Telia'z]]. This would have allowed different tax treatment and business flexibility.

## Decision

Rami dropped the idea of establishing a personal company. On-call compensation will flow through his regular employment payslip instead.

## Terms

- [[person/Oren Taliaz]] will continue supporting Rami's existing company entity through December 2025 (to "fill it a bit" with gap payments).
- After December 2025, the company arrangement is not expected to continue.
- Oren appreciated the decision, noting it removes complexity for due diligence and audits.

## Rationale

Oren explained that the company setup created complications:
- Due diligence auditors would question the arrangement.
- It deviated from standard employment practices.
- For only 4-5 remaining months, the administrative burden outweighed the benefits.

Rami recognized this caused Oren difficulty and voluntarily stepped back from the idea.
