# Adopt District Model for Clinic Israel

## Context

The clinic is restructuring its organisational model. The district model was selected as the target structure, which defines how clinical supervision, management, and staffing are organised.

## Options Considered

1. **District model** — Structured clinical supervision with dedicated Medical Director overseeing psychiatrists by district
2. **Other models** — Not explicitly documented in available records

## Decision

Adopt the district model as the organisational structure for Telia'z Clinic Israel.

## Rationale

Not explicitly stated in source records. The district model is referenced as established context rather than a proposal, suggesting it was decided prior to or during the 2026-02-22 meeting.

## Consequences

- Requires hiring a Medical Director (30% position) for clinical supervision
- Defines reporting lines: Medical Director reports to CMO on clinical matters
- Shapes how psychiatrists are grouped and supervised
- Drives the broader operational plan Basel is preparing
