# Make Teacher Questionnaire Optional for Children Intake

## Context

The children's clinic intake process at [[org/Telia'z]] required a mandatory teacher questionnaire. Out of approximately 30 children in the pipeline, 29 were stuck at this step because parents could not obtain the completed teacher form (coinciding with the school year transition).

## Decision

Make the teacher questionnaire optional rather than mandatory for children's intake. The questionnaire remains required only for specific clinical indications (e.g., ADHD/attention deficit assessments) as determined by [[person/Ahmed Masalha]] (Anan).

## Rationale

- Nurit (children's psychiatrist) confirmed the questionnaire is not clinically mandatory for all cases
- The requirement was blocking nearly all children from progressing through intake
- Children should not be denied treatment due to administrative barriers
- The same policy will apply to both Maccabi and private clinic children

## Follow-up

Anan to define the specific clinical criteria for when the teacher questionnaire is required.
