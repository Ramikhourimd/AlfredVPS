# Use HealthyMind as UK-Facing Clinical Brand

## Context

Telia'z is the corporate entity and technology platform name. For the UK clinical service, the team needed to decide whether to use the Telia'z brand directly or create a consumer-facing clinical brand.

## Options Considered

1. **Use Telia'z brand directly** — Corporate name for all markets
2. **Create UK-specific clinical brand (HealthyMind)** — Separate consumer-facing identity for clinical services
3. **Use partner brand (Leon's practice / GP Confed)** — White-label under partner identity

## Decision

The UK clinical service operates under the "Healthy Mind" brand (sometimes written as "HealthyMind"). Telia'z remains the corporate/technology entity, while Healthy Mind is the patient-facing service identity.

## Rationale

The GP Confed meeting notes describe "Telia'z (trading as Healthy Mind for the clinical service)." A UK-friendly clinical brand is more accessible to NHS patients and commissioners than the Hebrew-origin corporate name. It also allows brand separation between the technology platform (Telia'z) and the clinical service delivery.

## Consequences

Marketing materials, patient-facing communications, and the Leon practice website integration must use the HealthyMind brand. Brand consistency needs to be maintained across Track 1 (Leon), Track 2 (own CQC), and any future OMG/GP Confed pathways. Google Ads campaigns must be set up under the HealthyMind identity.
