# Steering Committee for Strategic Governance

## Context

Rami expressed frustration that clinical perspectives are not sufficiently integrated into company decision-making. The group recognized the need for a formal governance mechanism beyond informal consultation.

## Decision

Establish a steering committee with:
- CMO (Rami) as permanent member with clinical veto authority
- CEO (Oren) as chair
- COO (TBD) for operational perspective
- Regular meeting cadence for strategic decisions

The committee reviews and approves decisions with clinical implications before implementation.

## Rationale

Addresses the gap between clinical expertise and company decision-making. Provides a structured mechanism rather than relying on ad-hoc inclusion. Formalizes the clinical veto that was agreed upon in principle.

## Consequences

- Need to define committee charter, meeting frequency, and decision scope
- Must clarify which decisions require committee approval vs. informational only
- Board of Directors (Oren, Dekkel, Ilan Rosenfeld) remains highest authority; steering committee is operational governance
