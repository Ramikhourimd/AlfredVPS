# Limit Paper to Four or Five Figures Maximum

## Decision

The first research paper will contain a maximum of 4-5 figures to maintain focus and conciseness.

## Rationale

Dekkel set this limit to prevent scope creep. With 3,100+ patients and multiple possible analysis dimensions, the risk of the paper becoming unfocused was high. By limiting figures, the team is forced to prioritize the most impactful visualizations: likely a diagnosis distribution chart, demographic summary table, and time-to-treatment correlation plots.
