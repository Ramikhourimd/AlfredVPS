# Basel Takes Immediate Operational Ownership Upon Joining

## Context

Basel Kanaaneh joined Telia'z as Operations Manager / General Manager, Clinic. Rather than a gradual onboarding period, he immediately began asserting operational authority — a deliberate choice that was noted positively by both Rami and Oren during their December 2, 2025 management discussion.

## Options Considered

1. **Gradual onboarding** — observe existing processes, learn relationships, ease into authority over weeks/months
2. **Immediate operational ownership** — take charge from day one, identify problems, begin restructuring immediately

## Decision

Basel chose immediate operational ownership. From his first days, he began:
- Taking ownership of meetings previously run by Rami
- Identifying operational disorder and acting on it immediately
- Clarifying role divisions between existing staff (Rana and Shira)
- Requesting and centralizing agreements, protocols, and organizational documents
- After a meeting with Anan (children's services), declaring the need for fundamental restructuring in that area

## Rationale

The clinic's operational complexity had outgrown its informal management structure. Both Rami and Oren observed Basel's immediate impact positively — Oren noting his willingness to take ownership, Rami appreciating someone else driving operational structure. The urgency of the restructuring timeline did not allow for a slow ramp-up.

## Consequences

- Demonstrated Basel's operational capability early, building confidence in his appointment ([[assumption/Basel Can Lead Operations Despite Being a Recent Hire]])
- Created friction with Li Yamin when requesting document handovers — revealing her obstruction pattern
- Freed Rami from operational meetings he had been running by default
- Validated the decision to position Basel as the integration point above the three clinic pillars
- Triggered the need for a joint alignment meeting between Oren, Rami, and Basel ([[task/Arrange Joint Meeting Oren Rami and Basel]])
