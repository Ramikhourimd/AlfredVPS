# Route External Service Communication Through Tal

## Context

Li Yamin has been the primary contact for external service providers (lawyers, accountants, back-office). Friction arose because these providers repeatedly failed to file documents properly, requiring Li to re-send materials. Li also entered conflicts when chasing providers.

## Options Considered

1. **Keep Li as sole external contact** — status quo; causing friction
2. **Route external service communication through Tal** — chosen; reduces Li's friction points

## Decision

External service provider communication (lawyers, accountants, compliance) will now be routed through Tal. This removes a major source of friction from Li's role.

## Rationale

Separating the external communication channel from Li reduces conflict points and allows her to focus on core HR functions. Tal was recently brought on and can handle this interface.

## Consequences

Li's role scope narrows. Combined with the agreement handoff to Basel, Li's remaining responsibilities need to be clearly defined.
