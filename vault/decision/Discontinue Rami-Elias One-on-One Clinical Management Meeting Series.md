# Discontinue Rami-Elias One-on-One Clinical Management Meeting Series

## Context

Rami Khouri maintained a recurring one-on-one clinical management meeting series with Elias Jahjah, classified internally as "HM-RAMI-ELIAS." This series served as a direct channel for clinical operations coordination, including onboarding design, staff management, and clinical protocol discussions.

## Options Considered

1. **Continue the meeting series** — Maintain the recurring 1:1 cadence between Rami and Elias
2. **Discontinue the series** — End the recurring meetings and absorb relevant topics into other forums

## Decision

The HM-RAMI-ELIAS meeting series was discontinued as of February 2026. The February 12, 2026 session on onboarding structure appears to have been one of the final meetings in this series.

## Rationale

This decision aligns with the broader pattern of reducing Rami's direct operational involvement in clinic day-to-day management. As clinical and operational leadership chains are being formally separated (with Basel handling operations and a Medical Director handling clinical supervision), Rami's direct 1:1 clinical management meetings become redundant with the new reporting structure.

## Consequences

- Clinical management topics that were discussed in this forum must now flow through the new leadership structure (Medical Director for clinical, Basel/Shira for operational)
- Elias's clinical management coordination shifts to whoever fills the Medical Director role or to the restructured team meetings
- Reduces Rami's meeting load, consistent with the decision to redistribute his operational responsibilities
