# Brand UK Clinical Service as HealthyMind

## Context

Telia'z needed a patient-facing brand for the UK market. The parent company name "Telia'z" (rooted in the Hebrew name Taliaz) has no brand recognition in the UK and may be difficult for UK patients and GPs to pronounce or remember. A separate trading name was needed for the clinical service.

## Options Considered

1. **Use Telia'z brand directly** — simpler, single brand, but no UK recognition and potentially confusing name
2. **Create a UK-specific trading name (HealthyMind)** — separate patient-facing brand while Telia'z remains the corporate entity

## Decision

The UK clinical service operates under the trading name "HealthyMind." Telia'z remains the corporate/technology entity behind the service, but all patient-facing materials, GP communications, and marketing use the HealthyMind brand.

## Rationale

HealthyMind is a clear, English-language brand that immediately communicates the service domain (mental health). It avoids any pronunciation confusion and positions the service as a UK-native offering rather than an Israeli import. The demo rehearsal notes explicitly reference "Telia'z (trading as Healthy Mind for the clinical service)."

## Consequences

- All UK marketing, website, and patient materials use HealthyMind branding
- GP Confed and partner communications reference HealthyMind as the clinical service
- Regulatory filings (CQC) may need to reference both Telia'z and HealthyMind
- Creates a brand asset that could be used in other English-speaking markets
