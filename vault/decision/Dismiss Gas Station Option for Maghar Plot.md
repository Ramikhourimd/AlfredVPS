# Dismiss Gas Station Option for Maghar Plot

## Context

During the comprehensive feasibility analysis of the Maghar family land plot (Block 2054, Plot 32), a gas station was evaluated as a potential commercial use given the road frontage on the Maghar-Gilon regional road.

## Options Considered

1. **Gas station** — High-visibility roadside location, known revenue model
2. **Alternative commercial uses** — Parking, food trucks, EV charging, storage, etc.

## Decision

Gas station development was explicitly analyzed and dismissed as not viable for this plot.

## Rationale

Three disqualifying factors:
- **Zoning incompatibility**: Mixed urban zoning (ironi mesholav) does not automatically permit a gas station — would require a specific zoning change
- **Prohibitive investment**: Requires millions of NIS in capital investment, far exceeding other options
- **Traffic requirements**: Needs high traffic volume that may not exist on this regional road
- Not viable in any timeframe (short, medium, or long term)

## Consequences

- Eliminates gas station from future consideration for this plot unless zoning and traffic conditions fundamentally change
- Reinforces focus on lower-investment, phased development options (parking, storage, billboard, food trucks, solar)
