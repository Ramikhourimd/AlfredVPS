## Decision

[[person/Rami Khouri]] will seek a personal organizational assistant specifically designed to support his ADHD management needs, functioning as an external executive function scaffold.

## Context

Rami openly acknowledged that his ADHD significantly impacts his ability to maintain organizational structure, track commitments, and manage priorities. The discussion introduced the concept of an ADHD buddy: a dedicated support person (or potentially AI-assisted tool) who provides the external structure that ADHD individuals often lack internally.

## Rationale

- Rami holds multiple critical roles (CMO, innovation lead) requiring high organizational demand
- ADHD creates systematic challenges with task tracking, follow-through, and priority management
- Traditional secretary support does not address the cognitive support needs
- An ADHD buddy model provides externalized executive function: scheduling, reminders, accountability, priority enforcement
- This is an operational productivity investment, not a personal accommodation

## Proposed Role Characteristics

- Not a traditional secretary or administrative assistant
- Focused on cognitive support: task tracking, priority enforcement, accountability
- Helps maintain schedules, follow up on commitments, provide structured daily planning
- Could be a human assistant, AI-assisted tool, or hybrid approach
- Must understand ADHD-specific needs (gentle persistence, non-judgmental follow-up, structured routines)

## Feasibility Discussion

- Human assistant: most effective but highest cost, requires finding someone with ADHD coaching awareness
- AI-assisted: tools like task managers with AI reminders could partially address the need
- Hybrid: human assistant augmented by AI scheduling and tracking tools
- Team agreed this is a legitimate and high-ROI operational need

## Next Steps

- Define the specific functions and scope of the assistant role
- Evaluate human vs AI vs hybrid approaches
- If human: begin candidate search with ADHD coaching experience as a plus
- If AI: evaluate available tools and workflows
