## Decision

HR, marketing, finance, and technology support functions will remain centralized at the Telia'z parent company level, serving all clinic districts rather than being duplicated at each location.

## Rationale

- Cost efficiency — avoids duplicating support functions across districts
- Consistency — ensures uniform processes and standards
- Scale advantage — shared services can serve multiple clinics as the company grows
- Current team members (Adi for marketing, Roei for tech support, Tal for HR) already operate at the company level

## Implications

- Districts must coordinate with parent company for support services
- Service level agreements may be needed between parent and districts
- Potential friction point: response time and priority allocation across multiple districts
