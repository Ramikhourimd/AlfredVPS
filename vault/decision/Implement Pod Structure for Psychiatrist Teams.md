## Decision

Organize psychiatrists into pods of approximately 10 practitioners, each led by a senior clinician (pod leader). This structure enables scalable clinical management without requiring a single person to oversee all practitioners.

## Rationale

- A single manager cannot effectively supervise 30+ psychiatrists
- Pod leaders provide clinical mentoring and quality oversight at a manageable ratio
- Enables growth — when a pod reaches capacity, spin off a new pod
- Pod leaders report to the Medical Director / Clinical Director

## Implications

- Need to identify and develop pod leader candidates from existing staff
- Pod leaders need defined responsibilities and compensation for the leadership role
- Inter-pod coordination mechanisms needed (shared protocols, regular pod leader meetings)
