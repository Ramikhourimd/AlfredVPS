# In-Person Holiday Visit Planned for Continued Genealogy Research

## Context

During the 2025-12-02 voice memo conversation, Majdi Shaddad shared extensive oral history about the Shaddad/Khouri family. At the conclusion of the discussion, Majdi invited Rami to visit in person during the upcoming holiday period to continue the genealogy research. This invitation appears in three separate conversation records from the same session.

## Options Considered

1. **Continue via remote communication** — Phone calls, voice memos, or written exchanges
2. **In-person visit during holiday** — Face-to-face session at Majdi's location, allowing access to any physical documents or family tree materials he has compiled

## Decision

Majdi invited Rami to visit during the holiday period for a deeper, in-person genealogy research session. Rami's follow-up is pending.

## Rationale

An in-person visit would allow:
- Direct review of Majdi's ~45-year compiled family tree document
- Access to any physical records, photos, or documents Majdi has collected
- More detailed, iterative Q&A without voice memo limitations
- Potential introduction to other family members with genealogical knowledge

## Consequences

- Creates an opportunity to move beyond oral-only tradition (the primary constraint on this research)
- May yield access to Majdi's unpublished family tree document
- Could resolve open contradictions (e.g., Hauran vs Zahleh origin) through direct questioning
- Requires Rami to schedule travel during the holiday period
