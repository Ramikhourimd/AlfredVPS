# Separate Operational Plan from Restructuring Discussion

## Context

During the 2026-02-22 meeting, the team recognised that the clinic operational plan and the broader organisational restructuring are distinct topics that should be presented and discussed separately.

## Options Considered

1. **Combined presentation** — Present operational plan as part of the restructuring discussion
2. **Separate presentations** — Basel presents operational plan independently from restructuring

## Decision

Basel will present the clinic operational plan as a standalone presentation, separate from the broader restructuring discussion.

## Rationale

Separating the presentations allows focused discussion on operational specifics (KPIs, staffing, timeline) without conflating them with higher-level restructuring decisions.

## Consequences

- Basel must prepare a written operational plan document with KPIs, staffing plan, and timeline
- Leadership team will review the operational plan on its own merits
- Restructuring decisions can proceed on a separate track
