# Investment Property With Clinic Potential at 850K NIS Under Evaluation

## Context

On 2025-10-09, alongside rental viewings at the family-owned building, the family evaluated a separate property as a potential purchase/investment. Unlike the mini penthouse (rejected for kitchen ventilation) and the Asfiya property (rejected outright), this property was not dismissed and remains under consideration.

## Options Considered

1. **Pursue this property** — Acquire at ~850K NIS, invest in renovation (electrical, kitchen improvements), convert ground floor to home clinic
2. **Continue rental search** — Focus on the family building or similar rental options at 1,200-1,500 NIS/month
3. **Defer and keep looking** — Neither commit nor reject; continue evaluating both rental and purchase tracks

## Decision

No final decision reached. The property remains under active family evaluation — neither rejected nor committed to.

## Rationale

**Advantages discussed:**
- Ground floor or lower level could serve as a clinic, enabling home-based psychiatric practice (including night sessions)
- Large property (2,500 referenced — possibly sqm of land or a valuation metric)
- Lower level described as having "high potential" for multiple uses
- Veranda/balcony convertible to garden
- Kitchen improvement opportunities

**Concerns raised:**
- Location described as "a bit far" from preferred area
- Requires significant renovation — electrical cables, other improvements
- Acquisition cost ~850K NIS plus substantial renovation budget needed
- One family member noted current space "is not enough" for growing children

## Consequences

If pursued, this would shift the housing search from rental to purchase track, requiring:
- Renovation capital beyond the 850K purchase price ([[constraint/Affordable Purchase Properties Require Renovation Capital Beyond Purchase Price]])
- Resolution of the rent-vs-buy dilemma ([[contradiction/Rent vs Buy Path Unresolved]])
- Timeline management — renovation could take months before move-in

This is the only purchase property not yet rejected, making it the sole live option on the buy-side of the search.
