# Rami Receives On-Call Budget Gap as Bonus

## Context

[[person/Oren Taliaz]] allocates 25,000 NIS monthly for on-call coverage at [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]]. [[person/Rami Khouri]] distributes portions to other on-call staff (Atef, Elinor). The question arose of what happens to the undistributed remainder.

## Decision

The gap — the amount remaining from the 25,000 NIS monthly on-call budget after all distributions to other staff — goes to [[person/Rami Khouri]] as a personal bonus, added to his payslip.

## Terms

- Oren explicitly confirmed: "We add the gap to you as a bonus."
- Rami initially considered using the gap for conferences or team development.
- Oren encouraged taking it as personal compensation, reasoning the arrangement only lasts through December 2025.
- If Rami later identifies a specific use case (e.g., conference funding), he can propose it to Oren.

## Rationale

The on-call budget was originally intended to replace pricing Rami's own on-call work separately (which could have reached 30,000 NIS). The gap arrangement effectively compensates Rami for his on-call contributions while keeping the budget structure simple.
