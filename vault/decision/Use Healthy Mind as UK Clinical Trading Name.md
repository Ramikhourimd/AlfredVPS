# Use Healthy Mind as UK Clinical Trading Name

## Context

Telia'z needed a patient-facing brand name for UK clinical operations. The parent company name "Telia'z" (an Israeli tech company) would not resonate with UK NHS patients seeking ADHD assessment. A localized clinical brand was needed.

## Options Considered

1. **Telia'z** — Use the corporate name directly
2. **Healthy Mind** — A UK-friendly clinical brand for the patient-facing service

## Decision

Telia'z will trade as "Healthy Mind" for the UK clinical service. The Telia'z name remains for B2B partnerships (e.g., with GP Confederation, OMG Healthcare) while Healthy Mind is the patient-facing brand.

## Rationale

A localized brand name better serves patient trust and UK market positioning. "Healthy Mind" communicates the mental health service offering clearly to English-speaking patients. This separation also insulates the clinical brand from the parent company's Israeli identity, which could be a barrier in some UK patient segments.

## Consequences

- Marketing materials, landing pages, and patient communications must use "Healthy Mind" branding
- Leon's practice website integration must reference Healthy Mind, not Telia'z
- Google Ads campaigns for UK must be under Healthy Mind brand
- Dual branding creates complexity in partnership contracts (Telia'z entity, Healthy Mind trading name)
