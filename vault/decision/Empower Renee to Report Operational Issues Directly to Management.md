## Decision

Empower [[person/Rana Khouri]] (Renee) to report operational issues directly to the incoming clinic manager, creating a dedicated channel for surfacing day-to-day operational problems.

## Context

Currently, operational issues from the secretarial team are routed through [[person/Shira]], who is overloaded with clinical operations management. This creates a bottleneck where many operational problems go unreported or are significantly delayed. Renee, as the most experienced secretary with strong operational awareness, is well-positioned to identify and escalate issues that affect daily clinic operations.

## Rationale

- Shira is overloaded with clinical management responsibilities and cannot adequately handle operational issue reporting
- Renee has direct visibility into day-to-day operational challenges
- Creating a direct reporting line reduces information loss and response time
- The incoming manager needs an operational information source independent of the clinical pipeline

## Impact

- Renee gains a formal operational reporting responsibility
- Shira is relieved of non-clinical operational issue intake
- The incoming manager receives direct operational intelligence
- May require adjusting Renee's workload to accommodate reporting duties

## Dependencies

- Requires incoming clinic manager to be in place
- Renee needs clarity on what constitutes an operational issue vs clinical issue
- Reporting format and cadence should be defined
