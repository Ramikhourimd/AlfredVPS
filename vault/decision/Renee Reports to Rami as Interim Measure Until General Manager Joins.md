# Renee Reports to Rami as Interim Measure Until General Manager Joins

## Context

With Basel Kanaaneh not yet onboarded and no general manager in place, a temporary reporting structure was needed for clinic administrative staff. Renee (Rana Khouri) had begun formal handover processes with Li Yamin and Shira but needed a reporting line.

## Options Considered

1. **Renee reports to Oren** — CFO-level oversight, but Oren lacks operational clinic context
2. **Renee reports to Rami** — Rami has deepest clinic operational knowledge and is already coordinating staff
3. **Renee operates independently** — No oversight during transition period

## Decision

Renee reports temporarily to Rami until the new general manager (Basel) is fully onboarded and established.

## Rationale

Rami is the only person with sufficient clinic operational context to oversee Renee's clinical staff management responsibilities. This was explicitly framed as temporary — "until the new general manager arrives."

## Consequences

- Adds to Rami's already unsustainable workload (reinforces the 4-role overload problem)
- Creates a reporting line that must be formally transferred to Basel
- No defined sunset date or transition criteria were established
