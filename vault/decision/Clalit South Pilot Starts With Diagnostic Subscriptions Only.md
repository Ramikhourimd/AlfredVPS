# Clalit South Pilot Starts With Diagnostic Subscriptions Only

## Decision

The pilot phase of the Telia'z–Clalit South psychiatric referral system will begin exclusively with diagnostic subscriptions, deferring treatment subscriptions to a later phase.

## Context

Discussed and agreed upon during the [[conversation/Clalit South Referral System Setup Meeting 2025-11-10]].

## Rationale

- **Diagnostic subscriptions** do not require manual Form 17 approval from the district contact — the form is auto-generated by the Clalit system
- **Treatment subscriptions** require manual Form 17 approval from Tzachi or [[person/Michal Boguz]], creating a bottleneck
- Starting with the simpler pathway allows faster scaling and validates the operational workflow before adding complexity
- The constraint [[constraint/Clalit Form 17 Requires Manual Approval by District Contact]] is bypassed entirely for diagnostic subscriptions

## Impact

- Faster pilot launch (target: November 20, 2025)
- Lower operational friction during initial rollout
- Treatment subscriptions to be added once the Form 17 approval process is streamlined
