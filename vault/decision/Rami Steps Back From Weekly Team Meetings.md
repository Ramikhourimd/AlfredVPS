# Rami Steps Back From Weekly Team Meetings

## Context

As part of [[person/Rami Khouri]]'s transition toward a Global Medical Director role, he decided to reduce his involvement in weekly operational team meetings at [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]].

## Decision

Starting the week after 2025-09-01:
- Rami will **not** attend the weekly team meeting
- His AI note-taker will attend and record proceedings
- Rami **will** attend the last meeting of each month for a monthly review and closure
- [[person/Shira]] will manage the meeting agenda and lead the weekly sessions

## Rationale

Rami wants to empower Shira and Elinor to run operations independently, with Rami maintaining oversight through monthly check-ins and the weekly Rami-Shira 1:1 meeting.
