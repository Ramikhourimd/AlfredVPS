# Recruit UK Case Managers Locally Not From Israel

## Context

Dekkel asked whether case managers should be recruited in Israel (for UK English-language work) or locally in the UK.

## Options Considered

1. **Recruit in Israel** — Easier coordination, existing training infrastructure
2. **Recruit locally in UK** — Better for the UK clinic long-term, culturally appropriate, no timezone issues

## Decision

Recruit UK case managers locally in England. Adiel stated this is "the right thing for the company and for the clinic there."

## Rationale

Local recruitment ensures cultural fit, timezone alignment, and operational independence for the UK clinic. The Israeli stopgap is temporary only to avoid delaying launch while local recruitment proceeds.

## Consequences

- Need to establish UK recruitment pipeline for case managers
- UK HR/employment framework needed
- Stopgap Israeli coverage (Basem) bridges the gap
