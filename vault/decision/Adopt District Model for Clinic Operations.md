## Decision

Treat each clinic as a "district" under the Telia'z parent company, with its own management triad: Clinical Director, Operations Manager, and Administrative Manager.

## Rationale

- Enables scalable expansion — each new clinic follows the same template
- Separates local clinical and operational concerns from company-wide strategy
- Allows shared services (HR, marketing, finance, tech) to remain centralized at parent level
- Creates clear accountability within each clinic unit

## Implications

- Basel serves as Operations Manager for the Israel clinic district
- A Medical Director (30% position) needs to be hired for the clinic
- Renee or equivalent serves as Administrative Manager per district
- Parent company provides shared services to all districts

## Status

Conceptual agreement reached; formal organizational chart not yet finalized.
