# Position as Service Provider First Then Layer Technology

## Context
Telia'z could enter the UK market positioning as either a technology vendor or a clinical service provider. Jason and Rebecca advised on the approach.

## Options Considered
1. **Option A — Service-first** — Enter as an ADHD clinical service, then layer in technology as an additional income stream and efficiency lever
2. **Option B — Technology-first** — Lead with the AI platform and find clinical partners to use it

## Decision
Go service-first. Enter via ADHD clinical service delivery in partnership with GP Confed, then layer technology as additional value and potential separate revenue stream.

## Rationale
Rebecca: "If you put in the door with the service, then bring in the tech and charge for that." Technology alone triggers procurement complexity; a service partnership has faster path to patients and income. Massive NHS funding pots exist for AI/innovation that can be tapped separately once the service relationship is established.

## Consequences
- Initial revenue comes from clinical service delivery, not software licensing
- Technology is a competitive advantage, not the primary product
- Opens path to separate technology grants and funding from NHS England
