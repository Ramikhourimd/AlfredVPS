# Rivi Logs Overtime Clinical Hours Via Email to Basel

## Context
Rivi worked 1.5 extra hours in February 2026 beyond her contracted 20 clinical hours but was not compensated. Basel's automated payroll cannot detect overtime automatically.

## Options Considered
1. **Build automated detection** — System identifies hours beyond threshold
2. **Manual email logging** — Rivi reports extra hours to Basel with Rami's approval

## Decision
Manual email logging approach: Rivi emails Basel with extra hours beyond 20, Rami approves as overtime. February to be retroactively compensated.

## Rationale
Quick solution while automated detection is not available. Places minimal burden on Rivi while ensuring compensation.

## Consequences
- February 2026 overtime will be retroactively paid
- Going forward, any clinical hours beyond 20 must be explicitly logged
