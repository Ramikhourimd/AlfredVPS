# Telem Family Brochures Must Use Maccabi Branding Not Military Branding

## Context

The team was designing brochures for families of wounded soldiers eligible for psychiatric services through Telem (IDF Rehabilitation Division). A branding decision was needed: should brochures carry the Telem (military) brand or the Maccabi (healthcare) brand?

## Options Considered

1. **Option A** — Telem-branded brochures emphasizing the military rehabilitation pathway
2. **Option B** — Maccabi-branded brochures emphasizing the healthcare service pathway

## Decision

Brochures must be Maccabi-branded, not Telem-branded. This was a deliberate design choice.

## Rationale

Families should understand the psychiatric service as a healthcare service delivered through their HMO, not as a military program. Maccabi branding normalizes the service and reduces stigma associated with military rehabilitation channels. The brochure represents the care pathway, not the referral source.

## Consequences

- Requires Maccabi brand assets and compliance with Maccabi branding guidelines
- Reinforces the Telia'z-Maccabi partnership identity
- Each distribution point receives unique tracking URL/QR code for referral attribution
- Distributed through Telem channels to eligible families and at Maccabi clinics in key cities
