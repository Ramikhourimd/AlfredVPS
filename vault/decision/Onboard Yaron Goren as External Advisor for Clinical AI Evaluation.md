# Onboard Yaron Goren as External Advisor for Clinical AI Evaluation

## Context

The AI model evaluation workstream needed external expertise to ensure methodological rigor in comparing AI-generated psychiatric summaries across models (O1, O1 Med, Gemini 2.0 Flash). The internal team had built the evaluation spreadsheet and rubric framework but lacked independent clinical evaluation methodology expertise.

## Options Considered

1. **Rely on internal team only** — Rami and Ahmad handle evaluation methodology without external input
2. **Hire external clinical AI advisor** — Bring in domain expert for methodology guidance

## Decision

Onboard Yaron Goren as an external advisor specifically for the AI model evaluation methodology. His first sprint meeting was January 16, 2025.

## Rationale

Yaron immediately contributed value in the first meeting by raising the pairwise comparison methodology point — noting that head-to-head model comparison may capture quality differences lost in standalone scoring. This validated the decision to bring external perspective to the evaluation design. External advisors bring methodological rigor that internal teams focused on building may overlook.

## Consequences

- Yaron's pairwise comparison suggestion influenced the evaluation methodology design
- Added an external review layer to the AI evaluation framework
- Created dependency on external advisor availability for methodology decisions
