# Demo Clinical Platform to Leon Before Operational Launch

## Context

Leon is a family doctor lending his CQC registration for Telia'z to operate under. He needs to understand and be comfortable with the clinical system his patients will flow through — admin views, psychiatrist views, and patient pathways.

## Options Considered

1. **Full platform demo before contract** — Show Leon everything before he signs
2. **Demo after contract signing** — Sign first, then onboard Leon on the system
3. **No formal demo** — Rely on documentation and SOPs only

## Decision

Stav is preparing a dedicated demo of the admin and psychiatrist views for Leon. This was triggered by Leon expressing interest in seeing the system after Yael showed him the initial patient flow design on Thursday Feb 13.

## Rationale

Leon's confidence in the system is essential — he is lending his CQC registration and taking regulatory responsibility. A hands-on demo reduces risk of post-signing friction and ensures Leon understands what patients will experience under his practice name.

## Consequences

- Adds a demo preparation task to Stav's workload during a critical sprint
- Creates opportunity for Leon to raise clinical concerns before launch rather than after
- May surface UK-specific UX requirements not yet identified
