# Amani Rohana Selected as Primary PA Candidate

## Context

After deciding that a personal organizational assistant is needed to support Rami's ADHD management and operational continuity, the next step was identifying a candidate. During a November 9, 2025 discussion with a close family advisor, the advisor strongly endorsed [[person/Amani Rohana]] as the primary candidate, stating "You need her." The advisor had already informally mentioned the idea to Amani but no formal conversation had occurred.

## Options Considered

1. **Amani Rohana** — Known to Rami's circle, strong organizational ability, currently on extended maternity leave and unlikely to return to previous employer. Advisor endorses strongly.
2. **Open candidate search** — Broader search for someone with ADHD coaching experience. Higher uncertainty, longer timeline.
3. **AI-assisted / hybrid approach** — Technology-based executive function support. Lower cost but less contextual awareness.

## Decision

Pursue Amani Rohana as the primary candidate for the personal organizational assistant role. A formal discussion with Amani about the role and expectations is the agreed next step.

## Rationale

- Amani is known to have strong organizational ability and is detail-oriented
- She is familiar with Rami's working context
- She is likely available due to extended maternity leave from previous employer
- The family advisor — a trusted source — provides strong endorsement
- Market salary (10,000-13,000 NIS gross) fits within the financial framework being developed

## Consequences

- Rami must complete his own role split definition before approaching Amani (see constraint)
- A formal expectations-alignment conversation is required before any commitment
- The advisor recommends a 1-2 week trial period to validate the working arrangement
- If Amani declines or the trial fails, the search reverts to open candidate pool
