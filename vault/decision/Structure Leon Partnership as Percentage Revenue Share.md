# Structure Leon Partnership as Percentage Revenue Share

## Context

Telia'z needs to operate under Leon's CQC registration for the UK launch. The commercial relationship needed definition — how does Leon benefit from providing regulatory umbrella.

## Options Considered

1. **Percentage revenue share** — Leon receives a percentage of patient revenue through his practice
2. **Fixed monthly fee** — Flat payment for CQC umbrella access
3. **Per-patient fee** — Fixed amount per patient processed

## Decision

Structure the agreement as a percentage arrangement — Leon receives a percentage of revenue from patients flowing through his practice website into the Telia'z assessment pathway.

## Rationale

A percentage model aligns incentives: Leon benefits from patient volume growth, reducing risk of disputes. Telia'z has minimal fixed costs before patient volume is confirmed. Leon reviewed the contract over the weekend of Feb 15, 2026.

## Consequences

Leon's revenue scales with Telia'z success. However, margins decrease as volume grows (unlike a fixed fee). Variable cost structure means long-term profitability depends on negotiated percentage terms.
