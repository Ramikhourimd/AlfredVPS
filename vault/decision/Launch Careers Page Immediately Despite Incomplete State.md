# Launch Careers Page Immediately Despite Incomplete State

## Context

The Telia'z careers page had been in development with content for adult psychiatrists, child psychiatrists, and case managers. Final form testing required Roy Shur's involvement, causing delays. Meanwhile, recruitment marketing campaigns (Google Ads for both Israel and UK markets) were blocked waiting for the page to go live.

## Options Considered

1. **Wait for completion** — Complete all form testing, finalize all role category content, and launch only when fully polished
2. **Launch immediately** — Publish the page as-is to unblock recruitment campaigns, iterate afterward

## Decision

Launch the careers page immediately, even in an imperfect state. Dekkel explicitly pushed for this during the 2026-02-15 team meeting, overriding the tendency to delay for perfection.

## Rationale

The careers page is a hard prerequisite for recruitment marketing campaigns. Every day the page is not live, recruitment campaigns cannot run. Given the clinic's severe capacity constraints (945 patients/month vs 77 intake slots), recruitment delays compound the operational crisis. An imperfect live page is infinitely better than a perfect unpublished page. Israel campaigns can launch immediately once live; UK Google campaigns require separate approval (slower).

## Consequences

- Israel recruitment campaigns can launch immediately once the page is live
- UK Google campaigns still require separate approval process
- Page can be iteratively improved after launch
- Risk of poor first impression from incomplete content accepted as lower priority than recruitment velocity
