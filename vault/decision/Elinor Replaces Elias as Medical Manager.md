# Elinor Replaces Elias as Medical Manager

## Context

Elias was previously handling the medical management role at [[org/Telia'z]] Clinic Israel, including Clickx system management and clinical oversight. The transition had been planned and Rami sent an email to the entire team announcing the change.

## Decision

Elinor officially takes over from Elias as medical manager at [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]], effective 2025-09-01. Her role includes:

- **Clinical management**: Overseeing clinical operations, quality, and protocols
- **Medical staff management**: Managing psychiatrists, gradually taking over hiring decisions
- **Clickx system ownership**: Point of contact for all Clickx-related matters (access, summaries, compliance)
- **Psychiatrist coordination**: Working with Shira to jointly manage the clinical operation as a unit

## Terms

- 60 total hours: 20 clinical hours + 40 management hours
- Approximately one-third of a full-time position in Israel
- Gradual transition with Rami maintaining oversight initially

## Rationale

[[person/Rami Khouri]] aims to transition to a Global Medical Director role by January 2026, requiring local clinical leadership. Elinor and [[person/Shira]] will jointly manage the Israel clinical operation, analogous to a department head and head nurse dynamic.
