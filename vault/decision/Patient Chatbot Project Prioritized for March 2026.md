# Patient Chatbot Project Prioritized for March 2026

## Context

During the Omi AI device onboarding on 2026-03-18, Rami confirmed his monthly goal for March 2026 is to start working on the patient chatbot and patient app project. This aligns with the broader patient-facing technology roadmap.

## Options Considered

1. **Continue focus on internal clinical tools** — AI summaries, prompt engineering, staff-facing systems
2. **Shift to patient-facing technology** — chatbot, patient app, direct patient engagement tools

## Decision

Patient chatbot and patient app development is the primary innovation focus for March 2026, marking a strategic pivot toward patient-facing technology alongside ongoing internal clinical AI work.

## Rationale

The innovation program has matured its internal clinical AI tools (summaries, prompts, evaluation frameworks). The next logical frontier is patient-facing technology that can improve patient engagement, reduce intake dropout, and provide continuous support between sessions.

## Consequences

- Development resources shift toward patient-facing prototyping
- Requires patient privacy architecture alignment (existing stratified privacy model)
- Creates new evaluation needs for patient-facing AI quality
