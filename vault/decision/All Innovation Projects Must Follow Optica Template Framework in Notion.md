# All Innovation Projects Must Follow Optica Template Framework in Notion

## Context

Rami demonstrated the Optica innovation project template workflow in Notion during the 2026-03-17 meeting. Templates exist in two versions (long and short). The team needed a standardized way to document and track innovation projects through pipeline stages.

## Options Considered

1. **Ad-hoc documentation** — each team member documents projects in their own format
2. **Standardized Optica template** — all projects tracked through the same template framework in Notion

## Decision

All innovation projects must follow the Optica template framework in Notion. Team members duplicate the template and fill in responses per chapter. Projects track through Optica pipeline stages. Rivi, Ohad, and Stav should schedule a joint session to align on template usage.

## Rationale

Standardization ensures consistent documentation quality, enables gate reviews with business and marketing stakeholders, and provides a common language for project status across the innovation team.

## Consequences

- Every new project requires Optica template setup before active development
- Rivi's complex patient project specifically must complete chapters 1 and 2 of the full template
- Joint alignment session needed between Rivi, Ohad, and Stav
- Creates overhead but ensures no project advances without proper documentation
