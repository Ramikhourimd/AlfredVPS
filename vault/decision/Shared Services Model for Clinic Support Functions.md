## Context

During the leadership meeting on 2026-02-22, Oren strongly advocated for Telia'z to operate as a single unified company rather than as separate divisions with independent support structures. This was a key point of discussion as the team considered how to structure the relationship between the clinic operations and innovation/technology functions.

## Shared Services Scope

The following functions would operate as shared services across the company:

| Function | Current Owner | Shared Service Model |
|----------|--------------|---------------------|
| HR & Recruitment | Tal | Company-wide, serves both clinic and innovation |
| Finance & Billing | Shira | Company-wide financial operations |
| Marketing | Adi | Single brand strategy across all activities |
| Legal & Compliance | External + Internal | Unified legal framework |
| IT Infrastructure | Shachar (CTO) | Shared technology platform |
| Office Management | TBD | Central administrative support |

## Functions Remaining Within Clinic

- Clinical operations management (Basel)
- Medical secretaries (patient-facing and staff-facing)
- Practitioner management and scheduling
- Medical quality oversight (Medical Director)

## Key Arguments

### For Shared Services
- Avoids duplication of overhead across divisions
- Maintains single company identity and culture
- More efficient resource allocation at current company size (~50 employees)
- Enables consistent policies and standards

### Considerations
- Need clear service-level agreements between shared services and business units
- Risk of shared services not prioritizing one unit's needs over another
- As the company grows, some functions may need to be embedded within divisions

## Status

This model was broadly supported during the meeting but requires formal organizational chart documentation and role definitions to implement.
