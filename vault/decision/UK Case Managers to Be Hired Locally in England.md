# UK Case Managers to Be Hired Locally in England

## Decision

Case managers for the UK clinic will be hired locally in England rather than staffed permanently from the Israel team.

## Context

During the 2026-02-15 team meeting, Dekkel explicitly stated that UK case managers should be English locals, not Israeli staff working remotely. Bassem's role as interim case manager is temporary only.

## Rationale

- Local staff understand UK healthcare culture and NHS expectations
- In-person patient support improves care quality
- Time zone alignment with UK patients
- Remote case management from Israel is not sustainable for ongoing operations
