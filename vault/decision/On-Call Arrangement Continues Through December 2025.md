# On-Call Arrangement Continues Through December 2025

## Context

The on-call compensation structure at [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]] needed formal confirmation of its duration and relationship to [[person/Rami Khouri]]'s existing employment agreement.

## Decision

The on-call arrangement (25,000 NIS monthly budget managed by Rami) continues from August 2025 through December 2025. The existing December employment agreement remains unchanged.

## Terms

- The existing employment agreement signed in December (previous year) is **not modified**.
- No new employment agreement is needed — the on-call arrangement was the only reason a new agreement had been discussed.
- [[person/Oren Taliaz]] will arrange a short addendum or separate legal document to formalize the on-call compensation arrangement.
- Oren's legal counsel will determine the format (addendum vs. standalone document).
- The arrangement is explicitly described as temporary and point-in-time ("nkudati").

## Rationale

Both parties agreed that the scope of changes (on-call compensation only) did not warrant reopening the entire employment agreement. A lightweight addendum provides legal backing without the overhead of full renegotiation.
