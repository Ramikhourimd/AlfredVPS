# Route UK Patients Through Leon Practice Website

## Context

With the decision to operate under Leon's CQC registration, the team needed to define how UK patients would enter the Telia'z assessment flow — either through a Telia'z-branded UK website or through Leon's existing practice infrastructure.

## Options Considered

1. **Telia'z-branded UK website** — build a separate UK-facing site with Telia'z branding, routing patients into the assessment flow
2. **Leon's practice website** — embed the Telia'z patient flow within Leon's existing practice website, patients enter through his site

## Decision

Patients will flow through Leon's practice website into the Telia'z assessment flow. Li Yamin met Leon on Thursday (2026-02-13) and showed him the initial patient flow design for integration into his website.

## Rationale

Operating under Leon's CQC means patients are technically his practice's patients. Routing through his website maintains regulatory alignment and avoids the need for a separate Telia'z UK web presence at launch. Landing pages on his site will integrate the Telia'z patient flow.

## Consequences

- UK marketing campaigns (Google Ads) must drive traffic to Leon's website, not a Telia'z site
- Campaign preparation is dependent on Leon's website being ready with embedded patient flow
- Telia'z brand visibility is reduced at patient entry point
- Landing page design requires coordination between Telia'z marketing (Yael) and Leon's site
