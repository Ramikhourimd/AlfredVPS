# Noam Assigned as Paper Compilation Lead and Statistical Methodology Authority

## Context

The AI diagnostic research paper requires coordination between Rami (AI pipeline architect), Dekkel (senior clinical advisor), and a statistician who can compile, structure, and validate the paper. Across all 2026-02-22 meeting documentation, Noam consistently appears in this compilation and statistical authority role.

## Options Considered

1. **Rami compiles the paper** — Rami built the pipeline and understands the methodology deeply, but is already responsible for AI system architecture and data coordination with Shmulik.
2. **Noam compiles the paper** — Noam has statistical expertise and can integrate methodology with results into a coherent paper structure.

## Decision

Noam is the paper compilation lead and statistical methodology authority. Noam compiled all previously sent materials into a single document (method + analysis + results), sets statistical thresholds (e.g., 2.5 SD for outlier removal), flags data discrepancies (dataset count mismatch), determines figure requirements, and manages paper structure constraints (e.g., methods section length).

## Rationale

Noam's statistical expertise and paper compilation experience make her the natural authority for paper structure, analytical methodology, and quality control. Rami provides the AI pipeline methodology and system architecture; Dekkel provides clinical strategy and scope decisions; Noam integrates both into a publishable paper.

## Consequences

- All statistical methodology decisions (outlier thresholds, reporting metrics) flow through Noam
- Paper structure and figure count decisions require Noam's input
- Data quality issues (dataset discrepancies) are Noam's responsibility to investigate
- Rami must provide methodology materials to Noam in a format she can compile
