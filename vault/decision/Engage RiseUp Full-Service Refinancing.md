# Engage RiseUp Full-Service Refinancing

## Context

RiseUp offers two service tiers for mortgage refinancing advisory: partial service at 2,400 NIS and full service at 6,000 NIS. During the 2026-01-26 consultation, Rami indicated a preference for the full-service option.

## Options Considered

1. **Partial service (2,400 NIS)** — Limited advisory support through the refinancing process
2. **Full service (6,000 NIS)** — Comprehensive advisory and management of the refinancing process

## Decision

Rami is leaning toward the full-service engagement (6,000 NIS), but has not yet formally committed. The decision is pending — ball is in Rami's court.

## Rationale

- Given the complexity of the mortgage (four tracks, CPI indexation, dual employment) and the potential savings (~200,000 NIS), the full-service fee represents a small fraction of the expected benefit
- Rami prefers not to manage the process himself

## Consequences

- 6,000 NIS upfront cost for advisory services
- Full professional management of the refinancing process
- Rami needs to make formal contact with RiseUp to proceed
