# Clalit South Patients Can Continue Treatment Indefinitely

## Decision

Patients referred through the Clalit South District treatment subscription can continue psychiatric treatment indefinitely with no session or duration limits.

## Context

During the [[conversation/Clalit South Referral System Setup Meeting 2025-11-10]], Tzachi (Clalit South District representative) confirmed that once a patient is approved for a treatment subscription, there is no built-in cap on the number of sessions or overall treatment duration.

## Rationale

This policy is inherent to Clalit's treatment subscription model and represents a significant advantage over some other HMO referral arrangements that impose visit limits.

## Impact

- Enables long-term psychiatric care relationships
- Reduces administrative burden of renewal requests
- Supports better patient outcomes through continuity of care
- Important selling point for the [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel|Telia'z–Clalit partnership]]
