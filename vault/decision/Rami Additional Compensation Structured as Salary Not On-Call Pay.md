# Rami Additional Compensation Structured as Salary Not On-Call Pay

## Context

Rami and Dekkel discussed how to structure an additional payment for Rami. Two options were considered: on-call pay (כוננות) or a straight salary increase. Tax compliance and administrative simplicity were the key factors.

## Options Considered

1. **On-call pay (כוננות)** — Would provide a tax-advantaged structure but raises compliance concerns and adds payroll complexity
2. **Straight salary increase** — Simpler, fully transparent, but subject to full marginal tax rate

## Decision

Dekkel decided to add the compensation as a straight salary increase. His exact words: "Forget it, put it in the salary. I don't want to complicate things for you either."

## Rationale

- The on-call structure would not provide meaningful savings — "not even close to 2,000 NIS" difference
- Compliance risk outweighs the marginal tax benefit
- Administrative simplicity preferred over tax optimization
- Both agreed it "needs to make sense" from a compliance perspective

## Consequences

- Rami's salary increases directly, subject to full marginal tax rate
- No compliance risk from questionable on-call classification
- Sets precedent for straightforward compensation structures at Telia'z
