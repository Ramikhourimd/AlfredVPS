# Monthly Gate Reviews With Business and Marketing for Innovation Projects

## Context
Innovation projects need input from business (Adiel) and marketing (Adi) stakeholders before advancing from concept to prototype phase. Without this, innovation work risks being clinically sound but commercially misaligned.

## Options Considered
1. **Ad-hoc reviews** — Present to stakeholders only when ready
2. **Monthly standing meetings** — Regular cadence with business and marketing

## Decision
Monthly gate review meetings with Adi (marketing) and Adiel (business) for all innovation projects transitioning between phases. Each project must pass through a go/no-go gate before advancing.

## Rationale
Business and marketing perspectives can surface critical requirements early. Regular cadence ensures no project advances without commercial validation.

## Consequences
- Rivi must prepare questions for stakeholders in advance
- Gate 1 review for complex patient project to include Rami, Adi, and Adiel
