# Housing Search Focused on Haifa Metropolitan Area

## Context

Rami's family needs to find suitable housing for their growing family. The search could theoretically include any area in northern Israel, but all viewings and discussions center on the Haifa metropolitan area (Haifa proper, Krayot/Kiryat Haim, and surrounding neighborhoods).

## Options Considered

1. **Haifa metropolitan area** — Familiar territory, Rami previously lived in Kiryat Haim, existing community connections, proximity to work
2. **Maghar (family village)** — Family land available, strong community, but would require building from scratch and longer timeline
3. **Other northern cities** — Not discussed in any source records

## Decision

The active housing search is focused exclusively on the Haifa metropolitan area. While the Maghar land exists as a long-term option, no viewings or active searches were conducted outside Haifa/Krayot.

## Rationale

Haifa offers the combination of: (1) Arab community presence that the wife requires, (2) Rami's prior familiarity with areas like Kiryat Haim, (3) urban amenities and walkability, and (4) proximity to work. The Maghar option requires significant capital investment and construction time, making it unsuitable for the immediate housing need.

## Consequences

- Geographic search area is limited to Haifa, Kiryat Haim, and potentially mixed neighborhoods in the Krayot
- Maghar land remains a separate long-term consideration
- Coastal areas (Atlit, Neve Yam) were briefly mentioned but not actively pursued
