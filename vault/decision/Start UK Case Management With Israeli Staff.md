# Start UK Case Management With Israeli Staff

## Context
UK clinic needs case managers from day one, but hiring locally before patient volume is confirmed is premature.

## Options Considered
1. **Option A** — Use existing Israeli case manager (Bassem mentioned as capable, English-speaking) as temporary stopgap
2. **Option B** — Hire UK-based case manager immediately
3. **Option C** — Wait until patient volume justified before any case management

## Decision
Start with an Israeli case manager (Bassem) as stopgap while building UK patient volume. Ultimately, hire case managers locally in England for long-term operations.

## Rationale
Israeli staff already know the system and can begin immediately. Not practical to hire UK staff before patient volume is confirmed. Need to coordinate time allocation with Basel. Adiel firm: long-term solution must be local UK hires.

## Consequences
- Need Basel to allocate Bassem's hours for UK coverage
- Cross-timezone case management initially
- Training needed on UK-specific practices and patient expectations
- Creates knowledge base for eventually onboarding UK-local staff
