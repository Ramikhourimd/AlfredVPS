# Unify Staff Employment Agreements Across All Clinicians

## Context

Shira had been creating individual agreements per staff member ("pratanit le-fi ish tzevet"). Li Yamin's request for fee schedule updates revealed that agreements were inconsistent. Rami directed a shift to unified agreements.

## Options Considered

1. **Individual agreements per staff** — Current approach, each agreement unique per person
2. **Unified agreement template** — Single agreement structure for all staff ("ahidim la-kol") with rate tables standardized

## Decision

Switch from individual per-staff agreements to unified agreements for all clinicians. Four agreement categories: psychiatry adults, psychiatry children, clinical psychology intake, clinical psychology follow-up.

## Rationale

- Reduces administrative overhead for agreement management
- Prevents rate inconsistencies between staff doing the same work
- Simplifies onboarding for new hires
- Enables easier Clalit/Maccabi HMO contract alignment

## Consequences

- All existing agreements need review for alignment
- Shira must coordinate the transition
- Staff currently above unified rates will be grandfathered (not reduced)
