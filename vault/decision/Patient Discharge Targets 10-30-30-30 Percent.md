# Patient Discharge Targets 10-30-30-30 Percent

## Decision (Draft)

Rami proposed a structured patient discharge framework with targets at each clinical milestone:
- **After intake**: Discharge ~10% (patients who do not proceed)
- **After first follow-up**: Discharge ~30%
- **After second follow-up**: Discharge ~30%
- **After third follow-up**: Discharge ~30%

## Status

This is currently a proposal (draft). Rami will conduct a literature review to find evidence-based discharge benchmarks and develop a formal framework before implementation.

## Context

The Israel clinic received 945 new patient inquiries in January 2026 but only converted 77 (8.1%) to actual intake appointments. The bottleneck is not demand but clinic capacity — specifically psychiatrist and case manager availability. A discharge framework would free slots for new patients.

## Open Questions

- What do evidence-based benchmarks look like for psychiatric ADHD clinics?
- Should discharge targets vary by diagnosis (ADHD vs. depression vs. anxiety)?
- How should discharge be communicated to patients (letter, call, transition plan)?
