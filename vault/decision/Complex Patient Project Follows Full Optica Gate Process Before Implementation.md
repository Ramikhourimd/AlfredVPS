# Complex Patient Project Follows Full Optica Gate Process Before Implementation

## Context
Rivi presented her complex patient characterization framework. Before moving to prototype and implementation, the project must follow the full Optica innovation pipeline.

## Decision
The complex patient project must:
1. Complete Optica template chapters 1 and 2 (research and concept)
2. Pass Gate 1 review with Rami, Adi (marketing), and Adiel (business)
3. Build front-end prototype using Manus AI
4. Then proceed to phase 2 (technical feasibility with Ohad) and phase 3 (clinical validation with Stav)
5. Regulation stakeholders (Shachar, Yael, Shmulik) join in later phases

## Rationale
Structured gate process prevents investing engineering resources in concepts that lack commercial viability or clinical soundness.

## Consequences
- Rivi focuses on completing phases 1-2 documentation before any implementation
- Business and marketing get early input before prototype investment
