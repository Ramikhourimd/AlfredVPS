# Innovation Role Separate from CTO Responsibilities

## Context

Rami leads innovation and AI integration at Telia'z, while Shachar holds the CTO role overseeing technology. During the Feb 22 restructuring meeting, the relationship between these two functions needed clarification to avoid overlap and conflict.

## Options Considered

1. **Merge innovation under CTO** — All technology and innovation work under Shachar's authority
2. **Keep innovation separate with collaboration** — Rami's innovation work remains independent from CTO, with collaboration on shared projects

## Decision

Innovation and AI work remains separate from Shachar's CTO responsibilities. The two collaborate on shared projects but maintain independent domains.

## Rationale

Rami's innovation work is clinically driven (AI integration into psychiatric workflows) while the CTO role covers infrastructure, platform, and engineering. Merging them would either dilute clinical innovation or overload the CTO role. Keeping them separate preserves Rami's clinical perspective in product development.

## Consequences

- Clear domain boundaries needed between innovation and CTO
- Collaboration protocol required for shared projects
- Risk of duplication or gaps between the two domains
- Rami retains ownership of clinically-driven technology decisions
