# Survey Market and Build Composite Prototype for Next-Generation Clinical System

## Context

During the 2026-02-18 working session, Rami proposed a collaborative initiative to envision the most advanced possible clinical management system. This would serve as the foundation for an annual development roadmap for Telia'z AI Clinical Platform.

## Options Considered

1. **Incremental feature requests** — Continue adding features based on ad-hoc staff requests
2. **Competitive benchmarking only** — Survey existing systems and match their capabilities
3. **Aspirational composite prototype** — Survey all existing systems, combine best features, then brainstorm beyond current market offerings assuming unlimited technical capability

## Decision

Pursue option 3: a full aspirational prototype exercise. Steps: (1) survey all existing clinical systems, (2) build a composite model merging best features, (3) brainstorm innovative additions beyond what exists, (4) create a visual prototype concept with no budget constraints. Use this as the basis for annual development roadmap.

## Rationale

Incremental improvements risk missing transformative opportunities. By starting from an aspirational vision and working backward, the team can identify high-impact features that would otherwise never surface in day-to-day feedback.

## Consequences

- Rivi leads initial market research phase
- Ohad supports with literature and competitive research
- Creates a long-term vision document for product development
- Requires significant research investment before any development output
