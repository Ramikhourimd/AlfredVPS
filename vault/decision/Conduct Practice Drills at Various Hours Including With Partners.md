# Conduct Practice Drills at Various Hours Including With Partners

## Context

During the [[conversation/On-Call Incident Review Leadership Meeting 2025-09-22]], [[person/Alex Lellouche]] proposed a structured approach to practice drills that goes beyond the basic drill concept already identified in the [[note/On-Call Alert System First Activation Issues 2025-09-21]].

The first real on-call activation failed partly because doctors had never practiced the workflow. Alex proposed a multi-layered drill strategy.

## Options Considered

1. **Single internal test drill** — Just verify the system works once with the on-call doctors.
2. **Multi-layered drill program** — Internal drill first, then drill with partner therapists (Ra'ut), at both normal and unusual hours (e.g., 2am).
3. **No drills** — Fix technical issues and rely on documentation/training only.

## Decision

Option 2: Implement a multi-layered drill program:
1. **Internal drill first** — Test the full workflow internally with on-call doctors and the technical team
2. **Joint drill with Ra'ut** — Conduct a drill that includes Ra'ut (the referring therapist), serving dual purpose: system validation AND rebuilding her confidence in the service
3. **Off-hours drill** — Test the system at unusual hours (e.g., 2am) to verify coverage works when the on-call doctor may be sleeping
4. **Cover all scenarios** — "Let's recover all the options" as Alex stated

## Rationale

- Internal drill catches technical issues before involving external partners
- Including Ra'ut in the drill shows her the system has improved, rebuilding trust after the stressful incident
- Off-hours testing is essential because most real emergencies may happen at night
- The combined approach addresses both technical validation and partner relationship management

## Consequences

- Requires coordination with Ra'ut's organization for the joint drill
- Off-hours drill requires on-call doctors to be available for a test at 2am (or similar)
- Updates the existing [[task/Implement On-Call Practice Drills]] with these specific requirements
