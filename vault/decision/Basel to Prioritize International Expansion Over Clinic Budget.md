# Basel to Prioritize International Expansion Over Clinic Budget

## Context

Basel was being pulled in multiple directions — receiving emails from Shira and Oren with various requests and changes, while also being expected to contribute to the annual budget plan (takhlit) due Thursday. He expressed frustration about conflicting priorities and the difficulty of moving forward while also revisiting past decisions.

## Options Considered

1. **Basel works on both budget and international** — splits focus, likely does neither well
2. **Basel focuses on budget, international waits** — delays strategic priority
3. **Basel focuses on international expansion, Rami handles budget** — clear division of labor

## Decision

Adopt Option 3: Rami explicitly directed Basel to focus on international expansion ("boldanot") and deprioritize the annual clinic budget plan. Rami will handle the takhlit himself, even if it means working at night.

## Rationale

- The annual budget is time-critical (Thursday deadline) and Rami has the context to complete it
- International expansion is Basel's strategic responsibility and requires dedicated focus
- Splitting Basel's attention across both would dilute effectiveness on both fronts

## Consequences

- Rami takes on additional short-term workload (takhlit preparation)
- Basel can dedicate full attention to international expansion work
- Clear accountability: Rami owns clinic budget, Basel owns international
