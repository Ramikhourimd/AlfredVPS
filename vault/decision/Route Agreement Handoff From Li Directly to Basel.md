# Route Agreement Handoff From Li Directly to Basel

## Context

Basel Kanaaneh, newly onboarded as Operations Manager, needed employment agreements to audit compliance. Li Yamin (HR) redirected him to Shira instead of providing documents directly. This ping-ponging between staff was identified as a systemic problem.

## Options Considered

1. **Basel goes to Shira first, then Li fills gaps** — rejected; Shira couldn't handle this previously
2. **Li provides everything directly to Basel** — chosen; Li holds the canonical documents as HR manager

## Decision

All signed employment agreements and contract templates flow from Li Yamin directly to Basel Kanaaneh. No intermediary. Basel then identifies gaps by cross-referencing with Shira's operational knowledge.

## Rationale

Li is the HR manager and holds the canonical documents. Routing through Shira (who previously failed to manage this) creates unnecessary friction. Basel needs a clean top-down handoff to establish order.

## Consequences

Li must cooperate despite her resistance. Oren has spoken with her to set expectations. If she continues to obstruct, this feeds into the broader [[task/Evaluate Li Yamin Role Suitability]] discussion.
