## Review of the Super One 150 watt power inverter.

> [1](#startMs=1748428545312&endMs=1748428583312): Number one, Super One 150 watt power inverter. If you need a small but versatile power inverter, this is a great choice. It's got a bunch of ports, two USB, one AC, and even two cigarette lighter ones, so you can charge a ton of stuff. The cup holder design is a neat idea, but honestly, who wants to give up their drink space? We tested it out and it easily powered phones, iPads, chargers, and it even ran our hair dryer on low without any problem. Sure, 150 watts isn't huge, but it's plenty for most of the gadgets you'll want to use on a road trip. This one's the best if you need an inverter that does a bit of everything.
