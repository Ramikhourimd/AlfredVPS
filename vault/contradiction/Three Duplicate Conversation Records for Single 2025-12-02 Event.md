## Claim A

## Analysis

The duplication likely arose from the same transcript being processed multiple times. The conflicting statuses (active vs resolved) create ambiguity about whether follow-up action is needed. Similarly, the two research notes ("Khouri and Shaddad Family Migration History" and "Khouri Family Migration History and Genealogy") contain near-identical content with minor transliteration differences.

## Resolution
