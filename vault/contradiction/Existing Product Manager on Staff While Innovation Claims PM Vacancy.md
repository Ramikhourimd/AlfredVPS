# Existing Product Manager on Staff While Innovation Claims PM Vacancy

## Claim A

The HR system shows Yael Marciano as Product Manager (full-time, hybrid, Tel Aviv) in both December 2025 and February 2026 exports. Additionally, Nadav Blatt previously held the Lead Product Manager role before being terminated. The company has had and currently has product management capacity on staff.

## Claim B

The constraint "Innovation Function Requires Dedicated Product Manager" states that a product manager hire is needed to serve as the formal interface between Rami's innovation research and Shachar's engineering execution. The task "Establish Innovation-CTO Coordination Mechanism" lists "Product manager role defined or assigned" as an acceptance criterion, implying no PM currently serves this function.

## Analysis

The most likely explanation is scope segmentation: Yael Marciano serves the platform/core product (Telia'z AI Clinical Platform) rather than the innovation/research pipeline. Nadav Blatt's departure as Lead PM may have created a gap that Yael cannot fill alone, or the innovation function requires a PM with different skills (clinical domain knowledge, AI product development). However, this is never explicitly stated — the constraint simply says a PM is needed without acknowledging that one already exists on payroll. This creates ambiguity about whether the organization needs to hire a net-new PM or reassign existing capacity.

## Resolution

Unresolved. The relationship between Yael Marciano's current PM scope and the innovation PM need should be clarified before hiring.
