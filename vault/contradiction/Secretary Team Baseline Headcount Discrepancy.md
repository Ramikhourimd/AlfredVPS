# Secretary Team Baseline Headcount Discrepancy

## Claim A

The secretary team currently has 2 full-time positions. The existing assumption record and constraint records describe the team as having "2 full-time positions" with Alice barely working and Amda going on maternity leave. Growth target is from 2 to 5 FTEs.

## Claim B

The board presentation task lists "Medical secretary team expansion (3 → 5 FTEs)" under the clinic operational plan. This implies a current baseline of 3 FTEs, not 2.

## Analysis

The discrepancy may stem from different counting methods: the "2 full-time" figure may count only dedicated secretary positions, while "3 FTEs" may include Renee's current partial secretary duties before her transition to pure management. Alternatively, a new hire may have already been planned or onboarded between when the constraint was recorded and when the board presentation was drafted.

This matters because it affects the hiring gap calculation: growing from 2→5 requires 3 new hires, while 3→5 requires only 2. Given the maternity leave constraint, the accurate baseline determines urgency and budget.

## Resolution

Needs clarification: confirm whether the baseline is 2 or 3, and whether Renee's current duties are included in the FTE count.
