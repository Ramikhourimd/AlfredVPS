# Roy Shur Holds COO Title While Team Debates Creating COO Authority

## Claim A

Both the December 2025 and February 2026 HR system exports list Roy Shur as "Chief Operating Officer" — a full-time, hybrid position. This is an active C-level role in the formal organizational structure. (Sources: [[note/Telia'z Employee Roster Export December 2025]], [[note/Telia'z Employee Roster February 2026]])

## Claim B

The February 22, 2026 leadership meeting extensively debated whether Rami's CMO role should include operational authority (effectively CMO/COO) or whether clinical and operational leadership should be separated. Oren argued for separation. The discussion treats operational authority at the C-level as an open question to be resolved — as if no COO currently exists. Roy Shur is never mentioned in the meeting notes as holding this authority. (Sources: [[note/Telia'z Leadership Meeting 2026-02-22 Organizational Structure and Roles]], [[note/Organizational Strategy Meeting 2026-02-22]])

## Analysis

This contradiction reveals that Roy Shur's COO title may be nominal or scoped differently than what the restructuring discussion envisions. Possible explanations:
1. Roy's COO role covers parent company operations but not clinic operations — the debate is about clinic-level operational authority
2. Roy's role has evolved or diminished, and the title no longer reflects actual authority
3. The team has unconsciously excluded Roy from the clinic restructuring discussion because he is not involved in day-to-day clinic operations

Regardless of the reason, the fact that a COO exists on paper while the team debates COO authority from scratch indicates a significant disconnect between formal structure and operational reality — consistent with the broader pattern captured in [[contradiction/Formal HR Titles Misalign With Operational Role Reality]].

## Resolution

Unresolved. Clarifying Roy Shur's actual scope and authority would help resolve the CMO/COO debate by establishing what operational authority currently exists at C-level and what gap remains.
