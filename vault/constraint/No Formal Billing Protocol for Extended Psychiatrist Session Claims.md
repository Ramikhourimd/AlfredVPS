# No Formal Billing Protocol for Extended Psychiatrist Session Claims

## Constraint

There is no formal protocol governing how psychiatrists claim compensation for extended intake sessions or other non-standard billing scenarios. Currently, claims are handled ad-hoc on a case-by-case basis between the psychiatrist and the medical director (Rami).

## Source

Identified during 2025-12-04 payment data review session. Noa has been claiming extended intake sessions (counting one intake as equivalent to two sessions) through direct negotiation with Rami, with no standardized process or documentation.

## Implications

- Payments depend on what the employee self-reports rather than system records
- No audit trail for billing exceptions
- Inconsistent treatment between psychiatrists (some negotiate, others don't)
- Part of the broader need for a formal "sal shikum" (rehabilitation basket) protocol

## Expiry / Review

Should be resolved when the clinic protocol framework (currently being developed by Basel and Rami) includes a billing exceptions section. See [[task/Formalize Clinic Protocol Framework Document]].
