# Org Chart Must Be Finalized Before New Hires

## Constraint

The organizational chart reflecting the agreed-upon district model must be finalized — including role descriptions, reporting lines, and dotted-line relationships — before any new management hires can proceed. This is a sequencing constraint, not a regulatory one.

## Source

Explicitly stated in the "Finalize Organizational Chart" task: "A definitive chart is needed before new hires can proceed." The Feb 22 meeting produced conceptual agreement on the district model but did not produce a finalized chart. Multiple competing versions (Basel/Leah proposals vs Rami counter-proposals) remain unresolved.

## Implications

This constraint blocks the Medical Director hire, which in turn blocks Rami's role consolidation, which in turn blocks the full restructuring implementation. Until the org chart is finalized, the organization operates in an ambiguous state where reporting lines and authority boundaries are unclear. This creates a critical path dependency.

## Expiry / Review

This constraint resolves once the organizational chart is approved by leadership. Review target: before next board presentation to Alex.
