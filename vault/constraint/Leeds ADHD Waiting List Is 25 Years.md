# Leeds ADHD Waiting List Is 25 Years

## Constraint
The ADHD diagnostic waiting list in Leeds is approximately 25 years at current service capacity and referral rates. Each year of incoming referrals adds approximately 23 additional years to the backlog.

## Source
Stated by Sean Rejabris during the GP Confederation meeting on 2025-01-22. This is a system-level constraint driven by demand far exceeding capacity.

## Implications
- Face-to-face-only solutions cannot address the scale of demand
- Virtual/technology-enhanced services are essential to make any meaningful dent
- The Right to Choose programme exacerbates the problem by sending patients to external providers at local ICB cost
- Any solution must demonstrate massive scalability (thousands of patients, not hundreds)
- Creates political urgency for local commissioners to act

## Expiry / Review
Ongoing — will only improve with significant service expansion or demand reduction through effective triage.
