# Rental Budget Approximately 1200-1500 NIS Per Month

## Constraint

The family's apparent rental budget is approximately 1,200-1,500 NIS per month. This was the price range discussed and considered "reasonable for the area" during the family building viewing.

## Source

During the 2025-10-09 apartment viewing at the family-owned building, rent was discussed at 1,200-1,500 NIS/month with a 3-year fixed-price lease offered. This was evaluated positively, suggesting it falls within the family's acceptable range.

## Implications

This budget is dramatically below the broader Haifa rental market cited at 8,000-12,000 NIS/month (from neighborhood assessment 2025-09-23). The family building's pricing appears to be a significant discount — possibly due to personal/community connections, unfinished units, or location. This means the family may be relying on below-market-rate opportunities through personal networks rather than the open rental market. Finding comparable rates on the open market will be very difficult.

## Expiry / Review

Review when second child arrives and income situation changes. The 3-year fixed lease offer provides stability if accepted, but limits flexibility.

## ⚠️ Correction (2026-03-03)

The 1,200–1,500 NIS/month figure referenced above was **not** the family's general rental budget. It was the price offered for a specific unit in a family-owned building — a heavily discounted, below-market-rate offer accessed through personal/community connections.

**The actual rental budget for the open-market housing search is 7,500–8,000 NIS/month.**

This figure is derived from the family's broader housing strategy: their current house mortgage is 4,600 NIS/month, which they plan to offset by renting out that property. The net housing cost target is therefore 7,500–8,000 NIS/month for the new rental.
