# UK Clinical Report Formats Require Adaptation From Israel Templates

## Constraint

UK clinic operations require different clinical document formats than those used in Israel. Admin summaries, psychiatrist reports, and clinical documentation must be adapted to UK standards and conventions.

## Source

Stav Zamir raised this at the February 15 team meeting. Rami Khouri confirmed the work is needed but noted it "won't take long" — suggesting the adaptation is bounded but non-trivial enough to require dedicated effort and backlog prioritisation.

## Implications

- Report format specifications must be defined before system launch
- Stav and Rami need to coordinate on exact specifications
- This work competes with other backlog items (prescription system, scheduling) for development capacity
- Cannot launch UK clinic with Israel-formatted clinical documents

## Expiry / Review

Active until UK report formats are defined and implemented. Should be completed before March 31 system launch target.
