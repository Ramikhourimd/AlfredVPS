# CMO Capacity Bottleneck Limits UK Clinical Pathway Design

## Constraint

Dr. Rami Khouri (CMO) is at full capacity across multiple projects (Israel clinic operations, AI platform development, UK expansion). He explicitly flagged during the July 2025 pathway design meeting that he needs "focused, specific deliverables rather than open-ended process management." This limits the velocity of UK clinical pathway design, report format specification, and clinical governance decisions that require CMO input.

## Source

Rami stated this directly during the UK NHS ADHD Pathway Design meeting on 2025-07-03. The constraint is also evidenced by multiple tasks requiring Rami's input (UK report formats, prescription specifications, clinical pathway sign-off) that depend on limited CMO bandwidth.

## Implications

- UK clinical pathway design must be broken into small, specific deliverables rather than broad design sessions
- Other team members (Adiel, Stav) need to pre-structure questions and decisions before bringing them to Rami
- Clinical decisions may be delayed if queued behind Israel clinic priorities
- Risk of knowledge concentration — clinical pathway knowledge sits primarily with one person

## Expiry / Review

Review when team capacity changes (new clinical staff hired, Israel operations stabilize, or UK-based Medical Director appointed who can share clinical decision load).
