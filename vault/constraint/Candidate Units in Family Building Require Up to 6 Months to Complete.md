# Candidate Units in Family Building Require Up to 6 Months to Complete

## Constraint

Some units in the family-owned building viewed on 2025-10-09 are not yet move-in ready. They still require electrical work and painting, with an estimated completion time of approximately 6 months from the viewing date.

## Source

The building owner stated during the 2025-10-09 viewing that unfinished units would take approximately 6 months to complete. Only certain units on specific floors were ready for immediate occupancy.

## Implications

If the family selects one of the unfinished units (potentially the larger 140 sqm option), they face a ~6 month wait before moving in. Given the urgency driven by the wife's dissatisfaction with their current living situation, this delay may push toward either a finished unit in the same building or abandoning this option entirely. The family would need interim housing arrangements or must accept a smaller finished unit.

## Expiry / Review

This constraint should be re-evaluated around April 2026 (6 months from the October 2025 viewing). Construction timelines frequently slip, so the actual availability date may be later.
