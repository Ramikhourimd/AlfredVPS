# MDP-Based Symptom Framework Has Intellectual Property Considerations

## Constraint

The MDP-based (Markov Decision Process) symptom framework developed as part of the AI diagnostic pipeline is considered a novel methodology with intellectual property implications. This constrains how it can be disclosed, published, or described in the research papers.

## Source

Raised during the 2026-02-22 research meeting when allocating content between Paper 1 and Paper 2. The MDP framework was explicitly noted as having "IP considerations" alongside its designation as novel methodology, and was deferred to Paper 2.

## Implications

- The MDP framework cannot be fully detailed in Paper 1 (deferred to Paper 2 regardless)
- Paper 2 disclosure of the MDP methodology may require IP review before submission
- The level of technical detail published may need to be calibrated to protect IP while maintaining reproducibility
- Patent or provisional filing may need to precede publication

## Expiry / Review

Review before Paper 2 drafting begins. IP status should be clarified with Telia'z leadership before the methodology section of Paper 2 is written.
