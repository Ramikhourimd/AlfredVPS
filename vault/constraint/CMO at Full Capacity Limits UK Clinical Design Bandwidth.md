# CMO at Full Capacity Limits UK Clinical Design Bandwidth

## Constraint

Dr. Rami Khouri (CMO) explicitly stated he is at full capacity and can only handle focused, specific deliverables. Open-ended clinical design tasks cannot be absorbed into his current workload. This limits the velocity at which UK-specific clinical requirements can be defined.

## Source

Direct statement from Rami during the OMG pathway design meeting on 2025-07-03: he needs focused, specific deliverables rather than open-ended process management.

## Implications

All UK clinical design work must be broken into small, well-defined deliverables. Multiple downstream tasks compete for limited CMO bandwidth: UK report formats, prescription system specs, OMG data handoff requirements, NICE compliance mapping.

## Expiry / Review

Active as long as Rami remains sole CMO for both Israel and UK operations. Hiring UK-dedicated clinical leadership would relieve this constraint.
