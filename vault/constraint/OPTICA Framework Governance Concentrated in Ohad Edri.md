# OPTICA Framework Governance Concentrated in Ohad Edri

## Constraint

Ohad Edri owns 4 of 13 OPTICA innovation framework steps outright and is the primary contributor on 8 additional steps. This means a single person controls the governance process for nearly all innovation initiatives passing through the OPTICA gate system. Combined with his C-Level Shadowing obligation (2 days/week), his effective availability for OPTICA governance is structurally limited.

## Source

[[note/Ohad Edri Complete Work Manual]] — role documentation compiled 2026-03-19 from 16 source documents. The OPTICA ownership breakdown is explicitly documented in the manual.

## Implications

- OPTICA gate reviews may bottleneck on Ohad's availability, especially given his 2-day/week C-Level shadowing commitment
- If Ohad is unavailable (illness, leave, departure), the OPTICA governance process has no documented backup owner for most steps
- Innovation projects requiring gate approval may face delays disproportionate to their complexity
- Combined with Stav's concentration of clinical validation authority, the innovation pipeline has multiple single-point dependencies

## Expiry / Review

This constraint should be reviewed when OPTICA framework ownership is redistributed or when additional team members are trained on the framework steps.
