# Sunday Leadership Meetings Exclude Clinical Operations Agenda

## Constraint

The weekly Sunday leadership meetings — the primary regular forum for Telia'z company-level decision-making — are structured exclusively around marketing and business development topics: new patient counts, referral source analysis, campaign performance, new secretarial hires, UK expansion progress, and Clalit agreement status. There is no standing agenda item for clinical operations, quality metrics, staff wellbeing, or clinical KPIs.

## Source

Rami described this pattern in his December 5, 2025 personal debrief: the Sunday meetings are "entirely marketing and business-development oriented" with discussions about "new patient counts, referral source analysis (family doctors, campaigns), discussions about new secretaries, the UK expansion, and Clalit agreement progress." He explicitly noted there is "no space for clinical operations, quality metrics, or staff wellbeing" in these meetings. (Source: [[note/Rami Leadership Frustration Debrief 2025-12-05]])

## Implications

- Clinical operations — the core service delivery function — has no regular reporting channel to company leadership
- Rami's 60 clinical KPIs have no forum for review or discussion at the leadership level
- Quality and safety risks accumulate silently because they lack visibility in the primary decision-making forum
- This structural exclusion means clinical concerns only surface reactively (when problems escalate) rather than proactively
- The exclusion reinforces the financial tunnel vision that Rami identifies as a strategic risk ([[constraint/Clinical Perspective Excluded From Cross-Functional Planning Decisions]])

## Expiry / Review

This constraint would be resolved by either:
- Adding a clinical operations section to the Sunday meeting agenda
- Implementing the proposed steering committee ([[decision/Steering Committee for Strategic Governance]]) as an alternative governance forum
- Creating a separate clinical operations review meeting with leadership attendance
