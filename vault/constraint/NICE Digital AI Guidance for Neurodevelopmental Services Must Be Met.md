# NICE Digital AI Guidance for Neurodevelopmental Services Must Be Met

## Constraint

NICE released new guidance on digital and AI use in neurodevelopmental services around December 2024. Any ADHD service using AI-assisted diagnosis, AI-generated clinical reports, or digital clinical pathways must demonstrate compliance with this guidance. The specific requirements have not yet been mapped against Telia'z platform capabilities.
