# NICE Digital AI Guidance for Neurodevelopmental Services Applies

## Constraint

NICE released guidance covering digital and AI technologies in neurodevelopmental services (approximately December 2024). This directly applies to Telia'z's AI-enabled ADHD assessment service and must be complied with for UK operations.

## Source

NICE published guidance referenced in the GP Confederation meeting on 2025-01-22. Rebecca Wilson flagged this as a governance requirement needing mapping.

## Implications

AI features core to Telia'z's value — transcription, summarization, report generation, agenda recommendations — must be validated against this guidance. Non-compliance could block CQC approval, tender success, or trigger post-launch regulatory action.

## Expiry / Review

Active indefinitely. NICE guidance is periodically updated. Initial compliance mapping should be completed before UK launch.
