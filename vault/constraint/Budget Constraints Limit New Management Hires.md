## Constraint

The current budget limits the number of new management positions that can be created as part of the restructuring. Each new management hire must be justified against the clinic's revenue and growth projections.

## Impact

- Medical Director position is proposed at 30% (not full-time) to manage cost
- Some roles may need to be filled by existing staff in expanded capacities rather than new hires
- Shared services model is partly driven by cost efficiency (not just organizational logic)
- Financial plan must be updated and validated before finalizing the org chart
