# NICE Digital AI Guidance Applies to UK Neurodevelopmental Services

## Constraint

NICE released new guidance on digital and AI technologies for neurodevelopmental services (circa December 2024). Any ADHD service using AI-powered tools — including Telia'z's AI transcription, session summarization, agenda recommendations, and report generation — must comply with this guidance. The specific requirements of the guidance have not yet been reviewed against Telia'z capabilities.

## Source

NICE (National Institute for Health and Care Excellence) — the UK body that sets clinical standards. Rebecca Wilson flagged this new guidance during the GP Confederation meeting on 2025-01-22, noting it was released around December 2024 and specifically covers digital/AI in neurodevelopmental services.

## Implications

- Telia'z's AI capabilities (which are core to its value proposition) must be validated against NICE requirements before UK service launch
- GP Confederation and OMG partnership bids referencing AI/technology must demonstrate NICE compliance
- Non-compliance could block CQC approval or NHS procurement success
- The guidance may require specific transparency, explainability, or human oversight requirements for AI-generated clinical outputs

## Expiry / Review

NICE guidance is periodically updated. Initial review should happen immediately as part of the UK regulatory mapping workstream led by Rebecca Wilson.
