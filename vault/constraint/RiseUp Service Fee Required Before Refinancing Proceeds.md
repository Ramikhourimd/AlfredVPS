# RiseUp Service Fee Required Before Refinancing Proceeds

## Constraint

Rami must select and pay for a RiseUp advisory service tier (2,400 NIS partial or 6,000 NIS full) before the refinancing process can proceed. RiseUp will not begin work without formal engagement.

## Source

RiseUp's service model — presented during the 2026-01-26 consultation. Two tiers available: partial (2,400 NIS) and full (6,000 NIS).

## Implications

- The refinancing process is currently blocked pending Rami's decision on service tier
- The advisory fee is an upfront cost that reduces net savings (though small relative to ~200,000 NIS expected benefit)
- Ball is in Rami's court — no progress until engagement is initiated

## Expiry / Review

No stated deadline, but delays risk market condition changes that could affect the savings estimate.
