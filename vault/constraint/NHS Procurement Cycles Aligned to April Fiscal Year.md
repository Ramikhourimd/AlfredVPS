# NHS Procurement Cycles Aligned to April Fiscal Year

## Constraint

NHS procurement and contract awards are aligned to the NHS financial year, which starts in April. Tender bids must be submitted and evaluated in time for contracts to commence at the start of the fiscal year. This creates fixed procurement windows that external partners must align to.

## Source

Mentioned in the GP Confed partnership meeting (2025-01-22): "NHS financial year starts April 2025, so procurement needs to be in by then." This is a structural feature of NHS funding and contracting, not specific to the Telia'z engagement.

## Implications

- Missed procurement windows mean waiting an entire year for the next cycle
- All tender preparation, clinical pathway design, and governance mapping must be completed within the window
- Partner readiness (technology, staffing, regulatory) must align to the NHS fiscal calendar, not Telia'z's own timeline
- Budget allocations for ADHD services are set at the fiscal year level by ICB commissioners

## Expiry / Review

Permanent structural constraint of NHS operations. Review annually to confirm specific procurement timeline for each fiscal year.
