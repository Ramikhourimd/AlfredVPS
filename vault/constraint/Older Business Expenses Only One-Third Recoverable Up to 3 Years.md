# Older Business Expenses Only One-Third Recoverable Up to 3 Years

## Constraint

Business expenses paid from personal cards can be submitted for reimbursement up to 3 years after incurrence, but only one-third of the expense value is recoverable. This applies to expenses that were not submitted through proper company channels at the time of purchase.

## Source

Accountant advice during the financial planning discussion on 2025-09-23. The accountant noted this rule while discussing Rami's UK travel expenses paid on personal credit cards.

## Implications

- Expenses should be submitted promptly through company forms to recover full value
- Delayed submission results in 67% loss of recoverable value
- Creates urgency to process any outstanding personal-card business expenses before the 3-year window closes
- Company should pay directly from company accounts whenever possible to avoid this penalty

## Expiry / Review

Ongoing Israeli tax/accounting rule. Review if tax regulations change.
