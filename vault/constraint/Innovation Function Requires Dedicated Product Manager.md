# Innovation Function Requires Dedicated Product Manager

## Constraint

The innovation function led by Rami requires a dedicated product manager hire to execute the AI integration and product development pipeline. This is listed as a specific resource requirement in the board presentation agenda, indicating it is a recognized gap that blocks or limits innovation capacity.

## Source

The board presentation task includes "Resource requirements (product manager hire)" under the Innovation Roadmap section. This is not a speculative need — it is being presented to the board as part of the resource plan.

## Implications

- Without a product manager, Rami must handle both strategic innovation direction and tactical product execution, further stretching his already overloaded role
- This hire adds to the budget pressure alongside the Medical Director (30% position), Operations Manager, and secretary hires
- The product manager role must be scoped relative to the CTO's team to avoid overlap with R&D — reinforcing the existing constraint about CTO-Innovation coordination gaps
- Board approval may be required for this hire, adding it to the list of decisions gated by the Alex presentation

## Expiry / Review

This constraint persists until a product manager is hired or the innovation function is restructured to not require one. Review at board presentation outcome.
