# NHS Financial Year Cycle Drives Procurement Deadlines

## Constraint

The NHS financial year starts in April. All procurement for new services must align to this cycle — tenders need to be submitted, evaluated, and awarded in time for contracts to commence at or shortly after the financial year boundary.

## Source

Standard NHS procurement cycle. Jim Barwick confirmed at the Jan 2025 GP Confederation meeting that the ADHD tender was imminent and needed to align with the April 2025 financial year start.

## Implications

Any UK market entry via NHS tender pathway must plan backwards from the April financial year start. Missing a cycle means waiting an additional 12 months. This creates urgency on partnership agreements, clinical pathway design, and bid preparation.

## Expiry / Review

Recurring annual constraint. Each financial year creates a new procurement window.
