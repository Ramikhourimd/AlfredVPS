# No Development Capital Available for Maghar Land Construction

## Constraint

The Khouri family lacks the capital required to develop their Maghar land plot (~1 dunam, ~4M NIS value). Villa construction was explicitly ruled out as "not feasible due to lack of capital," and the joint venture option was considered suboptimal since it requires investment before returns. Rami stated directly that he "has cash flow for rent but not capital for building."

## Source

Family gathering discussion on 2025-09-22. Multiple family members acknowledged this financial reality while evaluating development options for the Maghar plot.

## Implications

- The "build on family land" housing path is blocked without external capital, a developer partner, or selling part of the asset
- Joint venture (iska) remains theoretically possible but is viewed unfavorably
- This constraint forces the family into the rental market despite holding a high-value land asset
- Selling the land is the only self-funded path to converting the asset into housing capital

## Expiry / Review

Review if family financial position changes, mortgage/loan options are explored, or a favorable developer partnership emerges.
