# Clalit Partnership Patient Volume Ceiling Unknown

## Constraint

The Telia'z team does not know whether the Clalit partnership has an implicit or explicit patient volume cap. If the clinic suddenly sends 300 patients per month through the Clalit pipeline, it is unknown whether the district contact would approve that volume or push back.

## Source

Identified during the 2025-11-09 Clalit partnership preparation meeting. The team flagged this as a question to clarify with the Clalit contact (Tzachi/Eli) at the next-day meeting.

## Implications

- Growth planning for Clalit cannot assume unlimited volume
- Marketing campaigns targeting Clalit-covered patients risk generating demand the partnership cannot absorb
- If a cap exists, it directly constrains the clinic's revenue diversification strategy away from Maccabi dependency

## Expiry / Review

Should be clarified in the first operational meeting with Clalit contact. Review status after partnership pilot period.
