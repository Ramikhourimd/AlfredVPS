# PA Role Must Be Genuine Employment Not Informal Support

## Constraint

The personal assistant role for Rami must be structured as a genuine working arrangement with clear deliverables, defined scope, and professional expectations — not as an informal personal support setup or performative employment. The family advisor explicitly emphasized this distinction during the planning discussion.

## Source

Family advisor guidance during the November 9, 2025 PA planning conversation. The advisor is supportive ("I can stand behind it for sure") but conditional — support depends on Rami presenting "a clear, well-thought-out proposal" that establishes real work, not a convenience arrangement.

## Implications

- Rami must define concrete role scope, time commitment, weekly structure, and responsibilities before approaching Amani
- The arrangement must withstand scrutiny as legitimate employment (important for family dynamics and financial justification)
- The 40,000 NIS annual savings narrative must connect to genuine productivity gains, not just personal convenience
- Trial period recommended to validate the arrangement is real and mutually beneficial
- If the role cannot be defined with genuine deliverables, the advisor's endorsement may not hold

## Expiry / Review

This constraint applies during the PA hiring process and initial employment period. Once the role is established and producing demonstrable value, the legitimacy question is resolved by evidence. Review after the 1-2 week trial period.
