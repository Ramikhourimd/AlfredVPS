# New Case Manager Onboarding Lacks Structured Orientation Process

## Constraint

New case manager employees (e.g., Muli Abrikan and others) reported receiving no onboarding orientation to the clinic. An Excel template for case presentations — a standard tool — has not been redistributed to new joiners. Ori Shukron, responsible for case manager training and supervision, has not sent training reminders consistently. Reserve duty (milluim) has further disrupted the training schedule.

## Source

Rivi reported these gaps during the 2026-03-17 innovation team meeting. She felt it wasn't her place to manage Ori's responsibilities but wanted to flag the issue. Rami agreed to raise it with Basel rather than intervening directly, preserving Basel's management authority.

## Implications

- New staff operate without baseline knowledge of clinic workflows, tools, and protocols
- Quality of case management may suffer during the unstructured ramp-up period
- Compounds the existing isolation constraint — new staff are both isolated AND untrained
- Creates risk of inconsistent clinical practices across the case manager team

## Expiry / Review

Active until Basel addresses training gaps and structured onboarding is established. Should be reviewed after reserve duty period ends and training resumes.
