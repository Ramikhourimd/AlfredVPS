# Google Ads UK Requires Separate Approval for Healthcare Campaigns

## Constraint

Google Ads requires approval for healthcare advertising in the UK market, separate from the approval already obtained for Israel. This adds lead time to the UK marketing launch.

## Source

Google advertising platform policies for healthcare services. In Israel, approval was already obtained. UK approval is a separate process.

## Implications

- Marketing campaign launch depends on Google approval timeline (which Yael cannot fully control)
- Need to initiate approval process as early as possible to avoid delaying patient acquisition
- Once approved, campaigns can be activated quickly ("just push the button")

## Expiry / Review

One-time approval required, then ongoing compliance.
