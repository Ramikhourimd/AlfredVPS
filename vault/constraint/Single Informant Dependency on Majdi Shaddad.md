# Single Informant Dependency on Majdi Shaddad

## Constraint

All genealogical claims, migration dates, branch relationships, surname origins, and diaspora connections in the Khouri Family Genealogy project trace to a single informant: Majdi Shaddad. No second independent oral historian, family elder, or parallel researcher has been consulted or cited in any source record. Even if written primary sources were found, they would still be interpreted through Majdi's framework.

## Source

Methodological observation across all six source records. Every conversation and research note attributes its content to the 2025-12-02 discussion with Majdi Shaddad.

## Implications

- Confirmation bias risk: Majdi's 45-year research may have settled on one narrative that excludes alternative interpretations
- No triangulation possible for contested claims (e.g., Hauran vs Zahleh origin)
- If Majdi's unpublished family tree document contains errors, there is no independent check
- Future research should prioritize finding a second informant from the Eilabun or Nazareth branches

## Expiry / Review

Review when a second informant is consulted or when Majdi's written family tree document is obtained and can be cross-referenced.
