# Clinical Viability Validation Authority Concentrated in Stav Zamir

## Constraint

Stav Zamir is designated as the "primary authority for validating clinical soundness and safety of all new and proposed technological solutions" at Telia'z. Every innovation initiative must pass through her clinical viability check before proceeding. This is in addition to her existing concentration of prompt engineering pipeline knowledge.

## Source

[[note/Stav Zamir Comprehensive Role and Operational Manual]] — comprehensive role manual compiled 2026-02-25. Her first responsibility pillar is explicitly titled "Clinical Viability Validation."

## Implications

- All innovation projects are gated on a single person's clinical judgment and availability
- Combined with her prompt engineering pipeline ownership (already documented), Stav represents a double bottleneck: she validates both the clinical concept AND the AI implementation
- Her dual role as active clinician (maintaining "hands-on clinical work to maintain expertise and user perspective") further constrains her availability for validation work
- No backup validator is documented — if Stav is unavailable, innovation projects cannot advance through clinical viability gates

## Expiry / Review

Should be reviewed when clinical validation responsibilities are distributed or when additional clinicians are trained to perform viability assessments for innovation projects.
