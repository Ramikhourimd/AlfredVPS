# CMO at Full Capacity Limiting Clinical Specification Throughput

## Constraint

Rami Khouri (CMO) explicitly stated he is at full capacity during the OMG pathway design meeting (2025-07-03). He requested "focused, specific deliverables rather than open-ended process management." Multiple UK launch workstreams depend on his clinical input, creating a single point of bottleneck.

## Source

Direct statement from Rami during the OMG pathway design session: "Rami flagged upfront that he is at full capacity and needs focused, specific deliverables rather than open-ended process management."

## Implications

- UK clinical pathway design, report format specifications, prescription requirements, and OMG handoff data definitions all require Rami's input
- No backup clinical authority has been identified for UK-specific work
- Open-ended process management requests to Rami will be deprioritised
- Tasks must be structured as specific, bounded deliverables to get Rami's attention
- UK launch timeline is directly coupled to Rami's availability

## Expiry / Review

Review when additional clinical staff are hired or when UK clinical specifications are complete. This constraint has persisted from at least July 2025 through February 2026.
