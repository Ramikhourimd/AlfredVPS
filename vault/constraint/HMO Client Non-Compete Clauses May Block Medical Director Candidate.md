# HMO Client Non-Compete Clauses May Block Medical Director Candidate

## Constraint

The leading candidate for the Medical Director position is currently employed at Clalit. Telia'z is a supplier (service provider) to Clalit. Hiring from a client organization may violate non-solicitation or non-compete provisions in the contractual agreements between Telia'z and the HMOs.

## Source

Rami and Dekkel identified this risk during their 2025-11-09 discussion about clinical manager candidates. The concern extends to Maccabi as well, since some staff work across both HMOs. Management-level hires may be treated differently from clinical staff in these agreements, but this has not been verified.

## Implications

- The top Medical Director candidate may be off-limits without contractual review
- Similar restrictions may apply to future hires from any HMO partner
- Violating non-solicitation clauses could jeopardize the entire HMO partnership
- Legal review of both Clalit and Maccabi agreements is required before approaching any candidate currently employed by these organizations

## Expiry / Review

Active until contractual terms are reviewed and confirmed. The task to check these clauses has been created but outcome is not yet recorded.
