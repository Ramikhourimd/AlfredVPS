# No Physician on Board of Directors

## Constraint

The Telia'z Board of Directors (Oren, Dekkel, Ilan Rosenfeld) currently has no physician member. For a company whose core business is medical services (telepsychiatry), this creates a governance gap where strategic decisions affecting clinical operations are made without direct clinical representation at the highest level.

## Source

Listed as unresolved item #5 from the Feb 22 leadership meeting. This concern connects to Rami's broader frustration that clinical perspectives are not sufficiently integrated into company decision-making — the Board represents the most senior level where this gap exists.

## Implications

- Strategic decisions (market entry, service expansion, partnerships with HMOs) lack formal clinical input at Board level
- The steering committee was proposed as a partial mitigation, but it operates below the Board
- Israeli legal precedent (Bagatz) regarding physician oversight of medical operations may create regulatory risk if challenged
- Hiring a CMO without Board representation means the CMO has operational authority but no governance voice

## Expiry / Review

Should be reviewed when the CMO role is formalized. Board composition may need adjustment as Telia'z scales internationally and regulatory requirements increase.
