# Israel Clinic at 8 Percent Intake Conversion February 2026

## Constraint

The Israel Telia'z clinic is currently converting only ~8% of new patient inquiries into intake appointments. In January 2026, 945 new patients contacted the clinic but only 77 received intake appointments.

## Root Causes

- **Psychiatrist shortage**: Not enough psychiatrists to handle intake volume
- **Case manager capacity**: Case managers are at maximum caseload
- **No discharge framework**: Patients accumulate without structured discharge, consuming ongoing capacity

## Impact

- Patients face long wait times or are turned away
- Revenue is capped by capacity rather than demand
- Staff burnout from overloaded caseloads
- Competitive risk if patients seek alternatives

## Mitigation Strategies Under Discussion

1. Launch jobs page to accelerate recruitment
2. Implement patient discharge framework (10/30/30/30)
3. Psychiatry Academy to train and pipeline new psychiatrists
4. Optimize scheduling and follow-up intervals
