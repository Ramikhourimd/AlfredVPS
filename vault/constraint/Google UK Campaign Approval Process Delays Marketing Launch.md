# Google UK Campaign Approval Process Delays Marketing Launch

## Constraint

Google UK requires campaign approval for healthcare advertising, adding lag time between campaign submission and activation. Unlike Israel where campaigns can go live immediately, UK campaigns have a review/approval step.

## Source

Discussed in team meeting. Adiel noted they are "stuck" with Google approval in the UK, while Israeli campaigns can launch as soon as the button is pressed.

## Implications

- UK patient acquisition campaigns must be submitted well ahead of target launch dates
- Cannot do rapid-response marketing in UK
- Israel campaigns can fill interim capacity while UK approvals process

## Expiry / Review

Ongoing constraint for all UK Google Ads campaigns.
