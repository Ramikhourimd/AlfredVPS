# Innovation and R&D Coordination Gap Blocks Product Alignment

## Constraint

Innovation (led by Rami) and R&D/CTO (led by Shachar) operate semi-independently with no formal coordination mechanism. Rami has produced a 70-page innovation plan that Shachar has not reviewed. This gap prevents alignment between innovation vision and technical execution.

## Source

Identified during 2026-02-22 leadership meeting and captured in the task to establish an Innovation-CTO coordination mechanism. The gap is structural — no product manager role or formal interface exists between the two functions.

## Implications

- Innovation ideas may be technically infeasible or redundant with existing R&D plans
- R&D priorities may not reflect strategic innovation direction
- No product manager role exists to bridge the gap
- Risk of duplicated effort or misaligned timelines
- 70-page innovation plan remains unreviewed by the person responsible for technical execution

## Expiry / Review

Should be resolved when the Innovation-CTO coordination mechanism is established (task assigned to Rami). Review at next leadership meeting.
