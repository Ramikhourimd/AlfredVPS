# Maccabi Prohibits Charging Patients for Additional Assessment Services

## Constraint

Under the clinic's agreement with Maccabi health fund, the clinic **cannot charge patients additional fees** beyond the standard session rate for assessment-related services.

## Impact

This constraint directly affects the clinic's ability to offer formal, extended ADHD assessment services (which require 2-3 hours of testing and evaluation). The economics do not work when the clinic can only bill for a standard session rate while delivering a multi-hour assessment.

This is one of the factors supporting the decision to limit the clinic's ADHD service to clinical diagnosis only, rather than offering formal MOH-compliant assessment reports.

## Scope

- Applies to all Maccabi-insured patients
- Covers any assessment, testing, or evaluation services beyond the standard psychiatric consultation
- Does not affect private-pay patients (though the clinic primarily serves HMO patients)

## Verification

Surfaced during the November 10, 2025 meeting between Rami and Ori. Should be verified against the actual Maccabi contract terms.
