# Psychiatrist AI Feedback Collected Through Informal WhatsApp Channel

## Constraint

Psychiatrist feedback on AI-generated clinical summaries is collected through informal WhatsApp group monitoring rather than a structured feedback system. Rami directed Rivi Idelman to "monitor psychiatrist WhatsApp complaints and extract insights" as the primary mechanism for identifying AI summary quality issues.

## Source

Operational practice established during the 2025-11-11 meeting between Rami Khouri and Rivi Idelman. No formal feedback collection system exists for AI summary quality assessment.

## Implications

- Research claims about AI system quality improvements rely on informally collected, potentially incomplete complaint data
- Systematic issues may go unreported if psychiatrists stop complaining or adapt workarounds
- The research paper cannot cite structured feedback metrics — only anecdotal/qualitative evidence from WhatsApp monitoring
- Any quality improvement claims (e.g., real-time vs transcript mode) depend on this informal feedback channel for validation

## Expiry / Review

Should be reviewed when/if a formal AI feedback system is implemented. Currently active for the duration of the research study.
