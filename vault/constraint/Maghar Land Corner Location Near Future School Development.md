# Maghar Land Corner Location Near Future School Development

## Constraint

The Maghar family land plot (~1 dunam, parcels 80 and part of 78) is a corner plot on the main road between Eilabun and Maghar, at a roundabout, adjacent to land allocated for a church with a future school planned. This specific location affects both development potential (commercial viability of a corner/main-road plot) and surrounding land use (school development will change traffic, demand, and zoning context).

## Source

Family gathering discussion on 2025-09-22 described the plot's precise location: corner plot on the main Eilabun-Maghar road at a roundabout. Nearby land has been allocated for a church, with a school planned on that site. This detail comes from family members with direct knowledge of the area.

## Implications

- **Development advantage**: Corner plots on main roads with roundabout access have higher commercial development potential than interior residential plots, potentially supporting the apartment-building option discussed by the family
- **Future school proximity**: A school development nearby would increase residential demand and property values, but may also introduce traffic and noise constraints
- **Zoning interaction**: The church/school land allocation signals active municipal planning in the area, which could affect what the family is permitted to build (existing takhrit documentation available for ~250 NIS)

## Expiry / Review

This constraint is tied to municipal planning decisions. Review when: (1) the church/school development progresses, (2) a land survey is completed, or (3) the family obtains the takhrit documentation.
