# Recruitment Marketing Campaigns Blocked Until Careers Page Is Live

## Constraint

Yael Marciano's recruitment marketing campaigns (Google Ads for both Israel and UK markets) cannot launch until the Telia'z careers/jobs page is live with a functional application form. Google UK campaigns additionally require a separate approval process (described as "slow"), while Israel campaigns can launch immediately once the page is published. The page structure and content are ready for adult psychiatrists, child psychiatrists, and case managers, but form submission testing requires Roy Shur's involvement, creating a secondary dependency.

## Source

Identified across the 2026-02-15 team meeting and associated task records. Li Yamin confirmed page structure is ready. Dekkel expressed explicit frustration about the delay, pushing for immediate launch "even imperfect." Multiple task records reference this as a blocker.

## Implications

Every day the careers page remains offline compounds the recruitment delay. Given the clinic needs approximately 176 quarter-position hires to reach target scale, and each hire requires sourcing through these campaigns, the recruitment timeline is directly extended by the page launch delay. The Google UK approval adds a further sequential delay for international expansion recruitment.

## Expiry / Review

This constraint expires once the careers page goes live and forms are tested. As of 2026-02-15, the target was same-day or next-day launch. Current status should be verified.
