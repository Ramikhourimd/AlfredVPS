# NHS April Financial Year Drives Procurement Timeline

## Constraint

NHS procurement cycles align to the April financial year. Any new service contract must have procurement completed before the financial year boundary. For the Leeds ADHD tender discussed in January 2025, this meant the bid needed to be submitted within approximately 4 weeks, with contract award before April 2025.

## Source

Standard NHS financial year structure. Jim Barwick (GP Confederation) confirmed during the January 2025 meeting that the tender was imminent and procurement needed to align with the April 2025 financial year start.

## Implications

- All partnership agreements, governance frameworks, and bid materials must be ready before the NHS procurement deadline
- Missing an April deadline means waiting until the next procurement cycle (potentially 6-12 months)
- Budget allocations for new services are tied to financial year planning — late entry means competing for already-committed funds
- This creates a recurring annual pressure point for any new NHS contract

## Expiry / Review

This constraint recurs annually. Each NHS financial year (April to March) has its own procurement window. Review before each tender cycle.
