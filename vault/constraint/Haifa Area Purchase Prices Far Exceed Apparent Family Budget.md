# Haifa Area Purchase Prices Far Exceed Apparent Family Budget

## Constraint

Suitable houses in the Haifa metropolitan area cost 4.3-5.2M NIS (for ~300 sqm), while the only purchase figure discussed by the family was approximately 850K NIS (for a property requiring significant renovation). This gap of 3.5-4.4M NIS effectively rules out house purchases in desirable Haifa neighborhoods without alternative financing or asset liquidation.

## Source

The neighborhood assessment (2025-09-23) documented purchase prices of 4.3-5.2M NIS for ~300 sqm houses. A casual family conversation mentioned 5-7M NIS as current going rates. Meanwhile, the property investment discussion (2025-10-09) referenced 850K NIS as an acquisition cost, with additional renovation needed — suggesting this is closer to the family's purchase capacity.

## Implications

- Purchasing a house in the desirable Haifa neighborhoods appears unaffordable without leveraging the Maghar land plot (valued at ~4M NIS)
- Renting remains the more viable short-term path
- If the Maghar land is sold or developed, it could bridge the gap to purchase
- Apartments (vs. houses) may be more affordable but were not priced in the source records

## Expiry / Review

Review if Maghar land is sold, family income changes significantly, or market conditions shift.
