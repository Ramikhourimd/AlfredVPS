# Israel Clinic at 8 Percent Same-Month Intake Conversion February 2026

## Constraint
Of 945 new patients registering in February 2026, only 77 (8%) received intake appointments in the same month. Approximately 600 have March appointments; ~300 have no appointment at all. The clinic opened February with near-full capacity.

## Source
Dashboard data (Rami's operational dashboard) presented at team meeting 2026-02-15.

## Implications
- Current capacity is severely inadequate for demand
- Adding 80 case manager hours will serve only ~60 more patients — still far from 945 demand
- End-of-year scaling would require 30-40 additional psychiatrists
- Must implement active patient discharge strategy alongside hiring
- The 8% figure alarmed leadership (Adiel calculated ~4% effective rate at current pace)

## Expiry / Review
This is a point-in-time operational metric. Should improve as new staff onboard and discharge strategy takes effect. Review monthly.
