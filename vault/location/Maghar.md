# Maghar

Arab village in the Lower Galilee, Israel. Mixed Druze and Christian community on the road between Eilabun and the main highway.

Significant to the Khouri family as an ancestral area with family land holdings, particularly in the Khallet al-Sharif neighborhood at the edge of the village. The family owns a corner plot of approximately 1 dunam (1,182 sqm) on the main road.

The village also has historical significance in the context of Christian settlement in the Galilee — the Maghar church founding is referenced in family genealogy research.
