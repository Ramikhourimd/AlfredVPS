# Center Parcs Alsace

Family holiday resort in the Alsace region of northeastern France, near Strasbourg. Part of the Center Parcs chain of holiday villages across Europe (France, Germany, Belgium, Netherlands).

## Vault Context

Mentioned by [[person/Rami Khouri]] during the [[event/Telia'z Company Fun Day 2025-09-21]] as a recent family vacation destination. The resort offers swimming pools, tennis, cycling, and children's games. Rami noted that staff spoke exclusively French — an observation about the strong French linguistic identity in Alsace.

[[person/Alex Lellouche]] was familiar with Center Parcs and mentioned his sister-in-law had also visited one. Alex noted they were "nice places" for families.
