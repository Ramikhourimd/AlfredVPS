# Rami Mortgage Refinancing

Personal mortgage refinancing project initiated January 2026 through the RiseUp (רייזאפ) platform. The mortgage was originally taken from Bank Hapoalim in May 2020 for approximately 790,000 NIS. Current balance is approximately 705,000 NIS with monthly payments that have risen from 3,400 to 4,600 NIS.

**Goal:** Reduce total mortgage cost by approximately 200,000 NIS while maintaining current monthly payment level and minimizing early repayment penalties (property sale planned in near future).
