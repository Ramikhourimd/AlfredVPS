# Khouri Family Genealogy

A personal research project tracing the Khouri and Shaddad family history. The Shaddad family migrated from Hauran (Syria) around 1725, through Lebanon, and eventually settled in Galilee villages in the mid-1800s. The name "Khouri" derives from "qasis" (priest), as a family member served as a priest in the village of Rameh.

Three known branches of the Shaddad family settled in Eilabun, Rameh, and Nazareth. The project aims to connect relatives across branches and diaspora (including Beirut and Chile).
