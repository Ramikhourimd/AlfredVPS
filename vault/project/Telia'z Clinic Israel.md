# Telia'z Clinic Israel

The main operational clinic of Telia'z, providing remote psychiatric care across Israel. Serves thousands of patients through telemedicine, with contracts with major HMOs (Maccabi, Clalit). Currently scaling up with plans to reach 7,000+ patients.

Key operational aspects:
- Telepsychiatry model (fully remote patient care)
- Psychiatrists, case managers (social workers), and medical secretaries
- Regulated by Israeli Ministry of Health
- AI-integrated clinical workflows
