# Telia'z UK Expansion

Proposed expansion of Telia'z telepsychiatry operations to the United Kingdom. This expansion serves as a key planning reference for the organizational restructuring — the structure being designed must support replicating the Israeli clinic model internationally.

Key considerations:
- Need a local Medical Director for each international clinic
- CMO at company level oversees clinical standards across all clinics
- COO at company level ensures operational consistency
- Local regulatory compliance (UK healthcare regulations)
- Staffing model adapted for UK market
