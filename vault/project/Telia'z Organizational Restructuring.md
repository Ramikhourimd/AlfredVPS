# Telia'z Organizational Restructuring

Comprehensive restructuring of Telia'z organizational hierarchy to support growth from a single Israeli clinic to a multi-country operation. Key areas of focus:

1. **C-Level Structure**: Defining CMO, COO, CTO, and Innovation roles
2. **Clinic Management**: Establishing Clinical Director, Operations Manager, and Administrative Manager under a "district" model
3. **Pod Structure**: Implementing team pods for scaling psychiatrists and case managers
4. **Resource Sharing**: Determining which functions (HR, marketing, finance) remain centralized vs. clinic-level
5. **Governance**: Establishing a steering committee (ועדת היגוי) with clinical veto authority
