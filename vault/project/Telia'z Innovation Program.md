# Telia'z Innovation Program

Innovation department at Telia'z bridging clinical needs and technological/process improvements. Translates clinical quality requirements into AI-powered solutions, operational efficiency tools, and evidence-based research outputs.

## Scope

- **AI Research Infrastructure** — AI research email workflows, NotebookLM presentations, Notion project templates
- **Complex Patient Screening Protocol** — Problem definition, research, and proposed clinical decision support
- **KPI Framework Development** — Clinical completeness and measurability metrics connected to database
- **Research Manuscripts** — Co-authoring with Noam on AI-enabled mental health care flow publications
- **Employee Experience Research** — Measurement frameworks and periodic survey development

## Operating Model

Top-down, template-driven concept design process:
1. Rivi owns problem definition, research questions, and solution options
2. Dr. Khouri gates approval before execution phase
3. WIP limit: Max 3 concurrent projects
4. Routing: Product features to Yael, operational issues to Shira, cross-functional items to Basel
