# Rami Housing Search

## Overview

Rami Khouri is searching for a larger family apartment. Key requirements include 3-4 bedrooms (for his small daughter and one child on the way), a home office, and an open kitchen. The search is focused on residential buildings in the area.

## Requirements

- 3-4 bedrooms minimum
- Home office space for Rami
- Open or large kitchen (strong preference)
- Privacy (concern about exposed balconies)
- Street parking availability
- Budget range: approximately 1,200-1,500 NIS/month
