# Telia'z AI Diagnostic Research Paper

Research paper investigating AI-assisted psychiatric diagnosis using a multi-agent LLM pipeline. The system extracts symptoms from patient questionnaires and case manager transcripts, then uses fusion models and expert/biased agents to predict DSM diagnoses. Key finding: psychiatrist PTSD diagnostic bias in post-war context can be replicated by giving LLM agents contextual priming.
