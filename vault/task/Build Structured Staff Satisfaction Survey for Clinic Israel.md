# Build Structured Staff Satisfaction Survey for Clinic Israel

Design a structured, periodic survey instrument for measuring clinical staff satisfaction at Telia'z Clinic Israel. The survey must move from qualitative impressions to measurable, trackable metrics.

## Context

During the 2026-02-18 meeting, Rami directed Rivi to approach staff experience research with academic rigor. Current findings are qualitative (interview-based). Need a quantitative instrument for periodic measurement.

### Required Dimensions
- Financial satisfaction (compensation, bonuses)
- Work-from-home experience
- Hours and scheduling preferences
- Workplace atmosphere and belonging
- Professional development opportunities
- Technology experience (systems, tools)
- Support systems (supervision, management access)
- Team feeling and isolation levels

### Segmentation Variables
- Role type (psychiatrist vs. case manager)
- Shift timing (morning vs. evening)
- Weekend work (Shabbat workers)
- Weekly hours at clinic
- Number of concurrent patients
- Tenure at Telia'z
