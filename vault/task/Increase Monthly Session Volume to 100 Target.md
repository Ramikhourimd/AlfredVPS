# Increase Monthly Session Volume to 100 Target

The January 2026 KPI dashboard shows 82 sessions conducted against a monthly target of 100. The 18-session gap represents missed revenue and unmet patient demand.

## Context

- 125 inquiries received in the month, 109 sessions scheduled, 82 conducted
- The funnel shows loss at two points: inquiry-to-scheduling (92% conversion) and scheduling-to-conducted (~75%)
- Cancellations and no-shows likely account for the scheduling-to-conducted gap
- The secretarial capacity constraint may limit scheduling throughput

## Potential Levers

1. Reduce cancellation/no-show rate (if that is the gap between 109 scheduled and 82 conducted)
2. Increase secretarial capacity to improve scheduling throughput
3. Add psychiatrist availability slots
4. Improve the 92% to 95% conversion target for inquiry-to-scheduling
