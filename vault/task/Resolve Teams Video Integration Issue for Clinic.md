# Resolve Teams Video Integration Issue for Clinic

The Microsoft Teams integration for telepsychiatry sessions is causing significant operational problems. Patients experience technical difficulties entering the video call, which leads to:
- Delayed session starts
- Complete no-shows due to frustration
- Wasted psychiatrist time (clinic pays for reserved slots)
- Estimated ~10%+ loss of potential revenue/gross profit

## Options Discussed
- **Switch to Zoom** — simpler but requires migration
- **Fix Teams integration within the app** — technically possible but substantial work
- **Hire a contractor** — Shachar estimated 3-4 months of development; Rami confirmed budget is available given growth trajectory
