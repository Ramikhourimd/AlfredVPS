## Description

Recruit a Medical Director for the Israel clinic at approximately 30% position. This person would serve as the clinical lead on-site, managing day-to-day clinical operations and supervising the psychiatrist team under Rami's overall clinical authority.

## Context

The restructuring discussion identified the need for a senior physician presence at the clinic level. Rami cannot fill this role full-time given his other responsibilities (innovation, overall CMO duties). The Medical Director would be the clinical anchor within the "district" model.

## Requirements

- Licensed psychiatrist in Israel
- Minimum 30% position (approximately 2 days/week)
- Experience in clinical team management
- Alignment with Telia'z telepsychiatry model
