# Formalize Clinic Protocol Framework for Leadership Presentation

Rami needs to take the brainstorming output from the 2025-12-04 session with Basel and create a formal protocol framework document. This document should:

1. Define the two protocol categories (clinical and administrative) with clear subcategories
2. Map each protocol area to measurable KPIs
3. Show how clinical outcomes (treatment response, remission, reduced hospitalizations) translate to business value (better HMO contracts, revenue growth)
4. Include training/onboarding and staff retention as key enablers
5. Present the gap between current operational focus and needed operational focus

The goal is to persuade leadership to shift Sunday meeting metrics and overall operational approach from surface indicators (new patients, campaign performance, monthly revenue) to the deeper operational and clinical metrics defined in this framework.
