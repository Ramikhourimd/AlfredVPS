# Bring Elinor Into On-Call Operational Management

[[person/Rami Khouri]] proposed bringing Elinor into operational management of the on-call psychiatry service, as well as similar projects that do not have designated case managers.

## Context

During the [[conversation/On-Call Incident Review Leadership Meeting 2025-09-22]], the team identified a structural gap: the on-call service had no operational project manager other than Rami. When Rami was abroad, training and coordination fell through.

Rami suggested: "I will manage this, and what I will do is bring in either Elinor or Shira to handle the operational management of this in parallel. I need another set of eyes on this."

Specifically for managing projects that do not involve case managers — where the usual clinical onboarding process (run by Shira) does not fully cover operational coordination.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Elinor (or alternative) is identified and briefed on the on-call service operational needs
- Clear handoff of specific operational tasks (training tracking, drill scheduling, system status monitoring)
- Rami retains overall ownership per [[decision/Rami Owns On-Call Project Management End-to-End]]
