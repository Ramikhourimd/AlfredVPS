# Map All Required Clinic Protocols and Identify Gaps

Conduct a comprehensive mapping of all protocols required for clinic operations and identify where gaps exist.

## Context

Discussed during the November 10, 2025 meeting between Rami and Ori. The clinic has some protocols from Elinor's previous work (primarily children-focused) but lacks a systematic inventory of all required protocols for both adult and children services.

## Protocol Categories to Map

1. **Clinical Protocols**
   - Patient intake (adult and children)
   - ADHD assessment and diagnosis
   - Risk management and safety
   - Treatment planning and monitoring
   - Medication management
   - Discharge and follow-up

2. **Administrative Protocols**
   - Scheduling and appointment management
   - Patient communication
   - Complaints handling
   - Staff onboarding and training
   - Billing and insurance claims
   - Data management and privacy

## Acceptance Criteria

- Complete inventory of required protocols documented
- Each protocol categorized as: exists, needs update, or missing
- Priority ranking for protocol development
- Timeline estimate for gap closure
