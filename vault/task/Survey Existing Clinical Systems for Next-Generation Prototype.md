# Survey Existing Clinical Systems for Next-Generation Prototype

Research existing clinical management systems to build a vision prototype of the most advanced possible system.

## Context

Rami proposed during the 2026-02-18 meeting that Rivi lead a research initiative to survey all existing clinical systems, identify best features, and design a forward-looking prototype. This would inform the yearly development plan for the innovation and product teams.

## Approach
1. Survey existing clinical management platforms (features, UX, capabilities)
2. Build a combined model of best features from all systems
3. Brainstorm innovative additions (AI-powered, next-generation capabilities)
4. Produce a concept document assuming unlimited technical capability
5. From this, derive a phased development roadmap
