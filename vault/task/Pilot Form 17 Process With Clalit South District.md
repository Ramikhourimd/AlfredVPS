# Pilot Form 17 Process With Clalit South District

## Objective

Run a pilot test of the Form 17 referral process with Clalit South District to validate the diagnostic subscription workflow end-to-end before full launch.

## Context

Agreed during the [[conversation/Clalit South Referral System Setup Meeting 2025-11-10]]. The pilot should focus on [[decision/Clalit South Pilot Starts With Diagnostic Subscriptions Only|diagnostic subscriptions only]], where Form 17 is auto-generated.

## Steps

1. Coordinate with Tzachi to identify a test referring physician in Clalit South
2. Have the physician create a diagnostic referral for a test patient
3. Verify that Form 17 is auto-generated by the Clalit system
4. Confirm that the referral reaches Telia'z and can be processed
5. Document any friction points or system issues

## Target Date

November 20, 2025 (pilot launch)
