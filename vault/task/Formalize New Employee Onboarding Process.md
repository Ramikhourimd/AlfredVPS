# Formalize New Employee Onboarding Process

Design and document a structured onboarding process for new clinic employees, incorporating the framework discussed by Rami and Elias on 2026-02-12.

## Context

The meeting identified that technology access (Teams, HoViD) is the critical path item that must be resolved on day one. Operational and administrative onboarding can happen gradually, but without technology access a new clinician cannot see patients.

Key elements to formalize:
- Three-track model: operational, administrative, clinical (with technology as mandatory prerequisite)
- Monthly or bi-monthly cadence depending on hiring volume
- Stav Zamir or Shira Lachman as delivery leads for the operational + technology combined session
- Clinical onboarding handled separately through supervision structure
