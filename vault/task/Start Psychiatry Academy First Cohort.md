# Start Psychiatry Academy First Cohort

Launch the first cohort of the Psychiatry Academy, a training program designed to upskill Telia'z clinicians in technology-assisted psychiatry. This also serves as a recruitment and retention tool.

## Context

Discussed at the 2026-02-15 team meeting. Rami proposed the academy concept as part of a broader strategy to:
1. Train existing staff to be "technology-enabled clinicians"
2. Use the academy as a recruitment differentiator
3. Position Telia'z as a place where clinicians learn to work at the frontier of clinical AI

Rami emphasized this needs substantive planning before being marketed externally — it must deliver real value, not just be a branding exercise. The program needs defined curriculum, resource allocation, and a clear offering before promotion.

**Timeline:**
- Closed cohort registration: early March 2026
- Training sessions begin: mid-March 2026
