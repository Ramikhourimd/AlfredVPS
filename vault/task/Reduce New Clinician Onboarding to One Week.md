# Reduce New Clinician Onboarding to One Week

Redesign onboarding to cut new clinician time-to-productivity from ~1 month to ~1 week.

## Four Training Layers
1. **Clinical** — general orientation (not all clinicians need)
2. **Operational** — Telia'z-specific workflows
3. **Administrative** — systems, scheduling, contacts
4. **Technical** — platform training (gating requirement for treating patients)

## Target: bi-weekly cohort-based onboarding sessions, automate/pre-record where possible.
