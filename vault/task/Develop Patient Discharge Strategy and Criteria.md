# Develop Patient Discharge Strategy and Criteria

Create a structured approach to actively manage patient discharge, targeting treatment duration of 4-6 months with specific discharge rates at each follow-up stage.

## Context

With 945 new patients monthly and only 8% getting intake appointments, the clinic must actively manage patient flow. Rami proposed discharge targets (10% after intake, 30% after each of 3 follow-ups) and outlined a research-driven approach.

Steps:
1. Literature review on psychiatric patient treatment duration
2. Analyze Telia'z patient data — identify natural churn patterns and characteristics
3. Define discharge criteria based on patient characteristics
4. Modify intake questionnaire to identify early discharge candidates
5. Build a psychiatrist-facing feature that flags patients for discharge

Dekkel requested the financial model be aligned with the discharge strategy.
