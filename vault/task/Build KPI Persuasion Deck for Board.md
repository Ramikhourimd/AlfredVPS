# Build KPI Persuasion Deck for Board

Create a presentation that articulates the causal chain from clinical excellence to revenue growth:
1. Better treatment response and remission rates
2. Reduced hospitalizations and ER visits
3. Lower burden on public health system
4. Better HMO contract terms (Maccabi, Clalit)
5. Higher clinic revenue

Rami emphasized this is critical: without a compelling case, leadership will continue reviewing surface metrics (new patients, Facebook campaigns, monthly revenue) and miss the operational depth needed for scaling.

Includes the hypergrowth model comparison (horizontal hiring vs. vertical efficiency).

This was identified as the key deliverable from the 2025-12-04 brainstorm session. Rami noted he will need to make this case repeatedly if not formalized now.
