# Build Structured Employee Satisfaction Survey Instrument

Design and build a periodic survey to measure staff satisfaction across multiple dimensions, enabling data-driven tracking over time. Must move from qualitative impressions to measurable, trackable metrics.

## Context

Rami directed Rivi during 2026-02-18 meeting to transform qualitative burnout findings into structured research. The survey is the primary measurement tool.

## Dimensions to Cover

- Financial satisfaction and compensation perception
- Work-from-home experience and comfort
- Hours/scheduling preferences and impact
- Workplace atmosphere and belonging
- Professional development and growth opportunities
- Technology experience and tool satisfaction
- Support systems and management accessibility

## Segmentation Variables

- Role type (case manager vs. psychiatrist)
- Morning vs. evening shift
- Weekend workers
- Hours per week
- Number of concurrent patients
- Tenure/time at Telia'z
