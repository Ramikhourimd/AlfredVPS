Create a concept document for the pre-treatment patient video initiative. Rivi already gathered initial requirements from Renee about what happens during first phone calls, where patients get stuck, and what information is missing.

## Context

The video is intended to set patient expectations before their first intake appointment. Rivi discussed this during the 2026-02-18 meeting and was reminded that her deliverable should be a concept document — not the video itself or its detailed production specs.

The concept doc should include:
- What problem the video solves (patient drop-off, unclear expectations, intake friction)
- What content should appear (especially explaining WHY follow-up sessions are important)
- Who the target audience is
- How this connects to the broader patient experience flow
- Optional: production recommendations (clearly labeled as extra/suggestions)

Handoff: Once the concept document is approved, it goes to the development/creative team for execution.
