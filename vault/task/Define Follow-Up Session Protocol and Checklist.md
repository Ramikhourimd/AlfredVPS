# Define Follow-Up Session Protocol and Checklist

Create a structured protocol defining what should happen during 3rd and 4th patient follow-up sessions at Telia'z Clinic Israel.

## Context

Rivi's staff research revealed that while intake and early follow-ups (1st, 2nd) have clear goals (assess side effects, check dosage response), later follow-up sessions lack defined structure. Case managers improvise content, leading to inconsistency and reduced engagement from both staff and patients.

An intake checklist Rivi previously created was well-received by case managers — they reported it clarified expectations and improved session quality. Staff explicitly requested a similar resource for follow-up sessions.

## Goals
- Define expected objectives for 3rd and 4th follow-up sessions
- Create a checklist of topics/assessments to cover
- Identify opportunities to leverage these sessions for patient engagement and outcome measurement
- Align with the pre-treatment patient video to set patient expectations about follow-up value
