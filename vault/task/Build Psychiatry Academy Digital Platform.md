# Build Psychiatry Academy Digital Platform

Create the infrastructure for the Psychiatry Academy — a structured training program for psychiatry residents at Telia'z Clinic Israel.

## Context

Three training tracks planned starting September 2025:
1. **First-year orientation** — weekly group sessions on residency integration, ER rotations, on-call duties
2. **Stage A exam preparation** — digital platform with lectures, practice questions, mock exams (replaces expensive external courses)
3. **Stage B exam preparation** — more complex, possibly in-person; may start later

## Deliverables by September 2025

- [ ] Digital platform with video lectures and exam questions
- [ ] Syllabus for Track 1 (first-year orientation) and Track 2 (Stage A prep)
- [ ] Recruit instructors for weekly group sessions
- [ ] Marketing materials to promote academy in recruitment outreach
