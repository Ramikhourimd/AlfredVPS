# Create Pre-Treatment Patient Video Concept Document

Write a concept document (not production spec) for a pre-treatment patient orientation video for Telia'z Clinic Israel.

## Context

Rivi has been working on a pre-treatment video idea based on conversations with Renee about where patients commonly drop off in the intake process. She drafted initial content but Rami clarified that her deliverable should be a concept document — defining the problem, the research behind it, and the proposed approach — not a production specification.

## Concept Document Requirements
- Problem statement: Where do patients drop off and why?
- Research basis: What information did Renee and staff feedback reveal?
- Proposed approach: What should the video cover conceptually?
- Key themes: Value of follow-up sessions, what to expect during treatment journey, reducing patient anxiety
- Optional addendum: Implementation recommendations (format suggestions, who should produce it)
