# Define Telia'z Company Identity and Strategic Vision

Rami identifies a fundamental identity vacuum at Telia'z. Team members — including Adi Lavi (marketing), Adiel, and others — don't know what the company truly is. Each interprets the identity based on their own role. The brand is currently being presented as a psychiatric service provider (Telia'z Health), but Rami believes the identity should be broader, leaving room for future expansion beyond psychiatry.

## Context

- Adi Lavi branded everything as "Telia'z Health" = psychiatric services
- Rami advocates for "Telia'z Health by Telia'z" to preserve brand flexibility
- Company identity must precede structural decisions (org chart, CEO role)
- Technology changes rapidly; the identity must be durable regardless of external shifts
- Rami believes he uniquely understands the company's DNA and trajectory
