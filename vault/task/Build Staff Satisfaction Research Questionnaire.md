# Build Staff Satisfaction Research Questionnaire

Build a structured, measurable staff satisfaction questionnaire that can be sent periodically to Telia'z clinical staff (psychiatrists and case managers).

## Context

Rami directed Rivi to treat staff feedback as academic research, not informal reporting. The questionnaire must produce measurable data that can track improvement over time. It needs to cover multiple dimensions and allow segmentation by staff type, schedule, and seniority.

## Required Dimensions
- Financial satisfaction (compensation, bonuses)
- Work comfort (home office setup, schedule flexibility)
- Hours and workload intensity
- Team atmosphere and sense of belonging
- Professional growth and development opportunities
- Technology and system quality
- Management support and responsiveness
- Mission and purpose alignment

## Segmentation Variables
- Role type (psychiatrist vs case manager)
- Weekly hours worked
- Shift pattern (morning, evening, weekend)
- Active caseload size
- Tenure at Telia'z

## Delivery Format
- Google Sheets with one research question per row
- Rami will build automated processing to return structured findings
