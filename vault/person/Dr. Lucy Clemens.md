# Dr. Lucy Clemens

GP and Clinical Lead for neurodevelopmental services at the GP Confederation in Leeds. Previously worked with Jason Brook. Also sits on the NHS national ADHD task force. Strong advocate for expanding ADHD services into primary care, involving GPs, nurse prescribers, and pharmacists alongside psychiatrists. Keen on understanding AI tool capabilities and clinical governance safeguards. Previously involved in BMA medical politics.

Key concerns raised during the meeting:
- Where UK-based psychiatrists are located and how shared care works
- Whether AI makes clinical decisions vs. serves as efficiency tool
- Future-proofing the model for primary care integration
- How Telia'z uses its profits (motivated by patient outcomes, not commercial gain)
