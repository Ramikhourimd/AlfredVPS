## Pre-Telia'z Background

### Career at Clalit Health Services (2017–2025)
- Joined Clalit in 2017 at the onset of Israel's mental health reform
- Established child/youth mental health clinic in Shefa-Amr from scratch (25 staff)
- Established neurology and child development center in same building (combined 52 staff)
- Established eating disorders clinic (~2022)
- Managed budgets for all 13 mental health clinics in Haifa and Western Galilee district
- Clinic maintained 3,800 patients with ~2 new referrals per day
- Clinical team: 8 psychiatrists, 1 neurologist, 6 psychologists, 4 social workers
- Promoted to national Field Activity Manager (marketing, service, retention) shortly before leaving
- Prior to Clalit: medical secretary and Deputy Manager of Clinics at Bikur Rofeh (Haifa/Krayot)

### Private Venture
- Founded private clinic company serving Clalit, Leumit, Meuhedet, and Ministry of Defense
- Sold 50% to Ali Awad (Shams Group); partner Dr. Wasim Reli as Medical Director
- Remains as shareholder/silent partner

### Research
- Coordinated clinical research with Erasmus MC Rotterdam on schizophrenia and autism
- Recruited multiple Israeli hospitals to participate (Rambam, Nahariya, Carmel, Nazareth, Soroka)
