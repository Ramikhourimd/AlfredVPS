# Maghar Land Plot Block 2054

Family-owned land parcel located in the Lower Galilee, adjacent to the regional road connecting Maghar with Gilon.

## Details

- **Block/Plot**: Block 2054, Plot 32
- **Area**: Approximately 1,100 sqm (~1.1 dunam)
- **Zoning**: Mixed urban (ironi mesholav) — permits residential, commercial, hospitality, employment
- **Road access**: Direct frontage on regional road between Maghar and Gilon
- **Infrastructure**: No existing water, electricity, or sewage connections; road access only
- **Environmental restrictions**: None known
- **Estimated value**: Approximately 4 million NIS (per 2025-09-22 family discussion)

## Zoning Permissions

The mixed urban zoning allows a wide range of uses without requiring a zoning change:
- Residential construction
- Commercial/retail
- Hospitality and tourism
- Employment and light industry
- Mixed-use development

## Key Constraints

- No physical infrastructure on site (water, electricity, sewage)
- Family lacks capital for development
- Olive trees on adjacent parcel serve as protective measure against land interference
- Previous sale attempts fell through due to trust/brokerage issues
