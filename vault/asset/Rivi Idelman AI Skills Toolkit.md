# Rivi Idelman AI Skills Toolkit

## Details

Suite of 8 integrated AI agent skills designed for [[person/Rivi Idelman]] in her UX Lead role on the [[org/Telia'z]] Innovation Team. Built as part of the agentic workspace initiative. Skills cover daily work management (rivi-pa), UX domain expertise (rivi-ux-expert), workflow automation (rivi-workflow), stakeholder relationship management (rivi-stakeholders), knowledge curation (rivi-knowledge), OPPO problem framing methodology (rivi-problem-framer), training content creation (rivi-training-creator), and career development tracking (rivi-career-coach).
