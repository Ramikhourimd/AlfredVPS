# Vehicle Service Visit Warranty Dispute and Repairs 2025-10-09

## Context

Voice memo recorded on October 9, 2025 during a service center visit for Rami's vehicle (pre-BYD, approximately 3 years old at the time). This is a continuation of the same visit documented in [[note/Vehicle PPF Removal Damage Discovery 2025-10-09]], capturing the warranty negotiation and additional repair items discussed with service technicians.

## Headlight PPF Warranty Dispute

The central dispute concerned headlight damage discovered after PPF (Paint Protection Film) removal:

- **Service center position:** Classified the headlight damage as "impact damage" (מכה) — not covered under warranty.
- **Rami's position:** Insisted there was no impact — the damage appeared after 3 years under PPF and he believes the PPF itself caused deterioration.
- **Technician's explanation:** PPF generates heat that damages LED headlights. The technician stated he has seen multiple cases of PPF applied to headlights causing the headlights to burn out. He advised never to apply PPF to headlights.
- **Outcome:** Service refused warranty coverage. Rami expressed frustration ("I paid 600,000 on vehicles, this is a matter of principle — a vehicle like this shouldn't break down") and instructed them to remove the headlight repair from the service order. He plans to pursue the issue independently.

## Other Service Items Addressed

### Spoiler Peeling
- Spoiler was peeling and scheduled for replacement under the service visit.
- Estimated repair time: approximately 40-45 minutes (combined with steering wheel work).

### Steering Wheel Issue
- A steering wheel problem was identified and included in the service order.
- Repair combined with spoiler work at approximately 40-45 minutes.

### License Plate Screw
- The front license plate mounting screw came out because the hole is not threaded — it's just a punched hole.
- Technician proposed drilling a new hole to re-secure the plate. Rear plate has proper threading.
- Technician will check if the service center can do the drill work on-site.

### Sunroof/Window Motor Issue
- Rami reported an issue with the sunroof or window mechanism — it's not operating smoothly, appearing to skip or stutter rather than running continuously.
- Technician diagnosed a possible internal motor failure — the motor responds but intermittently stops.
- If motor replacement is needed, repair time may extend beyond the baseline estimate.

### Seat Issue
- A seat problem was flagged for warranty evaluation.
- Warranty coverage: 4 years and 80,000 km. Technician indicated the seat issue is likely covered but needs inspection to confirm no external damage caused it.

### Tire Rotation
- Tires had reached 20,000 km. Tire rotation was completed during this visit.
- Technician noted this was the last rotation needed — next visit will require new tires rather than rotation.

### Additional Items
- Wiper filter replacement was also on the service ticket.

## Estimated Service Time

Total estimated repair time: approximately 1.5 hours for the standard items (spoiler, steering wheel). Additional time if the sunroof motor needs replacement.

## Significance

This service visit, combined with the PPF headlight damage discovery documented earlier the same day, represents a frustrating experience for Rami with his pre-BYD vehicle. The warranty refusal on what Rami considered a manufacturing-related defect, and the principle-based frustration over a vehicle costing hundreds of thousands of shekels, likely contributed to the decision to purchase the [[asset/BYD Vehicle]] the following month (November 2025).
