# On-Call Budget Allocation and Compensation Structure 2025-09-01

## Overview

[[person/Oren Taliaz]] provides a fixed monthly on-call budget of **25,000 NIS** for [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]]. [[person/Rami Khouri]] has full discretion over how this budget is distributed among on-call staff. The arrangement runs from August 2025 through December 2025.

## Budget Mechanics

| Component | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Total monthly budget | 25,000 NIS | Fixed allocation from Oren |
| Atef — half-month rate | 5,000 NIS | Pro-rated: 2,500 NIS/week |
| Elinor — variable | Per-day rate | Proportional to days worked; no phone-call pay |
| Rami — "the gap" | Remainder | Whatever is left after distributions = bonus |

## Distribution Process

1. Rami calculates each person's on-call days for the month.
2. Rami determines the proportional compensation per person.
3. Rami sends Oren a monthly breakdown table.
4. Rami submits a payment request (drishat tashlum) followed by an invoice.
5. The remainder after all distributions goes to Rami as a bonus via payslip (tlusha).

## Key Constraints

- Elinor does not get compensated for phone calls, only for actual on-call shift days.
- Atef's rate is benchmarked at 5,000 NIS per half-month (same proportional rate as Elinor).
- Rami dropped the idea of routing payments through a personal company; compensation goes through the payslip instead.
- Oren asked psychiatrists to submit invoices promptly — delays push payments to the next approval cycle.

## Duration

- **Start**: August 2025 (pro-rated for partial month).
- **End**: December 2025.
- The existing December employment agreement remains unchanged.
- Oren will issue a short addendum or separate document to formalize this arrangement.
