# Ambient Voice Memo — Song of Songs Allegorical Interpretation 2025-09-22

## Context

Brief ambient voice memo captured on 2025-09-22, likely during a Shabbat gathering or religious study setting. An unidentified speaker explains the traditional Jewish allegorical reading of Song of Songs.

## Content Summary

The speaker references the verse from Song of Songs: "As a lily among thorns, so is my beloved among the daughters" (כשושנה בין החוכים, כן רעתי בין הבנות). They explain that according to the sages (חכמינו), this is not simply a love poem between a man and a woman — it is an allegory for the love of the Holy One, Blessed Be He (הקדוש ברוך הוא) for Israel.

This reflects the dominant rabbinical interpretive tradition that Song of Songs describes the relationship between God and the Jewish people, which is why it was included in the biblical canon despite its overtly romantic imagery.

## Original Hebrew

> שיר השירים שכתוב שיר האהבה, כשושנה בין החוכים, כן רעתי בין הבנות. אומרים חכמינו זה לא סתם אהבה של גבר ואישה. הקדוש ברוך הוא.
