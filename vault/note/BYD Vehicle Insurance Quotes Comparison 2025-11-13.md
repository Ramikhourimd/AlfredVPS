## Key Details

- **Bituach Yashir is an official BYD insurer** with full cooperation partnership, meaning no anti-theft device (Ituron) or security system required
- **9 (Tisha) and Bituach Yashir are the same company** operating under two different brands, though pricing can differ between brands
- Rami noted the previous vehicle owner paid only 6,000 NIS comprehensive through Bituach Yashir; Shiri explained this was likely a BYD launch promotion for new vehicles that cannot be applied to 2023 model transfers
- Rami also mentioned paying less for his Tesla through Phoenix Insurance, noting the BYD quotes seem high by comparison
- **Bundle discount applied** because Rami has been insured with Bituach Yashir for mortgage insurance for 5+ years

## Decision

Rami decided to proceed with Bituach Yashir despite it being the most expensive option. Key factors:
- Official BYD insurer status (full manufacturer cooperation)
- No anti-theft device requirement (saves additional cost)
- Bundle discount with existing mortgage insurance
- Full coverage including original windshields and replacement vehicle
- No interest charges on comprehensive installment payments
- Payment spread over 11 installments (mid-period start)
