# Nova Survivor Patient Intake Platform Coordination 2025-10-09

## Context

Voice memo captured on 2025-10-09. A series of phone calls at Telia'z Clinic Israel coordinating the intake of a high-risk patient — a Nova festival volunteer who attempted suicide approximately two weeks after the Nova event. The patient had been receiving treatment for several weeks and presented at the clinic that day.

Multiple staff members coordinated across three sequential calls to handle the clinical and administrative workflow.

- **Speaker 1:** A clinical staff member (case manager or coordinator) who initiated the calls and coordinated the overall workflow
- **Speaker 2:** Another staff member assisting with the platform intake process
- **Speaker 3:** [[person/Rami Khouri]] — provided technical platform support, confirmed system email delivery
- **Speaker 4:** [[person/Ahmed Masalha]] (Ahmad) — psychiatrist on-call, received the summary email and discussed forwarding referral to the therapist

## Patient Case Summary

- The patient was a **Nova festival volunteer** who had been with agencies working at Nova
- Approximately **two weeks after Nova**, the patient attempted suicide
- The patient had been in treatment for roughly 2-3 months
- The patient presented at the clinic on this date
- The case involves **military affairs** (עסקרית מע') — indicating the patient has military service connections

4. Once the patient fills out the required form, they will automatically receive an appointment scheduling notification
