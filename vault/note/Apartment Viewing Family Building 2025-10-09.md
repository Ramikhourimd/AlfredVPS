# Apartment Viewing Family Building 2025-10-09

## Context

On 2025-10-09, Rami Khouri visited a family-owned residential building to view available apartment units. The visit was captured via voice memo (Arabic, Levantine dialect). The building owner (name not provided) showed the units and discussed terms.

- Relatively new construction
- Ground floor serves as storage — each apartment has its own dedicated storage unit
- Electrical infrastructure closet on the ground floor

- Pricing discussed as reasonable for the area

## Rami's Requirements and Preferences

- **Bedrooms**: 3-4 rooms needed (small daughter, one child expected)
- **Home office**: dedicated workspace for Rami
- **Kitchen**: strong preference for an open or large kitchen
- **Privacy**: concern raised about exposed balcony — feels too open to neighbors or passersby
- **Parking**: street parking situation discussed — potential difficulty with on-street availability

## Assessment

The building offers competitively priced units with flexible lease terms and dedicated storage. The 140 sqm unit could meet the family's space needs. Key concerns are balcony privacy, unfinished state of some units (6-month wait), and street parking limitations. The family ownership structure suggests stable, long-term landlord relationship.
