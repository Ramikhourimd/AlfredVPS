# Shira Contract Bonus Negotiation Call 2025-09-01

## Context

On 2025-09-01, Rami called [[person/Alex Taliaz]] to discuss [[person/Shira]]'s unsigned employment contract at [[org/Telia'z]] Clinic Israel. Rami had previously spoken with Lior (likely [[person/Liron Megidash]] from [[org/NB Law]]) about the contract terms. The call covered two key issues blocking Shira's signature.

## Issue 1: Bonus Percentage Too Low

The original contract defined Shira's bonus at **0.5% of total clinic revenue**. At the clinic's current monthly revenue of approximately **400,000 NIS**, this yields only **200 NIS/month** in bonus — an insignificant amount.

- Even reaching 750 NIS/month would be "nice" but the existing **cap of 1,500 NIS** was described as "very far away"
- Both the percentage and the cap made the bonus feel meaningless to Shira

**Agreement reached:** Double the bonus percentage from 0.5% to **1% (0.01) of total clinic revenue**.
- At 400,000 NIS revenue, the bonus would be **400 NIS/month**
- As revenue grows (three months, higher numbers), she would approach the cap faster
- The **1,500 NIS cap remains unchanged** for now
- Both agreed to **review the cap at year end**, based on whether Shira is genuinely converting potential into revenue

Rami's rationale: the bonus itself is not a large amount, but it matters symbolically. They want Shira to receive the bonus and feel recognized, while she demonstrates her ability to grow clinic revenue.

## Issue 2: Social Benefits on Bonus

Shira requested that **social benefits (pension contributions / הפרשות סוציאליות)** be applied to the bonus component of her compensation. Rami noted this is unusual — he does not fully understand the implications — but asked Alex to approve it so the matter could be closed.

Rami's position: "I don't really understand this, I'm not familiar with it in the corporate world. In my opinion, it's possible to give her this." He asked Alex to confirm or reject, and to communicate the decision to the lawyers.

## Urgency and Motivation

Rami emphasized the need to **finalize the contract quickly**:
- Shira has not yet signed the employment contract
- She is "starting to show effort and momentum" (מתחילה להראות שהיא משתדלת ומתאמצת)
- Rami wants to "not break this momentum" by letting the contract remain unresolved
- He requested Alex send an email confirming both changes, so the lawyers can update the contract and get Shira to sign promptly
