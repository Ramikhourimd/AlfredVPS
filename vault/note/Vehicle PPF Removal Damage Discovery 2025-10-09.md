# Vehicle PPF Removal Damage Discovery 2025-10-09

## Context

Voice memo recorded on October 9, 2025 at Rami's location. Rami was examining his vehicle (pre-BYD, valued at approximately 300,000 NIS) with at least one other person after removing PPF (Paint Protection Film) stickers that had been applied for three years.

## Key Findings

### Damage Discovered Under PPF

After removing the PPF sticker from the headlights, Rami found visible damage — dents and surface deterioration — across multiple headlights (both front and rear). Rami stated unequivocally that this damage was not pre-existing and offered to undergo a forensic inspection if needed. He suspects the PPF mechanism itself may have caused the deterioration.

### Stress Cracks Identified

A companion (speaker 6) examined the headlights more closely and identified:
- A scratch on the surface
- What appeared to be a **stress crack** ("seder mimtakh"), not an impact mark
- The crack pattern appeared from both sides, suggesting a structural or tension-related issue rather than external impact
- Assessment: "Something is not right here"

### Action Requested

Rami asked someone to tell "Alex" to come look at the damage for a second opinion.

### Frustration Expressed

Rami expressed frustration that a vehicle costing 300,000 NIS should not have this kind of quality issue with its headlights.

## Additional Context

The initial portion of the recording includes a brief financial discussion (in Arabic/Hebrew) about account calculations — a 150 shekel discrepancy needing resolution. One participant mentions dealing with "Tesla" regarding separate matters.

The conversation also touched on buying conditions and negotiating leverage when purchasing two vehicles at the 300,000 NIS total range.

## Timeline Connection

This vehicle damage discovery predates Rami's purchase of the [[asset/BYD Vehicle]] in November 2025 by approximately one month. The headlight quality issues with his previous vehicle may have been a contributing factor in the decision to purchase a new vehicle.
