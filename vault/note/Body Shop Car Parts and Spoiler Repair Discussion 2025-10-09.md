## Car Parts Discussion (Arabic)

The main Arabic segment covers a discussion between Rami and a body shop technician about vehicle headlights and spoiler work:

### Headlight Pricing
- Technician offered to check availability and pricing for replacement headlights (فوانيس)
- Quoted 250 NIS per headlight for used/aftermarket units
- All four headlights (inner and outer pairs) quoted at 4,000+ NIS
- Outer headlights alone quoted at 2,500 NIS
- Rami provided his phone number for a callback on final pricing

### Spoiler Repair Plan
- The spoiler was found to have been reattached with non-original adhesive pads ("medicat") rather than proper flat-mat adhesive
- The technician proposed a multi-step plan:
  1. Remove the spoiler from the vehicle
  2. Take it offsite for professional cleaning to remove all non-original adhesive
  3. Bring it back clean for inspection
  4. If the underlying surface is defect-free, reattach with proper adhesive under warranty
- The shop cannot take warranty responsibility if defects are visible under the non-original adhesive
- Rami can leave the vehicle at the shop for all the work to be done in one go

### Shop Capabilities
- The shop mentioned working with Tesla, police vehicles, and GMC — positioning themselves as experienced with various vehicle types
- A worker named Said (first name only) was referenced as being on-site
