# Children Clinic Patient Management and Guardian Documentation 2025-11-10

## Context

Discussion during the 2025-11-10 operations meeting about the children clinic patient management, with 188 Maccabi children currently in the system. Rami raised several operational concerns about parental consent, guardian documentation, and questionnaire integration.

## Patient Volume

The children clinic currently has 188 patients under Maccabi. This volume requires structured processes for parental consent and custody documentation.

## Parental Consent Workflow

Rami directed the team to establish clear guidelines for the children clinic on how to handle parental consent:
- Every child patient requires parental consent before treatment
- The intake process must verify who has legal authority to consent
- Jenny should be consulted on how the current consent flow works and where gaps exist

## Guardian and Custody Documentation

A key policy discussion emerged around guardian claims:
- When one parent claims to be the sole guardian, the clinic **must** require a court order as documentation
- This protects the clinic legally — accepting verbal sole-guardian claims without documentation creates liability
- Standard separation/divorce situations where both parents share custody follow normal consent procedures
- The court order requirement applies specifically to sole guardianship claims

## Conners Questionnaires

Rami raised the need to integrate Conners questionnaires (ADHD assessment tools) into the Clickx system:
- Currently these may be handled outside the EHR system
- Integration would streamline the children clinic workflow
- Need to check feasibility with the Clickx system capabilities

## Key Takeaways

1. **188 Maccabi children** in the system — significant volume requiring structured processes
2. **Court orders required** for sole guardian claims — non-negotiable policy
3. **Conners questionnaires** need Clickx integration for streamlined workflow
4. **Parental consent verification** must be part of the intake checklist
