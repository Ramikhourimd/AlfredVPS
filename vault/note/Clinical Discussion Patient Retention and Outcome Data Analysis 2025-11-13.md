## Key Points

### Patient Retention and Natural Discharge
- After approximately one year of treatment, most patients leave the program naturally
- This natural attrition is framed positively — described as "health" (i.e., recovery and appropriate discharge)
- Suggestion to exclude year-plus patients from active calculations since they are no longer clinically relevant to current caseload metrics

### Psychiatrist Recommendations vs Actual Outcomes

- Psychiatrists document when they want to see patients next; comparing this against actual visit patterns could reveal gaps in care adherence
- One speaker expresses trust in the other's ability to perform this analysis

### Predictive Modeling Discussion
- Discussion about building equations or models with potential variables to predict patient outcomes
- Testing predictions against actual patient results to validate the model
