# Clinic Israel Operations Discussion 2025-12-02

Voice memo transcript of an operational call conducted in Arabic (Levantine dialect) between Rami Khouri, Basel Kanaaneh, and a third participant about Telia'z Clinic Israel operations.

## Topic 1: Meeting Cadence Optimization

Rami suggests that the Sunday and Monday meetings are redundant — most topics discussed on Sunday repeat on Monday. He recommends:
- **One-on-one meetings with staff** are no longer necessary in the current structure. The format was needed under the previous organizational setup but is now redundant.
- **Clinical meeting (clinica):** Once per week is fine; reduce one-on-one sessions to once per month.
- Basel should decide which cadence works best for his workflow.
- Exception: If there are additional topics (academia, protocols, kishut/licensing matters), those can be addressed separately.

## Topic 2: Psychiatrist Pay Schemes

A participant asks about the pay schemes (eskimot) for psychiatrists:
- There is a **graduated pay scale** (tiered): starts around 300, then 350, then 450 NIS (per session or per hour — exact unit unclear).
- These schemes are already in the system and can be viewed there.
- Rami has a document showing each doctor by name and their current rate.
- **Action:** Rami will ask Shira to send the current pay scheme documents to Basel.
- There are draft schemes for each psychiatrist type/tier.

## Topic 3: Underperforming Psychiatrist (Elias)

Discussion about a psychiatrist named Elias who appears to have availability or performance issues:
- The third participant suggests they should not let the situation continue — if someone is not performing, they should not remain.
- Rami distinguishes between two separate concerns:
  1. The **clinic's operational need** to fill capacity (other doctors may have slots available; not Elias-specific)
  2. The **specific issue with Elias** as a psychiatrist — what to do about him
- Decision: **Wait one day** before taking action on Elias. If other doctors have availability, don't rush to fill specifically from Elias. Don't take premature steps.
- The third participant agrees to wait.

## Topic 4: Secretarial Responsibilities Documentation

The third participant (likely Basel) plans to:
- Document each secretary's responsibilities and current thread of work ("khuyut" — threads/lines of responsibility)
- Include administrative movements since joining
- Will send the document to Rami for review **before** distributing it to the team.
- Rami agrees and asks to see it first.

## Topic 5: Rana's Incentive/Bonus Restructuring

Discussion about Rana Khouri's bonus structure:
- Currently, a "mekhirot" (sales/attendance) incentive is being converted to a bonus for secretaries.
- Shira sent a message yesterday about the mekhirot issue — these need to be formalized.
- Rami says the mekhirot incentive should be **phased out by end of December 2025**, not abruptly removed.
- Two objectives for the new incentive structure:
  1. **Motivation** — it should give something meaningful to work toward
  2. **Accountability** — it should be tied to measurable performance (not an easy win)
- **Action:** Basel should take time to understand what KPIs make sense for Rana's bonus, how to measure them.
- Rami notes the mekhirot were originally introduced when secretaries had sales-like duties (bringing in patients). Now that context has changed, the incentive no longer fits.
- Rana should be informed that the mekhirot are **not related to her position scope**, and the change is coming by end of December.
- A separate bonus linked to actual work performance should replace it.

## Topic 6: Annual Plan (Takhnit) Deadline

- There is a **Thursday deadline** to submit the annual operational plan (takhnit) for the clinic.
- The plan must cover: projected patient numbers, hiring needs, spending projections, and operational targets for 2026.
- Rami says he needs to work on both the clinic plan and other initiatives (possibly innovation plans).
- Basel is overwhelmed — received thousands of emails from Shira, Oren, and others, feels pulled in competing directions.
- **Rami's directive to Basel:** Focus on the immediate operational priorities:
  1. Protocols with Shira (responsibilities and role definitions)
  2. Assignments for Rana (the two items discussed)
  3. Talk to someone about the clinical flow/current state
- **Do NOT work on the annual plan** — Rami will handle it. Basel should focus on the "belden" (international/countries) work.
- They agree to discuss further the next day (Tuesday).

1. Meeting cadence to be streamlined — fewer redundant meetings
2. Pay schemes to be shared with Basel via Shira
3. Elias situation to be handled carefully — separate clinic needs from individual issues
4. Secretarial responsibilities being documented by Basel (or third participant)

6. Annual plan deadline Thursday — Rami owns this, Basel focuses on operational priorities
