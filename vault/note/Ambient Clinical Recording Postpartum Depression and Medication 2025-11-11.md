This recording captures a textbook postpartum depression consultation with an Arabic-speaking patient. The clinician's approach is notable for:

1. **Psychoeducation:** Clear explanation of hormonal mechanisms contributing to postpartum depression
2. **Normalization:** Framing the depression as an expected response to difficult circumstances plus biological changes
