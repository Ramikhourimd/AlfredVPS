# Telia'z Company Credit Card and Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Context

On 2025-12-04, Rami asked Basel about the difference between using a company credit card and a personal card for business expenses. The exchange revealed a misunderstanding about how Telia'z handles expense reimbursement, which Basel corrected on the spot.

## Key Points

### No Invoice-Based Reimbursement on Salary
Telia'z does **not** operate a reimbursement model where employees pay out of pocket and submit invoices (hashbonit) for salary additions. Rami had assumed this was the case, based on how his separate clinical practice works (where personal purchases with invoices are reimbursed on top of salary). Basel explicitly told him this is not how it works and asked him not to do it again.

### Company Credit Card Holders
Only **two people** currently hold Telia'z company credit cards:
1. **Basel Kanaaneh** — Operations Manager
2. **Shira** — Clinical Operations Manager (holds a card so she can make purchases on behalf of the company when Basel needs her to)

No other staff members have company credit cards.

### Implication
Any business purchases should go through Basel or Shira's company cards. Staff should not make business purchases on personal cards expecting reimbursement.
