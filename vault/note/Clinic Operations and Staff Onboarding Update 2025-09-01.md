# Clinic Operations and Staff Onboarding Update 2025-09-01

## New Staff Onboarding Status

Three new clinicians joined [[org/Telia'z]] Clinic Israel during the week of 2025-08-25:

1. **Menahal** (new manager) — Started with 40 clinical hours. Began entering summaries into Clickx after training from [[person/Ori Shukron]]. Estimated 15 hours/week for summary entry, not yet verified in practice. Total workload slightly above 50 hours/month including clinical and summary hours.

2. **David** — Had his first day receiving patient appointments on 2025-09-01. Also had follow-up sessions with patients from other clinicians.

3. **Itamar** — Already started before the others. Had some initial communication issues (overly intense email patterns during onboarding) but professionally competent with strong experience. Was staggered into the schedule intentionally to avoid flooding available hours. Initially pushed to start immediately but then realized August childcare obligations would delay him to September.

## Technical Support Gap

During a week when both [[person/Shachar]] and [[person/Roy Shur]] were on vacation simultaneously, [[person/Shira]] faced significant operational difficulties:

- New staff email setup required technical access that only Shachar and Roy could provide
- Itamar could not log into Teams/Retool; instructions sent by Roy remotely did not resolve the issue
- Shachar accidentally opened system permissions for [[person/Maram Faris]] instead of the new manager
- Multiple interruptions to vacation staff were required
- Shira identified the need for a technical support backup plan for periods when both tech staff are unavailable

## Clickx System Transition

- Clickx system management responsibility moved from Elias to [[person/Ori Shukron]]
- Ori was in a reduced-activity period (maternity leave) but expected to return fully by end of September
- Ori trained the new manager on Clickx summary entry
- Elinor designated as the new overall Clickx point of contact going forward

## Management Structure Evolution

[[person/Rami Khouri]]'s plan for gradual transition:
- Shira + Elinor to jointly manage all clinical operations in Israel
- Rami to transition to Global Medical Director role by January 2026
- Focus shift to oversight, metrics, and innovation across Israel and UK clinics
- Rami to stop attending weekly team meetings, joining only the monthly review session
