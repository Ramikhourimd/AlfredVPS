# UK Trip Travel Mishap and Rebooking Costs — September 2025

## Context

Voice memo from a casual conversation at the Telia'z Company Fun Day on 2025-09-21. Rami recounts a travel mishap during his September 2025 UK business trip (Israel → Frankfurt → London → Leeds route). Also includes a brief exchange about rent and interest income.

## Financial Fragment

Someone hands Rami money from rental income ("from the rent") along with interest payment. Brief exchange — no further financial details discussed.

## Travel Mishap — Missed Frankfurt to London Flight

### What Happened

1. Rami's trip involved two airport terminals in Frankfurt — he needed to return the rental car at a different terminal from his departure gate.
2. He booked a flight from Frankfurt to London, with a pre-booked train connection from London to Leeds.
3. He realized the car return terminal was at a different location — approximately two hours from his departure terminal.
4. He missed the Frankfurt-to-London flight.

### Rebooking

- Found a replacement flight departing Frankfurt at 7pm, but landing at an airport roughly two hours outside London (not a London airport).
- Also had to deal with check-in complications.
- Described the whole situation as a frustrating mistake.

### Financial Impact

- **Extra flight cost:** ~$600 USD (~2,000 NIS) — a flight he would not have needed at all if the original had been caught.
- **Hotel loss:** The London hotel was non-refundable and went unused since arrival time shifted.
- The listener commented it both ruined part of the trip and added unnecessary expense.

### Resolution

- Despite the mishap, Rami and his travel companion(s) adapted. They visited a location near Frankfurt and continued the trip.
- Spent the remaining two days in France.
- Rami acknowledged it was an expensive mistake but said it ultimately did not ruin the overall trip.
