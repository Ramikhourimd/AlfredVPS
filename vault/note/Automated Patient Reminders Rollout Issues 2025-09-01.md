# Automated Patient Reminders Rollout Issues 2025-09-01

## Background

Approximately one month before this meeting, [[org/Telia'z]] Clinic Israel transitioned from manual secretary-made reminder calls to automated phone reminders. The automated system calls patients the evening before (for early morning appointments) or the morning of (for appointments from 10:00 onwards) with a recorded reminder and appointment confirmation.

## Issues Identified

### Missing Technical Instructions SMS

A supplementary SMS with detailed technical instructions (how to download Teams, connection steps, troubleshooting) was drafted by [[person/Shira]] but never actually sent as part of the automated flow. The implementation team (Eitan and [[person/Shachar]]) apparently missed this component during rollout.

### Increased Patient Technical Difficulties

After the transition:
- Secretaries reported more patients struggling to connect to sessions
- Patients who previously received human guidance during reminder calls now had no technical support
- Elinor reported that all her no-shows for the month were due to technical connection issues (not clinical)
- In three cases, the case manager had noted in advance that the patient had technical difficulties requiring a direct phone call, but these notes were not acted on

### Impact on Session Quality

Some psychiatrists spent up to 25 minutes of session time helping patients with technical setup, effectively turning intake sessions into no-shows from a clinical perspective.

## Root Cause

The automated system replaced only the reminder function of the secretary calls but not the technical support function. The human touch of secretaries verifying patients could connect and walk them through the app download/setup was lost without a replacement mechanism.

## Proposed Solutions

1. Immediately send the drafted technical instructions SMS alongside automated reminders
2. Establish a flagging system for patients with known technical difficulties to receive a human follow-up call
3. Consider dedicated technical support availability during peak session hours
