# Clinic Operations Coordination 2025-12-02

Voice memo recording of an operational coordination call between Rami and Basel (with a brief third participant), covering multiple clinic management topics at Telia'z Israel clinic.

## Meeting Frequency Optimization

Rami proposed reducing the meeting cadence. Currently there are Sunday and Monday meetings, but most content from Sunday repeats on Monday. Rami's recommendation:
- **Clinical team meetings:** Weekly (keep as-is)
- **One-on-one meetings:** Reduce to once per month instead of weekly
- **Rationale:** The organizational structure Rami was building previously required frequent one-on-ones, but he now believes they are no longer necessary. Basel should find the rhythm that works best for him.

## Psychiatrist Fee Schedules (Eskimim)

Basel needs the current fee schedules from Shira. The clinic operates with tiered pay rates for psychiatrists:
- Tier 1: ~300 NIS
- Tier 2: ~350 NIS
- Tier 3: ~450 NIS

These rates are available in the system (likely BigQuery/DWH). Rami has a document showing each doctor by name and their current rate, and will ask Shira to send the full fee schedule drafts to Basel. There are draft schedules for each contract type.

## Elias Situation — Psychiatrist Staffing Issue

A psychiatrist named Elias has no available patient slots. Key discussion points:
- Basel suggested having another clinician (possibly Rana) take Elias's patient intake ("expressions/referrals") to avoid delays
- Rami cautioned separating two distinct issues:
  1. **Clinical urgency:** Patients needing to be seen — can other doctors with availability take them?
  2. **Psychiatrist management issue:** What to do specifically about Elias
- Rami noted this pattern results from not having had someone in Basel's role previously to handle these situations quickly
- Decision: Wait one more day before taking action specifically on Elias, but address patient access independently — if another doctor has capacity, route patients there regardless of the Elias-specific issue
- Both agreed: if a psychiatrist consistently fails to see patients, that behavior should not continue ("this person shouldn't stay employed doing this")

## Secretary Accountability Documentation

Basel plans to create a document for Shira and the secretaries outlining:
- Each secretary's active case threads
- Their operational movements and responsibilities since Basel started

Rami asked Basel to share the document for review before distributing it. Basel agreed.

## Rana's Bonus and KPI Structure

Discussion about designing Rana's performance incentive:
- Currently Rana has "makhirot" (sales/referral incentives) — these are being phased out at end of December 2025
- Rami noted the makhirot were originally given because secretaries had a role in bringing in patients (metupalim), but the context has changed
- For the new bonus scheme, two goals must be balanced:
  1. **Motivation:** Give Rana something meaningful to work toward
  2. **Accountability:** Tie the bonus to measurable KPIs so it's earned, not automatic
- Rami told Basel to take time understanding what KPIs make sense for Rana's administrative role before rushing a structure
- Shira also sent information about makhirot that needs to be communicated to staff — this is the last month for the old system
- Rami emphasized telling staff clearly: makhirot end after December, and the team will find a replacement incentive tied to actual work performance

## Annual Budget Plan (Takhlit)

A budget/operating plan must be submitted by Thursday (likely to Alex/board):
- Content: Next year's operational plan — how many therapists, how many to hire, how many to onboard, spending projections
- Rami needs to work on it (mentioned working on it at night)
- Basel asked about a Gantt chart for the clinic/org that was sent by Shira and Oren — Basel hadn't received it
- Rami acknowledged having thousands of unread emails from Shira and Oren
- Basel expressed frustration about being pulled in multiple directions and receiving conflicting requests
- **Key directive:** Rami told Basel explicitly to **focus on international expansion (boldanot)** and not worry about the annual budget plan — Rami will handle the takhlit himself

## Basel's Focus Areas

Rami gave Basel clear prioritization guidance:
1. **Primary focus:** International expansion work (countries/boldanot)
2. **Secondary:** Protocol for Shira and operational responsibilities; secretary thread documentation
3. **Deprioritized:** Annual budget/takhlit — Rami handles this
