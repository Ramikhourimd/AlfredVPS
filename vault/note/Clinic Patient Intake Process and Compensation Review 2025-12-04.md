## Patient Intake Workflow

The discussion maps out the current patient onboarding flow at Clinic Israel:

1. **Application** — Patient referral comes in (discussed specifically for Clalit HMO patients, but also general "ovedim"/workers)
2. **First Contact (Rana)** — Rana conducts the initial phone screening ("rishon mitkadem" / first contact interview). She handles the preliminary assessment
3. **Professional Interview (Shira/Uri/Waseem)** — A clinical professional conducts a deeper evaluation. Shira or Uri handles standard cases; Waseem handles couples ("zug")
4. **Assignment** — Patient is assigned based on clinical needs and availability. The speaker notes they handle the "riyon mitpa'i" (clinical interview) and "riyon klali" (general interview)
