This is a continuation of the payroll review session from the same day. The conversation was in colloquial Arabic with Hebrew workplace terminology.

## Three-Tier Compensation Structure

Rami and Basel calculated rates across three tiers ("shlavim") for the psychiatrist compensation schedule:

### Tier 1 (Entry/Standard)
- **Intake (Antek):** 225 NIS for 45-minute session
- **Follow-up (Ma'akev):** 30-minute session, calculated at 148.50 NIS
- Rate derived using 0.75 coefficient applied to base rate
- Calculation: 225 per 45 min = 300/hour, then 0.75 coefficient = 225 per intake

### Tier 2 (Mid-Level)
- **Intake (Antek):** 262.50 NIS
- **Follow-up (Ma'akev):** 173.25 NIS
- Higher tier for experienced psychiatrists

### Tier 3 (Senior/Expert)
- **Intake (Antek):** 400 NIS
- **Follow-up (Ma'akev):** 200 NIS
- Applies to specialists like Ahmed and Yael
- Ahmed: 400 intake, Yael: 400 + 200
- Total per full session: 600 NIS

### Standardized Rate
- **All "stradikim" (standard practitioners):** 250 NIS follow-up rate
- This is the target rate for alignment — any practitioner getting less should be raised to match
