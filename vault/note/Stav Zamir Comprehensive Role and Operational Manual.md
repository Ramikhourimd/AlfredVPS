## Role Purpose

Stav serves as a critical bridge between Taliaz's Clinical Operations and Innovation teams. She ensures AI-powered mental healthcare solutions are clinically relevant, effective, and seamlessly integrated into daily workflows.

## Three Responsibility Pillars

1. **Clinical Viability Validation:** Primary authority for validating clinical soundness and safety of all new and proposed technological solutions.
2. **Clinical Needs Analysis:** Acts as "field sensor" to proactively identify clinical problems, user pain points, and workflow inefficiencies. Converts findings into formal innovation initiatives with defined KPIs.
3. **Implementation and Training:** Coordinates and leads clinical pilots, identifies and resolves implementation barriers, develops training for staff adoption.

## Additional Duties

- Manage and contribute to existing innovation projects
- Provide continuous clinical input throughout product development lifecycle
- Perform hands-on clinical work to maintain expertise and user perspective
- Build and maintain evaluation frameworks (graders) for AI prompts
- Maintain comprehensive documentation for all innovation projects
- Collect and synthesize stakeholder feedback to inform development priorities
