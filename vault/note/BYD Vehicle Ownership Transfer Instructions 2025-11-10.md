# BYD Vehicle Ownership Transfer Instructions 2025-11-10

## Context

Voice memo recorded on 2025-11-10 from the previous vehicle owner (seller), providing Rami with detailed instructions for completing the BYD vehicle ownership transfer process. The seller offered to help handle the procedure if Rami is too busy.

## Ownership Transfer Steps

### 1. Government Ownership Transfer Form
- The seller already has a copy of the ownership transfer form (tofes ha'avara)
- Once Rami receives the official ownership transfer document, he should screenshot or photograph it

### 2. Contact BYD Service
- Call BYD customer service (the seller is sending the phone number separately)
- Send BYD the ownership transfer document
- BYD confirmed they will process the transfer once they receive the document

### 3. BYD Manufacturer Registration (China)
- BYD Israel forwards the ownership transfer to the manufacturer in China
- The manufacturer will register Rami in the system and remove the previous owner
- **Important:** The seller will NOT disconnect from the BYD system yet — if he disconnects before the transfer completes, Rami will lose access to the car

### 4. BYD App Registration
- After the manufacturer processes the transfer, BYD will send Rami an invitation to register for the BYD app using his email address
- This gives Rami full digital access to the vehicle

## Key Notes

- The seller offered to handle the entire procedure on Rami's behalf if needed
- The seller has Rami's email and the transfer form
- The seller already spoke with BYD service and confirmed the process
- Temporary dependency: the seller stays connected to the BYD system until Rami's registration is complete, to ensure uninterrupted vehicle access
