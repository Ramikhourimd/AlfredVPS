## Summary

Multi-topic team meeting covering:

### 1. Telem Brochure Development
- Telem (military rehabilitation unit) works with the IDF Rehabilitation Division to provide psychiatric services for families of wounded soldiers
- A brochure is needed for Maccabi patients specifically — must be Maccabi-branded, not Telem-branded
- The brochure should explain the service, how to access treatment, and what to expect
- Amiad (Telem manager) is the key contact driving this initiative
- Discussion about whether to extend the brochure to Clalit (decision: start with Maccabi first)

### 2. Referral Tracking System Design
- Need unique tracking links or QR codes per clinic/city to measure referral effectiveness
- URL parameters to identify which brochure/location generated each referral
- Goal: understand which outreach channels and locations are most effective
- Rami proposed adding tracking parameters to the existing intake form URL

### 3. Dashboard Review — Patient Referral Statistics
- Shachar presented current patient referral data
- Approximately 170 new patients per week
- Monthly rate: ~714 (up from ~400 previously)
- 190 new patients in the reviewed period, 131 from Maccabi
- 20 paid follow-up sessions recorded

### 4. City-by-City Analysis
- Ashdod/Ashkelon leading in referral volumes
- Tel Aviv showing growth
- Outreach planned for Afula
- Ori reported on recent clinic visits and relationship-building meetings

### 5. Referral Source Analysis
- Direct doctor referrals: primary source
- Family recommendations: growing channel
- Marketing campaigns: declining effectiveness
- Need for systematic tracking of interventions and their impact per city over time
