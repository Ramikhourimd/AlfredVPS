# Rami Oren On-Call Compensation Discussion 2025-09-01

## Summary

One-on-one call between [[person/Rami Khouri]] and [[person/Oren Taliaz]] to settle the on-call (konanut) compensation mechanics for the remainder of 2025. The conversation took place on 2025-09-01.

## Key Points

### On-Call Budget and Distribution
- [[person/Oren Taliaz]] allocates a fixed monthly budget of **25,000 NIS** for on-call coverage at [[project/Telia'z Clinic Israel]].
- [[person/Rami Khouri]] manages the distribution of this budget among on-call staff.
- Oren will instruct relevant parties that the 25,000 is distributed per Rami's allocation decisions.

### Atef's Compensation
- Atef covered on-call for the last week of August 2025.
- Agreed rate: **5,000 NIS per half-month**, so one week = **2,500 NIS**.
- Rami will add this to Atef's compensation.

### Elinor's Compensation
- Elinor requested to do fewer on-call days (less than half a month).
- Compensation will be calculated **proportionally per day** based on her actual coverage.
- Elinor does **not** receive compensation for phone calls — only for on-call shifts.
- No signed contract for Elinor's on-call arrangement yet; Rami sent the terms but hasn't received the signed document back.

### The "Gap" — Rami's Bonus
- After distributing the 25,000 NIS to on-call staff (Atef, Elinor, etc.), the **remainder (the "gap") goes to Rami as a bonus**.
- Oren explicitly confirmed: "We add the gap to you as a bonus. That's what we're agreeing on."
- Example given: if Atef gets 2,500 and that's all the distribution, Rami receives the remaining 22,500.
- Rami initially considered using the gap for conferences/team development instead of taking it as salary, but Oren encouraged him to take it as personal compensation.
- Oren's reasoning: this arrangement runs only through December 2025, so Rami should benefit directly rather than save it.

### Company Setup Decision
- Rami **dropped the idea of setting up his own company** (previously discussed as a vehicle for receiving compensation).
- Oren appreciated this, noting it simplified legal/accounting (due diligence concerns, explaining the arrangement to auditors).
- Oren offered to continue supporting Rami's existing company through December 2025 anyway — to "fill it a bit" with the gap payments.
- After December, the expectation is that the company arrangement will not continue.

### Employment Agreement Status
- The existing December agreement (signed earlier) **remains unchanged**.
- No new agreement is needed; the on-call arrangement was the only reason a new agreement had been discussed.
- Oren will arrange a **short addendum or separate document** to formalize the on-call compensation arrangement through December 2025.

### Invoice Timing
- [[person/Adiel Levin]] flagged that psychiatrists need to submit invoices on time.
- Rami acknowledged the challenge: reports only become available at month-end, then psychiatrists need to review, add hours, and get Rami's approval.
- Oren asked Rami to put pressure on the key people to submit promptly, as delayed invoices delay payment approvals.

### Practical Next Steps
- Rami will send Oren a detailed email with August payment breakdown.
- Rami will issue a payment request (drishat tashlum) before the actual invoice.
- Rami will send a monthly table showing psychiatrist compensation distribution.
- Next in-person meeting: Sunday.
