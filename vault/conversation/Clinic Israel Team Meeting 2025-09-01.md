# Clinic Israel Team Meeting 2025-09-01

## Context

Weekly team meeting at [[org/Telia'z]] Clinic Israel. First meeting with Elinor officially starting her medical management role replacing Elias. Participants included [[person/Rami Khouri]], [[person/Shira]], [[person/Ori Shukron]], Elinor, Anan ([[person/Ahmed Masalha]]), and others from the clinical team.

## Key Topics Discussed

### Meeting Structure

Rami announced the meeting structure going forward:
- Rami and Shira: Weekly meeting on operational/strategic matters and KPIs
- Shira and Elinor: Weekly meeting on clinical matters
- Rami and Elinor: To be scheduled for medical management coordination
- Rami will stop attending the weekly team meeting starting next week, but will join the last meeting of each month for a monthly review. His note-taker (AI assistant) will attend in his place.

### Elinor Introduction and Role

Elinor was formally introduced as replacing Elias in the medical management role. Today was her official first day in the management capacity. She takes on clinical management, medical staff oversight, and will gradually take over psychiatrist hiring decisions.

### Clickx System Management

Elinor was designated as the new point of contact for all Clickx-related matters. Key issues discussed:
- Manal was entering summaries using Elias's user account (incorrect practice)
- Each psychiatrist needs individual Clickx access with proper OTP authentication
- A new staff member (Oriel/Uriel) still lacked Clickx access
- The process should be: each psychiatrist logs into Clickx at session start, enters diagnoses and medications; summary copy-paste is done by a dedicated person from each psychiatrist's account
- A cloud-based remote desktop solution exists for overseas psychiatrists (Elinor, Noa) to access Clickx without VPN

### Children's Clinic B2C to Maccabi Transition

- 37 children currently in the pipeline at [[org/Maccabi Healthcare Services]]
- Teacher questionnaire was blocking progress (29 of 30 children stuck) — decision made to make it optional
- Anan to determine which cases specifically require teacher questionnaires (e.g., ADHD assessments)
- Ori reported difficulties coordinating with Eitan (pilot project manager) and Shachar (on vacation)
- Rami explained Eitan's pilot role as project manager following Nadav Blatt's departure

### Automated Patient Reminders

- Manual secretary reminder calls were replaced with automated phone reminders approximately one month prior
- A supplementary technical instructions SMS (how to download Teams, connect to sessions) was supposed to accompany the automated calls but was never sent
- Multiple patients experienced technical difficulties connecting to sessions without the human support previously provided by secretaries
- Elinor reported that all her no-shows that month were due to technical issues, not clinical ones

### Company Fun Day — September 21

- Company event scheduled for September 21 at 12:00
- Rami pushed strongly for all psychiatrists to attend
- Offered to arrange compensation for freelance psychiatrists attending on a non-work day
- Anan cited on-call schedule conflicts; Elinor and Noa had travel concerns
- Discussion about a second event in December when more overseas staff would be in Israel

### WPA Conference — Prague, October

- Rami announced the WPA (World Psychiatric Association) conference in Prague in early October
- Offered ~50% subsidy on registration, flights, and hotel for attending psychiatrists
- Also mentioned EPA and ECNP conferences as options
- Elinor to check interest among the psychiatric team

### Re-intake Policy for Returning Patients

- Discussion about patients returning after extended absence (6+ months) or switching psychiatrists
- New psychiatrist Yehuda (replacing Talia at Maccabi) raised the need for re-intake assessments
- Policy: patients absent over 6 months should go through a new intake process
- Elinor to be involved in formalizing this policy

## Outcome

Multiple action items assigned. Meeting structure formalized for ongoing operations.
