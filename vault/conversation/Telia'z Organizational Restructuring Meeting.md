## Summary

A roughly 2-hour strategy meeting among Telia'z co-founders and key stakeholders to discuss organizational restructuring. The meeting addressed role definitions, clinic management structure, resource sharing between parent company and clinic, and governance.

## Key Discussion Points

1. **CMO vs COO Role Debate** — Rami argued for a combined CMO/COO role with full authority over the clinic. Oren pushed for separating clinical oversight (CMO) from operational management (COO), proposing Basel as operations lead.
2. **Clinic as District Model** — Agreement to treat the clinic as a "district" under the parent company, with its own Clinical Director, Operations Manager, and Administrative Manager.
3. **Pod Structure for Scaling** — Proposed organizing psychiatrists into pods of ~10, each led by a senior clinician, to enable scalable management.
4. **Centralized Shared Services** — HR, marketing, finance, and tech support to remain at parent company level, serving all districts/clinics.
5. **Innovation & CTO Relationship** — Rami's innovation work to remain separate from Shachar's CTO responsibilities, with collaboration on shared projects.
6. **Secretarial Staff** — Debate over whether Renee should serve the clinic exclusively or be shared across the organization.
7. **Board Governance** — Ilan Rosenfeld's role as investor representative on the board was briefly discussed.

## Emotional Undercurrents

- Rami expressed frustration about feeling sidelined from operational decisions despite being the physician founder.
- Oren emphasized the need for professional management separate from clinical leadership.
- Basel navigated carefully as the new hire caught between the co-founders' differing visions.

## Outcome

No final decisions were made. Several action items were assigned (see linked tasks). The group agreed to continue discussions with clearer role proposals.
