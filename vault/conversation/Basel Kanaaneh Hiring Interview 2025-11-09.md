# Basel Kanaaneh Hiring Interview 2025-11-09

## Context
Hiring interview for the Telia'z Clinic General Manager position. Basel Kanaaneh (age 30) was the candidate, interviewed by Rami Khouri and Dekkel Taliaz at Telia'z offices on November 9, 2025.

## Connection
Basel was connected to the opportunity through his sister Ranin, who works with Rami Khouri's sister. Rami had a preliminary phone call with Basel before this in-person interview.

## Topics Covered
1. **Early career** — Started as medical secretary at "Bikur Rofeh" (home visits company) in Haifa/Krayot, advanced to Deputy Manager of clinics for Kiryat Shmona, Krayot, and Haifa
2. **Clalit tenure (2017–2025)** — Joined with mental health reform; built clinics from scratch in Shefa-Amr
3. **Clinic establishment** — 25-person child/youth mental health clinic + neurology/child development center = 52 staff total
4. **Clinical staff** — 8 psychiatrists, 1 neurologist, 6 psychologists, 4 social workers, plus admin/support
5. **Patient volume** — 3,800 maintained patients, ~2 new referrals per day
6. **Budget management** — Managed budgets for all 13 mental health clinics in Haifa and Western Galilee district
7. **Innovation** — Converted salaried psychiatrists to independent contractors to optimize budget and service capacity
8. **Eating disorders clinic** — Established ~3 years prior to interview
9. **Regulatory compliance** — Worked with Ministry of Welfare, Ministry of Health, Ministry of Defense on agreements and standards
10. **Private venture** — Ran clinics serving multiple health funds; sold 50% to Ali Awad (Shams Group); partner Dr. Wasim Reli as Medical Director
11. **Research** — Coordinated clinical research with Erasmus MC Rotterdam on schizophrenia and autism
12. **Recent promotion** — Field Activity Manager at Clalit (national level) — marketing, customer service, retention
13. **Motivation** — Frustrated by Clalit's slow pace of change; drawn to Telia'z's innovation/AI vision
14. **Commitment** — Expressed willingness to leave Clalit for full-time Telia'z role
15. **Compensation** — Discussed salary expectations, options/bonuses mentioned post-interview

## Outcome
Positive impression from interviewers. Post-interview discussion referenced compensation structure including options and bonuses. Basel was subsequently hired as General Manager of the Telia'z Clinic division.
