Weekly one-on-one call between [[person/Rami Khouri]] and [[person/Shira]] covering clinic operations at [[org/Telia'z]] Israel. Shira had just returned from vacation and was managing the onboarding of three new clinicians simultaneously.

## Key Topics

Itamar had already started and was having follow-up sessions. The new manager began with 40 clinical hours and was estimated to need approximately 15 additional hours per week for entering summaries, though the actual time had not yet been measured.

Both [[person/Shachar]] and [[person/Roy Shur]] were on vacation simultaneously, creating significant operational difficulties. The Clickx system responsibility was transferred from Elias to [[person/Ori Shukron]]. Ori provided training to the new manager on Clickx. Ori was in a period of reduced activity (maternity-related leave) but expected to return to full capacity by end of September. A meeting between Rami and Elinor was being planned for role coordination.

### Rami's Vision for Management Transition

Rami outlined his plan to gradually step back from day-to-day clinical operations. He wants Shira and Elinor to jointly manage the clinical operation in Israel as a unit. By January, Rami aims to transition to a Global Medical Director role focused on oversight across Israel, UK, and future clinics, with emphasis on innovation rather than daily operations.

### Shira Performance Recognition

Rami praised Shira's handling of operations during his vacation. Neither [[person/Alex Taliaz]] nor [[person/Dekkel Taliaz]] had escalated any issues, indicating the senior leadership trusted Shira's management.

### Psychiatrist Recruitment

Shira was managing recruitment of new psychiatrists. Multiple candidates had applied. She was coordinating interview scheduling and balancing the mix of junior and senior psychiatrists as previously discussed with Rami.
