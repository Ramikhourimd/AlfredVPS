# Rami Personal Assistant Planning Discussion 2025-11-09

## Overview

Voice memo recorded on November 9, 2025. Rami discusses with a close family advisor the practical steps toward hiring [[person/Amani Rohana]] as a personal organizational assistant. The conversation follows an earlier discussion the same day about [[conversation/Operational Processes and Office Planning Discussion 2025-11-09|operational processes and office planning]].

## Participants

- **Rami Khouri** — Initiator, seeking organizational support
- **Family Advisor** (unnamed) — Trusted advisor providing guidance and financial perspective

## Key Themes

### 1. Candidate Selection
- Amani Rohana is the primary candidate
- The advisor has mentioned the idea to Amani informally but no formal conversation has occurred
- The advisor strongly encourages pursuing Amani: "You need her"

### 2. Expectations Alignment
- The most critical step is setting clear expectations between Rami and Amani
- Need to define: role scope, time commitment, weekly structure, responsibilities
- Rami must first complete his own role split definition before approaching Amani

### 3. Financial Framework
- 40,000 NIS annual savings from role restructuring creates budget room
- Reference salary: 13,000 NIS bruto (gross) for the PA position
- Discussion of employer cost versus net pay implications
- 20,000 NIS added to payslip yields approximately 6,000 NIS net (tax impact)
- The 6,000 NIS net goes to savings, not disposable income

### 4. Timeline and Urgency
- Target event (birth) expected in February
- Rami wants to act sooner — December or January
- Advisor suggests a trial period: "Take a week or two to try it out"
- Without PA support during the birth period, Rami would need to stop working entirely

### 5. Advisor Guidance
- Supportive but cautious approach
- Emphasizes genuine working arrangement over performative setup
- Wants Rami to present a clear, well-thought-out proposal
- "I can stand behind it for sure" — will support once plan is solid

## Action Items Identified

1. Rami to define his role split and time allocation clearly
2. Have a formal discussion with Amani about the PA role and expectations
3. Prepare a financial proposal for the PA hire (salary budget, cost structure)
