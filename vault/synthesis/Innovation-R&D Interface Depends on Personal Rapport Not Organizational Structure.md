# Innovation-R&D Interface Depends on Personal Rapport Not Organizational Structure

## Insight

The interface between Innovation (Rami) and R&D/CTO (Shachar) functions not because of organizational design but solely because Rami and Shachar personally get along. There is no formal oversight, no regular joint meetings with leadership, no product manager mediating, and no one from above managing the boundary. Rami explicitly stated in December 2025: "The work functions because Rami and Shachar personally get along." This creates a single-point-of-failure: if either person leaves, changes roles, or the personal relationship deteriorates, the entire innovation-to-engineering pipeline collapses.

## Evidence

- 2025-12-02 (HR Challenges note): Rami describes the Innovation-R&D interface as functioning on personal rapport alone, with no formal oversight from above
- 2025-12-02: Rami notes that "in a properly structured company, the CEO or CTO would coordinate this" — acknowledging the organizational absence
- 2025-12-05 (Frustration Debrief): Rami's frustration with Shachar's velocity estimates (8 months vs 2 weeks) shows strain on the personal-rapport-based interface
- 2026-02-22 (Leadership Meeting): The team acknowledges needing a product manager and formalized prioritization — confirming the structural gap persists 3 months later
- 2026-02-22: Shachar indicates willingness to parallelize but needs prioritized requirements — showing the collaboration works informally but lacks process

## Implications

This is a scaling fragility. As the company grows and adds international operations (UK), the innovation-R&D interface cannot continue depending on two individuals' goodwill. The proposed product manager hire addresses the symptom but not the root cause — there is no governance mechanism to resolve priority conflicts between innovation and platform work when they inevitably arise. The 16:1 velocity estimate disagreement is an early warning sign that personal rapport alone cannot bridge fundamental differences in approach.

## Applicability

Applies directly to Telia'z organizational restructuring, UK expansion planning (will innovation pipeline serve UK clinics?), and the proposed steering committee governance structure.
