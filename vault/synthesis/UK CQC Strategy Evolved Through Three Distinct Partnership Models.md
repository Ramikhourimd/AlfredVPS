# UK CQC Strategy Evolved Through Three Distinct Partnership Models

## Insight

Over 13 months (January 2025 to February 2026), Telia'z explored three distinct CQC partnership models, each representing increasing pragmatism about speed-to-market:

1. **GP Confederation Leeds (Jan 2025)** — Operate under GP Confed's CQC as an embedded service, tied to NHS tender process and procurement timeline
2. **OMG Healthcare (Jul 2025)** — OMG holds CQC and handles governance; Telia'z provides psychiatric expertise under OMG's regulatory umbrella
3. **Leon (Feb 2026)** — Individual GP with own CQC registration; fastest path to launch via direct partnership contract

The pattern shows a consistent strategy of borrowing CQC registration from partners rather than obtaining their own, but the partner selection evolved from institutional (GP Confed, OMG) to individual (Leon), trading scale for speed.

## Evidence

- **Jan 2025**: GP Confed meeting — Rebecca to map CQC integration as "embedded model, not subcontract." Tied to tender timeline.
- **Jul 2025**: OMG pathway design — OMG "holds the CQC registration and handles clinical governance/safeguarding." Structured as a joint service delivery model.
- **Feb 2026**: Leon partnership — Telia'z operates under Leon's personal CQC registration with a percentage revenue arrangement. Contract expected imminently.

Notably, these are not sequential replacements — by Feb 2026, Track 1 (Leon) is the fast launch path while Track 2 (own CQC via NHS) remains the longer-term institutional path. The GP Confed and OMG relationships appear to feed into Track 2.

## Implications

- The team has learned that institutional CQC partnerships (GP Confed, OMG) move slowly due to governance requirements
- Individual partnerships (Leon) offer faster launch but less scale and institutional backing
- The dual-track strategy (fast individual launch + slow institutional build) is a pragmatic response to 13 months of partnership friction
- Own CQC registration remains an unresolved strategic goal — every current model depends on a partner's goodwill

## Applicability

This pattern of "borrow credentials to launch fast, build own credentials in parallel" may apply to other international expansion markets where Telia'z lacks local regulatory registration.
