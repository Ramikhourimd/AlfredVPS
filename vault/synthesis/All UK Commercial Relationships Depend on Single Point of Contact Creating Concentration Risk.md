# All UK Commercial Relationships Depend on Single Point of Contact Creating Concentration Risk

## Insight

Across four source records spanning 13 months (January 2025 to February 2026), Adiel Levin is the sole commercial point of contact for all three UK partnership tracks:

1. **Leon Partnership**: Adiel manages the contract negotiation, reported on Leon's weekend review, and drives the CQC paperwork timeline
2. **GP Confederation Leeds**: Adiel led the in-person presentation in Leeds (Jan 2025), manages the Jim Barwick relationship, and coordinates the tender process
3. **OMG Healthcare**: Adiel built the pathway spreadsheet with Rami and manages the OMG governance relationship

No backup or secondary contact exists for any of these relationships.

## Evidence

- **Feb 15, 2026 team meeting**: Adiel reports on Leon contract status, Track 2 CQC progress, and UK staffing — all under his purview
- **Jan 22, 2025 GP Confed meeting**: Adiel presents Israel model alongside Jason Brook
- **Jul 3, 2025 OMG session**: Adiel and Rami map the NHS pathway; Adiel drives the commercial structure
- **Feb 15, 2026 operations review**: Adiel is the sole reporter on all three UK tracks

## Implications

- If Adiel is unavailable (illness, departure, overload), all three UK partnerships stall simultaneously
- There is no documented handoff protocol or secondary relationship owner for any track
- This compounds with [[synthesis/Single Israeli Team Serves Dual Markets Creating Resource Contention]] — the team is already stretched thin
- Unlike the CMO bottleneck (which is about bandwidth), this is about relationship concentration — partners know and trust Adiel specifically

## Applicability

This risk applies to the entire UK expansion project and would affect all three partnership models simultaneously. It's particularly acute during the current launch phase where commercial negotiations are active and personal relationships drive progress.
